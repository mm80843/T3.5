## Property: has_MitigationPrinciple

Promoting personal hygiene practices such as frequent hand washing, using hand sanitizers, practicing respiratory etiquette (covering mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing), and staying at home and avoiding contact with others when sick to minimize the transmission of contagious diseases.
