* [Implementing the use of ppe can create a safer work environment, reducing the risk of covid-19 transmission among workers and potentially decreasing the number of sick days taken. this can contribute to improved employee health, increased productivity, and potentially reduced healthcare costs for employers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_401.md)

## Name of the Benefit

Proper use of PPE, including facemasks, respirators, face shields, goggles, gloves, and gowns, can provide workers with enhanced protection against COVID-19 transmission.
