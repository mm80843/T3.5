# __Reduction of airborne virus transmission__

## Name of the Benefit

High-efficiency air filters, such as HEPA filters, can effectively capture and remove small particles, including viruses, from the air, reducing the risk of transmission.

## Description of actual returns pertaining to this benefit

* [Implementing high-efficiency air filters can provide a safer work environment, preventing the spread of covid-19 among workers. this can contribute to improved employee health, increased productivity, and potentially reduced healthcare costs for employers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_398.md)
