# __Inactivation of infectious microorganisms__

## Name of the Benefit

UVGI can effectively inactivate airborne infectious agents, reducing the risk of transmission and improving indoor air quality.

## Description of actual returns pertaining to this benefit

* [Improved health and well-being of building occupants, reduced transmission of infectious diseases, and reduced healthcare costs.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_467.md)
