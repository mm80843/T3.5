# RISK: __Higher COVID-19 hospitalizations and mortality among immigrants__ (PBN__Risk_1843)

## Property: has_RiskGBN

* [No](PBN__RiskGBN_0)

## Property: has_RiskName

Immigrants face a higher risk of severe illness, hospitalization, and death from COVID-19 compared to the native-born population.

## Property: has_RiskDescription

Immigrants are more likely to be hospitalized and experience higher mortality rates due to factors such as comorbidities, limited access to healthcare, and language barriers.
