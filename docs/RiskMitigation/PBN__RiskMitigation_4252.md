# __Performance-based modeling__

## Property: has_MitigationPrinciple

Using advanced modeling and simulation tools, such as CAD and CFD models, to study airflow patterns and the effectiveness of ventilation systems, enabling architects to optimize design strategies to improve indoor air quality and reduce the risk of transmission.
