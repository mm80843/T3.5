# __Self-sufficiency in shopping facilities__

## Property: has_MitigationSource

* [Housing Experience in Gated Communities in the Time of Pandemics: Lessons Learned from COVID-19](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Article/PBN__Article_87.md)

## Risks possibly mitigated by this Mitigation

* [Risk of infection](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Risk/PBN__Risk_2469.md)

## Property: has_MitigationPrinciple

The availability of on-site shopping facilities within gated communities reduces the need for residents to travel outside, minimizing the risk of infection transmission in public spaces such as malls or grocery stores.
