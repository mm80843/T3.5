# __Opening presence for air circulation and sun exposure__

## Property: has_MitigationPrinciple

To avoid high humidity and virus accumulation, openings such as windows should be provided to allow for air circulation and natural light in living areas.

## Risks possibly mitigated by this Mitigation

* [High humidity in room, virus accumulation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Risk/PBN__Risk_2908.md)
