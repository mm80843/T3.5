# __Climate control in indoor environments__

## Property: has_MitigationPrinciple

Adjusting temperature and humidity levels in indoor environments using HVAC systems to create conditions that may help inactivate the virus and reduce its ability to survive on surfaces

## Property: has_MitigationSource

* [Effects of temperature and humidity on the spread of COVID-19: A systematic review](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Article/PBN__Article_228.md)

* [hvac systems](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Technology/PBN__Technology_185.md)

## Risks possibly mitigated by this Mitigation
