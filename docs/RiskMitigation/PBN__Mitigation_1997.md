# __Green view from home windows__

## Property: has_MitigationPrinciple

Having a view of green spaces from home windows is associated with a lower increase in anxiety, anger, fear, confusion, moodiness, boredom, irritability, poor concentration, and sleep disturbance during the COVID-19 lockdown.

## Property: has_MitigationSource

* [Association between indoor-outdoor green features and psychological health during the COVID-19 lockdown in Italy: A cross-sectional nationwide study](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Article/PBN__Article_146.md)
