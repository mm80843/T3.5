# RiskMitigation: __PMMoV as an internal reference__ (PBN__Mitigation_2413)

## Property: has_MitigationRisk

* [Variability in wastewater samples](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Risk/PBN__Risk_3448.md)

## Property: has_MitigationPrinciple

PMMoV, as the most abundant RNA virus in human feces, can be used as an internal reference to control for sample-to-sample variability in wastewater dilution or processing, allowing for more accurate quantification of SARS-CoV-2 titers in the samples.
