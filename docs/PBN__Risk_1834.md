# RISK: __Material selection for buildings__ (PBN__Risk_1834)

## Property: has_RiskGBN

* [Yes](PBN__RiskGBN_1)

## Property: has_RiskName

Increased risk of malaria transmission due to the use of traditional building materials.

## Property: has_RiskDescription

Use of traditional materials (mud walls, thatched roofs, etc.) in construction increases the attractiveness of buildings to mosquitoes, increasing the risk of malaria transmission.
