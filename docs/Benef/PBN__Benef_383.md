# __Enhanced worker safety__

## Name of the Benefit

Establishing and enforcing workplace rules and signage can promote social distancing and enhance worker safety by minimizing close contact and the risk of virus transmission

## Description of actual returns pertaining to this benefit

* [By prioritizing worker safety, construction companies can attract and retain skilled workers, reduce absenteeism, and minimize the impact of potential outbreaks on project continuity and productivity](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_411.md)
