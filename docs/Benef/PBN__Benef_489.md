# __Improved ventilation and physical barriers__

## Description of Benefits pertaining to Beneficiaries

* [Private investors in healthcare facilities would benefit from investing in these engineering controls as it would provide a safer environment for healthcare workers and patients, reduce the risk of nosocomial infections, and enhance the reputation and quality of the healthcare facility, attracting more patients and potential business opportunities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_532.md)

## Name of the Benefit

Enhancing ventilation systems, adding HEPA filters, creating negative pressure AIIR in ICU, and implementing physical barriers improve infection control and reduce the risk of transmission within healthcare settings.
