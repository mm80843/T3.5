# Benef: __Improved ventilation and physical barriers__ (PBN__Benef_489)

## Property: has_BenefDetails

Enhancing ventilation systems, adding HEPA filters, creating negative pressure AIIR in ICU, and implementing physical barriers improve infection control and reduce the risk of transmission within healthcare settings.

## Property: has_BenefReturn

* [Private investors in healthcare facilities would benefit from investing in these engineering controls as it would provide a safer environment for healthcare workers and patients, reduce the risk of nosocomial infections, and enhance the reputation and quality of the healthcare facility, attracting more patients and potential business opportunities.](../BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_532)
