# __Reduction in infection transmission__

## Name of the Benefit

Implementation of prevention measures, identification and isolation of infected persons, and workplace flexibilities and protections reduce the risk of transmission and impact on businesses.

## Description of actual returns pertaining to this benefit

* [Private investors would benefit from investing in the implementation of these plans as it would lead to a safer workplace, reduced transmission of the virus, and minimized impact on business operations, which can protect and enhance the value of their investments.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_531.md)
