# __Quantification of deaths averted__

## Name of the Benefit

Comparing the number of deaths averted with and without interventions provides a clear measure of the impact of interventions on mortality, highlighting the effectiveness of control measures.

## Description of Benefits pertaining to Beneficiaries

* [Investing in interventions that reduce mortality can save lives and prevent the overwhelming of healthcare systems, minimizing the economic and social costs associated with a high number of deaths.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_900.md)
