# __Worker safety__

## Name of the Benefit

Developing industry-specific working procedures can help protect the health and safety of workers, reducing the risk of virus transmission and other hazards.

## Description of actual returns pertaining to this benefit

* [Private investors can benefit from improved worker safety through reduced absenteeism, better project management, and improved reputation, leading to higher asset value and profitability.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_1133.md)
* [Investing in mitigation measures and worker safety measures can reduce the risk of infection among construction workers, ensuring their well-being, productivity, and ability to provide essential services.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_1435.md)
