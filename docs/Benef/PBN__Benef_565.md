# __Financial stability__

## Name of the Benefit

Financial support from the government to reduce the contractors' burden in complying with the SOP. Some SOP costs can be borne by the government, such as the additional cost to provide extra accommodation for workers due to social distancing, cost to disinfect and sanitize the site.
