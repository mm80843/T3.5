# __Risk mitigation__

## Name of the Benefit

Robust contract and claims management minimizes the risk of disputes and legal issues, ensuring project timelines and costs are effectively managed.

## Description of actual returns pertaining to this benefit

* [Private investors benefit from reduced legal costs and the avoidance of project delays and financial losses. efficient contract and claims management enhances the reputation and credibility of construction firms, attracting more investors and clients.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_728.md)
* [Private investors can benefit from early serving of notices by ensuring project continuity, preventing delays, and minimizing financial losses.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_1132.md)
* [Private investors investing in organizational measures would see improved asset value by reducing the risk of outbreak and potential business disruptions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_1514.md)
* [Private investors investing in environmental measures would see improved asset value by creating a safer and healthier work environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_1515.md)
* [Private investors investing in personal protective equipment and hygiene products would see improved asset value by providing essential protection to individuals in the built environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_1516.md)
* [Investing in credit guarantee schemes can lead to reduced credit risks and potential losses for banks, improving their financial stability and profitability.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_1552.md)
