# __Increased flexibility in office space provision__

## Name of the Benefit

Transforming the office portfolio to include flexible working options, such as remote work and co-working spaces, can provide employers with increased flexibility in office space provision.

## Description of actual returns pertaining to this benefit

* [Private investors would benefit from investing in portfolio transformation as it would allow for a more agile and adaptable workplace strategy, reducing the risk of obsolescence and vacancy, and attracting a wider range of tenants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BenefReturn/PBN__BenefReturn_1039.md)
