# RISK: __Decoupling of larger aerosol particles from air stream in upper-room UVGI system__ (PBN__Risk_1175)

## Property: has_RiskGBN

* [Yes](PBN__RiskGBN_1)

## Property: has_RiskName

The decoupling of larger aerosol particles undermines the effectiveness of the upper-room UVGI system in disinfecting the air

## Property: has_RiskDescription

Larger aerosol particles may decouple from the air stream due to gravitational deposition or remaining suspended in the breathing zone, reducing their exposure to the UV-C light
