# __Urban housing improvement__

## Property: has_MitigationPrinciple

Good quality housing and air conditioning can reduce exposure to mosquitoes and protect against arboviral infections. Insecticide-treated screens can also help reduce infection rates.

## Property: has_MitigationSource

* [Challenges to Mitigating the Urban Health Burden of Mosquito-Borne Diseases in the Face of Climate Change](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Article/PBN__Article_95.md)
