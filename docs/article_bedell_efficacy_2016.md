## Abstract

Efficient and automated methods of disinfecting surfaces
contaminated with the Middle Eastern respiratory syndrome
coronavirus (MERS-CoV) may prevent the spread of the virus.
Here we report the efficacy and use of an automated
triple-emitter whole room UV-C disinfection system to
inactivate mouse hepatitis virus, strain A59 (MHV-A59) and
MERS-CoV viruses on surfaces with a \textgreater5 log 10
reduction.

Infect Control Hosp Epidemiol 2016;37:598–599
