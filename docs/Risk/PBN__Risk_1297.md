# __Lack of communication and transparency__

## Name of the risk

Lack of compliance with mitigation measures, increased fear and anxiety among occupants, misinformation and rumors.

## Description of the risk

Lack of clear and timely communication about contagious diseases and mitigation measures can lead to confusion, misinformation, and distrust among occupants.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establish effective communication channels, provide clear and transparent information, address concerns and questions, promote trust and understanding.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1779.md)
