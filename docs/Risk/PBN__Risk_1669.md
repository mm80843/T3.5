# __Increased risk of mosquito-borne diseases from bodies of water__

## Name of the risk

Increased disease burden, public health impact

## Description of the risk

Bodies of water in urban areas provide breeding habitats for mosquitoes, increasing the risk of disease transmission

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Water circulation and aeration, mosquito control measures, public education on mosquito prevention](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2224.md)
