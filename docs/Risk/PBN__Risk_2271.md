# __Surface stability of SARS-CoV-2__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and disinfection of frequently touched surfaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2959.md)

## People affected by this risk

* [individuals in contact with surfaces](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_1522.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of virus transmission through contact with contaminated surfaces.

## Description of the risk

SARS-CoV-2 can survive on surfaces such as plastic and stainless steel for up to 72 hours, increasing the risk of fomite transmission.
