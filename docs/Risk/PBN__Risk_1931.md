# __Reduced demand for public transport__

## Name of the risk

Financial losses for public transport operators, reduced revenue, potential service cuts or closures

## Description of the risk

Decreased number of passengers using public transport due to fear of contagion and shift to alternative modes of transport

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing safety measures to build passenger confidence, promoting the benefits of public transport (e.g. reduced traffic, lower emissions), offering flexible ticketing options](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2535.md)
