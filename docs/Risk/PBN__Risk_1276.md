# __IoT device vulnerabilities__

## Name of the risk

Compromised patient data, potential harm to patients, loss of trust in IoT devices

## Description of the risk

Vulnerabilities in IoT devices used for collecting and transmitting medical data, allowing unauthorized access or manipulation

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular security updates, encryption of data during transmission, strong authentication measures](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1755.md)
