# __Impact on Mental Health__

## Name of the risk

Decreased quality of life, increased psychological distress, and potential long-term mental health issues.

## Description of the risk

Lockdown measures and quarantine during the COVID-19 pandemic can lead to negative mental health effects such as depression, anxiety, and stress.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide mental health support services, promote healthy coping mechanisms, and ensure access to healthcare resources.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1523.md)
