# __Religious pilgrimages__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Limit the number of pilgrims, implement rigorous testing and quarantine measures, enforce physical distancing and hygiene protocols.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2482.md)

## People affected by this risk

* [pilgrims visiting saudi arabia for religious purposes](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_1279.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased transmission due to large gatherings and close contact among pilgrims, potentially leading to localized outbreaks.

## Description of the risk

Religious pilgrimages, such as the Umrah and Hajj, attract a large number of visitors to Saudi Arabia and may contribute to the spread of contagions.
