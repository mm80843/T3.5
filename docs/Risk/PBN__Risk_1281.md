# __Transmission via aerosols__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensuring proper ventilation, wearing masks in indoor settings with poor ventilation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1762.md)

## People affected by this risk

## Name of the risk

Indirect transmission of the virus through inhalation of contaminated aerosols

## Description of the risk

Spread of COVID-19 through microscopic respiratory droplets that remain suspended in the air for an extended period
