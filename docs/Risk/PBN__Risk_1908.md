# __Vaccine distribution and storage__

## Name of the risk

Reduced vaccine efficacy, increased wastage of vaccines, limited availability of vaccines.

## Description of the risk

Ensuring proper distribution and storage of vaccines at appropriate temperatures is crucial to maintain vaccine efficacy and prevent wastage.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establish cold chain management systems, including proper storage and transport facilities, temperature monitoring, and staff training. Prioritize high-risk populations for vaccination.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2509.md)
