## Name of the risk

Risk of large-scale outbreaks or epidemics.

## Description of the risk

Structural changes in the coronavirus spike glycoprotein enable binding to new receptor types and permit the jump from an animal host to a human host, increasing the risk of large-scale outbreaks or epidemics.
