# __Inadequate Compliance Monitoring__

## Name of the risk

Customers and employees may not adhere to social distancing measures, increasing the risk of disease transmission and posing a threat to public health.

## Description of the risk

Lack of proper monitoring and enforcement of social distancing measures by retailers may result in uncontrolled spread of contagious diseases within their premises.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Retailers should implement strict monitoring and enforcement procedures, such as regular inspections, staff training, and the use of surveillance technology, to ensure compliance with social distancing measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2292.md)
