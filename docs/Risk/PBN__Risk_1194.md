# __Protection by ventilation__

## Name of the risk

Reduces risk of cross-infection by diluting contaminated air with clean air

## Description of the risk

Proper ventilation can prevent cross-infection by supplying clean outdoor air

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase ventilation rates, use proper air distribution patterns](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1646.md)
