# __Delay in diagnosis and poor infection control practices__

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission, overwhelmed healthcare system

## Description of the risk

Residents are not promptly diagnosed with COVID-19 and do not adhere to proper infection control practices

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improve access to testing and healthcare facilities, promote and enforce proper infection control measures](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_3093.md)
