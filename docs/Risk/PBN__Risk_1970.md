# __Limited access to private green spaces__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote the availability and affordability of housing with private green spaces](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2583.md)
* [Encourage utilization of private green spaces, create guidelines for safe access, facilitate use of nearby green spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2766.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced mental well-being, increased negative emotions.

## Description of the risk

Restrictions on access to private green spaces can lead to decreased mental well-being, increased boredom, and irritability.
