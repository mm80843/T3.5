# __Inadequate cleaning and disinfection__

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of transmission of infectious diseases and contamination of surfaces.

## Description of the risk

Failure to regularly clean and disinfect surfaces can result in the buildup and spread of microorganisms.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop and implement proper cleaning and disinfection protocols, provide training and resources for cleaning staff, conduct regular inspections and audits.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1780.md)
