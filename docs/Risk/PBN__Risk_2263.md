# __Cutting public social expenditures__

## Name of the risk

Deterioration of public health and education, increased inequality.

## Description of the risk

Governments cutting social spending while reducing taxes have led to inequality, reduced public health and education, and increased debt.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Reduce tax cuts and increase public social expenditure to improve health and education.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2949.md)
