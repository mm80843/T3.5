# __Impact of social distancing and home confinement on mental health__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement comprehensive mental health support programs; increase access to mental health services; provide resources for self-care and stress management.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2604.md)

* [individuals with previous mental health issues](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_1339.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased levels of anxiety, depression, and stress; higher risk of developing mental illnesses; decreased overall mental well-being.

## Description of the risk

Measures such as social distancing and home confinement implemented during the COVID-19 pandemic have resulted in increased prevalence of mental health issues such as anxiety, depression, and PTSD in the general population.
