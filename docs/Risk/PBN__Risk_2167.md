# __Insufficient surveillance of asymptomatic and unreported cases__

## Name of the risk

Persistent transmission, increased risk of resurgence, higher number of malaria cases

## Description of the risk

Lack of surveillance and detection of asymptomatic and unreported cases can lead to persistent transmission and hinder elimination efforts

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Strengthen surveillance systems to actively detect and treat asymptomatic and unreported cases](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2833.md)
