# __Increase in outdoor noise during lockdown__

## Name of the risk

Increased annoyance and discomfort for individuals staying at home during lockdown

## Description of the risk

Increase in noise complaints related to outdoor sources (e.g., birds, construction) during the lockdown period compared to before the lockdown
