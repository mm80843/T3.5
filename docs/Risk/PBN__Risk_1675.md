# __Insufficient air circulation and ventilation__

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission, decreased indoor air quality

## Description of the risk

Poor air circulation and ventilation in apartments can lead to the accumulation of viruses and other contaminants

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper ventilation systems, regular cleaning and maintenance of HVAC systems, promote natural ventilation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2230.md)
