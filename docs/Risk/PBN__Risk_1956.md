# __Uncertainty in Construction Market__

## Name of the risk

Decreased construction activities, potential project cancellations, financial challenges for construction companies.

## Description of the risk

Market disruption and reduced investment in construction projects due to the economic impact of the pandemic.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Diversifying project portfolios, focusing on shovel-ready projects, seeking government support.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2567.md)
