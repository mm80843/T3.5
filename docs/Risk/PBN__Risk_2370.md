# __Fluctuations in sewage flow rate__

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate estimation of viral prevalence in communities, leading to misinformed decision-making and intervention strategies.

## Description of the risk

Variations in sewage flow rate, especially during weather events, can impact the detection and estimation of viral titers in wastewater samples.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop correction methods and algorithms to account for variations in sewage flow rate, collect and analyze data on weather events and their impact on sewage flow.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_3077.md)
