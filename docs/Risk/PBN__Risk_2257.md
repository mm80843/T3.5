# __Loss of UGS due to urban development__

## Name of the risk

Decreased access to nature, reduced quality of life

## Description of the risk

As cities expand, natural green spaces are often replaced by buildings and infrastructure, reducing access to UGS

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporating green infrastructure and UGS in urban planning and development, implementing green roof and wall initiatives](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2936.md)
