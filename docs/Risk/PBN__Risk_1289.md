# __Limited access to green space__

## Name of the risk

Reduced physical and mental well-being, lack of physical exercise, increased stress and anxiety

## Description of the risk

Social distancing measures limit access to green spaces such as parks and gardens

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase access to private gardens, allocate dedicated time slots for outdoor activities, create temporary green spaces in urban areas](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1770.md)
