# __Inconsistencies in Accuracy Measures__

## Name of the risk

Difficulty in comparing accuracy results and assessing the clinical dependability of wearable technology

## Description of the risk

Lack of uniformity in reporting and using accuracy measures for evaluating the performance of wearable technology in detecting COVID-19 infection
