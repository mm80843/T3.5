# __Inadequate antibacterial and antiviral properties of stainless steel surfaces__

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission in public spaces and healthcare settings.

## Description of the risk

Stainless steel surfaces do not have sufficient antibacterial and antiviral properties, leading to the potential transmission of diseases.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Replace stainless steel surfaces with copper coatings, which have antimicrobial properties.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2015.md)
