# __Airborne pathogen generation__

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of spreading diseases and infections

## Description of the risk

Pathogens are generated in respiratory system and released in exhaled air, increasing risk of cross-infection

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improve hygiene practices, use personal protective equipment](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1640.md)
