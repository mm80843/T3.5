# __Lack of privacy in apartments__

## Name of the risk

Decreased mental well-being, increased stress

## Description of the risk

Inadequate layout and spatial configuration in apartments can lead to a lack of privacy and increased stress for residents

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Optimize layout to provide separate spaces, utilize room dividers and soundproofing materials](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2231.md)
