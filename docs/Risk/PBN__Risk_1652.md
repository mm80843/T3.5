# __Inadequate public transportation infrastructure__

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission and outbreaks in crowded public transportation systems

## Description of the risk

Insufficient capacity and poor design of public transportation systems lead to crowded conditions and increased risk of disease transmission
