# __Risk of transmission in public spaces__

## Name of the risk

Increased infection rates among the public, potential outbreaks, health and safety risks

## Description of the risk

Risk of transmission of contagious diseases in public spaces, such as transportation hubs, retail stores, and entertainment venues
