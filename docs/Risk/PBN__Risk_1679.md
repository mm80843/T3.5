# __Inadequate ventilation in indoor spaces__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Upgrading ventilation systems to meet recommended air exchange rates and introducing measures such as air filtration, use of portable air cleaners, and opening windows for natural ventilation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2234.md)

* [hospitals](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_64.md)
* [schools](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_366.md)
* [occupants of indoor spaces such as offices](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_1107.md)
* [etc](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_1108.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher transmission rates within indoor spaces, potential for superspreader events, and increased infection rates among occupants.

## Description of the risk

Poor ventilation in indoor spaces can facilitate the spread of respiratory droplets and aerosols, increasing the risk of contagion.
