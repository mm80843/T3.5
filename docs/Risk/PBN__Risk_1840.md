# __Limited flexibility in adjusting to shocks__

## Name of the risk

Reduced capacity to absorb and adapt to shocks, leading to economic instability.

## Description of the risk

Localized supply chains have limited flexibility in adjusting to shocks, as domestic markets bear most of the adjustment pressures.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote diversification of supplies and improve flexibility in sourcing inputs across multiple suppliers and countries.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2426.md)
