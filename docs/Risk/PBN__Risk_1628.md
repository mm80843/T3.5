# __Safety of laboratory personnel and environment during sample handling and analysis__

## Name of the risk

Failure to ensure safety measures during sample handling and analysis can result in the spread of the virus in the laboratory and potential infections among laboratory personnel.

## Description of the risk

The handling and analysis of wastewater samples for SARS-CoV-2 detection can pose risks to laboratory personnel and the environment in terms of exposure to the virus.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of safety protocols, including personal protective equipment (PPE), proper handling and disposal of samples, and adherence to biosafety guidelines, can minimize the risk of exposure and spread of the virus in the laboratory.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2169.md)
