# __Arsenic exposure in Bangladesh resulting from the construction of tube wells__

## Name of the risk

Arsenic poisoning, increased risk of cancer and other health problems

## Description of the risk

Construction of tube wells leads to arsenic contamination of groundwater in Bangladesh

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Proper testing and treatment of groundwater, alternative sources of clean water](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2218.md)
