# __Lack of UGS density__

## Name of the risk

Limited availability of UGS, overcrowding in existing green spaces.

## Description of the risk

The density of UGS per person in Buenos Aires City is below the recommended levels, resulting in insufficient green spaces for the population.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase the density of UGS per person by creating new green spaces and expanding existing ones.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2248.md)
