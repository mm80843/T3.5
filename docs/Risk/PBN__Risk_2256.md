# __Limited access to UGS for vulnerable populations__

## Name of the risk

Isolation, decreased physical and mental well-being

## Description of the risk

Vulnerable populations, such as elderly or disabled individuals, may have limited access to UGS during the COVID-19 pandemic

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Providing support and assistance to vulnerable populations in accessing UGS, such as transportation services or home visits](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2935.md)
