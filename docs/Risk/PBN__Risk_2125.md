# __Disruptions to global and national supply chains__

## Name of the risk

Difficulty in accessing necessary goods, economic impacts, and potential social unrest.

## Description of the risk

Reductions in air cargo volume and shipping can lead to delays, shortages, and increased costs of essential goods, including medical supplies and food.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Diversification of supply chains, local production capabilities, and contingency planning for disruptions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2776.md)
