## Mitigations of this risk

* [Media should accurately and responsibly report on preprints, and the public should rely on trusted sources for information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_3032.md)

* [general public](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_29.md)

## Name of the risk

Misinformation can lead to incorrect public health behaviors, such as not wearing masks or avoiding vaccination.

## Description of the risk

Preprints can be misinterpreted or sensationalized by media and bad-faith actors, leading to misinformation and misguided public health behaviors.
