# __Labour impact and job loss__

## Name of the risk

Financial difficulties, reduced income, unemployment, reduced productivity.

## Description of the risk

Workers are impacted by job losses, reduced working hours, and reduced income due to the suspension of projects and the need for social distancing.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Workforce reduction](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_833.md)
* [Providing financial support for affected workers, exploring alternative job opportunities, implementing safety protocols](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1902.md)
