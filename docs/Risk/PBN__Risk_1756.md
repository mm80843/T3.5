# __Effectiveness of UVGI decreases as humidity increases__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Control humidity levels to 60% or less when using UVGI systems](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2325.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased effectiveness of UVGI in controlling the spread of airborne infectious particles

## Description of the risk

Higher humidity reduces the effectiveness of UVGI systems in inactivating microorganisms
