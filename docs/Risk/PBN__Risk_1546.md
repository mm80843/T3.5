## People affected by this risk

* [urban populations in highly urbanized areas](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_1001.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced opportunities for outdoor activities and physical exercise, increased stress and mental health issues, limited social cohesion

## Description of the risk

High levels of urbanization can result in limited availability and accessibility of green spaces, depriving urban populations of the benefits of nature and outdoor activities.
