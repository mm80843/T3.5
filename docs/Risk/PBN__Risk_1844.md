# __Lower vaccination rates among immigrants__

## Name of the risk

Immigrants have lower protection against COVID-19, increasing their risk of infection and severe illness.

## Description of the risk

Immigrants have lower vaccination rates against COVID-19 due to factors such as limited access to healthcare, language barriers, and socio-economic factors.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improve access to vaccination for immigrants, provide targeted outreach and education on the importance of vaccination.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2430.md)
