# __Limited Generalizability of ADMs__

## Name of the risk

Inability to apply ADMs across different wearable devices and settings

## Description of the risk

Anomaly detection models (ADMs) for early detection of COVID-19 are not device-agnostic, limiting the types of compatible wearable technology and scalability

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop device-agnostic ADMs that can be applied to various types of wearable technology](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1719.md)
