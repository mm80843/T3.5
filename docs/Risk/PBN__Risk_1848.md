# __Changes in disease distribution__

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of disease transmission in previously unaffected regions.

## Description of the risk

Climate change alters the geographical range of mosquitoes and ticks, leading to the spread of diseases to new areas.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhance surveillance systems to detect and respond to emerging diseases in new areas.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2434.md)
