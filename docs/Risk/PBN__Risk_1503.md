# __Inadequate communication and awareness__

## Name of the risk

Lack of understanding and compliance with precautionary measures

## Description of the risk

Lack of clear communication and awareness about precautionary measures and risks in the facility, leading to increased risk of disease transmission

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop and implement effective communication strategies to educate and inform individuals about the risks and precautionary measures in the facility](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2028.md)
