# __Inadequate maintenance of green spaces__

## Name of the risk

Reduced usability and accessibility of green spaces, increased risks of accidents and injuries

## Description of the risk

Lack of proper maintenance of green spaces can lead to degradation, reduced usability, and increased risks of accidents and injuries.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement regular maintenance plans and ensure proper care and upkeep of green spaces](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2074.md)
