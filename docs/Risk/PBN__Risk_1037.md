# __Limited access to support services for victims of intimate partner violence__

## Name of the risk

Increased risk and vulnerability for victims without access to support services

## Description of the risk

Closure of shelters and limited availability of support services due to lockdown measures restricts access to safety and assistance for victims of intimate partner violence

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establish alternative support systems such as online counseling and helplines, ensure shelters remain open and accessible during lockdowns](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1470.md)
