# __Limited access to physical activity environments__

## Name of the risk

Reduced physical activity levels, increased risk of chronic conditions such as obesity, diabetes, and cardiovascular disease.

## Description of the risk

Stay-at-home measures and curfews may limit opportunities for physical activity in public spaces, especially for the urban poor in informal settlements.

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote safe physical activity guidelines and create opportunities for physical activity in public spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_2444.md)
