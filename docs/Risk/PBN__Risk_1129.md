# __Inadequate healthcare infrastructure__

## Name of the risk

Delayed or inadequate medical care, increased mortality rates, and reduced capacity to handle other healthcare needs

## Description of the risk

The risk of healthcare systems being overwhelmed by the number of COVID-19 cases

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase investment in healthcare infrastructure, establish temporary medical facilities during outbreaks](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/main/docs/RiskMitigation/PBN__RiskMitigation_1577.md)
