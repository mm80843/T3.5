## Title of the category

This category of benefits describes the various aspects related to energy usage and efficiency in infrastructure. It includes measures such as energy saving, improved efficiency, reduced consumption and costs, and stable supply. These benefits also pertain to energy demand monitoring, adaptation, and management, which help enhance energy infrastructure and promote affordability and security of energy resources. Additionally, this category encompasses factors like increased accuracy in energy consumption simulation, improved understanding of building energy use, and identification of consumption drivers. It also focuses on fostering an energy-saving conscious attitude and reducing energy waste, leading to more accurate energy billing and ultimately contributing to sustainable energy use.
