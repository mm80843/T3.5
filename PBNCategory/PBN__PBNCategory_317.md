# __Effective control and optimization of power usage.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Energy Management](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Enabler_29.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who play a crucial role in the management of energy within infrastructure, particularly green buildings neighborhoods. They possess expertise in monitoring, optimizing, and controlling energy usage and consumption. They are responsible for implementing energy-efficient systems and technologies, such as intelligent lighting and HVAC systems, to ensure minimal wastage and maximum energy conservation. These professionals actively promote the use of renewable energy sources, such as solar panels and wind turbines, to reduce reliance on fossil fuels. They also work towards integrating energy storage systems, such as batteries, to efficiently manage surplus energy and meet the demand during peak times. Overall, Energy Management Enablers focus on implementing innovative energy solutions to create sustainable and energy-efficient environments.
