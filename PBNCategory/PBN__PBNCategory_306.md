# __Virtual healthcare services for remote medical consultations and diagnoses without in-person interaction.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Telemedecine](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Enabler/PBN__BP_Enabler_17.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who are involved in the field of telemedicine. Telemedicine refers to the practice of remotely diagnosing and treating patients using telecommunications technology. These Enablers are likely to include healthcare professionals, such as doctors, nurses, and specialists, who use video-conferencing, remote monitoring, and other digital tools to provide medical consultations, advice, and treatments to patients without requiring them to physically visit a healthcare facility.
