# __Working remotely from home or a location other than the traditional office setting.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Teleworking](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Enabler_23.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who have the ability and resources to work remotely, away from traditional office spaces. They can perform their tasks and responsibilities using technology and communication tools, allowing them to work from any location with an internet connection. Teleworking enables individuals to avoid commuting to the office, reducing their exposure to contagious diseases spread within infrastructure such as green buildings neighbourhoods.
