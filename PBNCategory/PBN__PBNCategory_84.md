## Title of the category

This category of Risks describes various challenges and issues related to data accuracy, uncertainty, inconsistency, and misleading information. The individuals in this list may be researchers, analysts, or decision-makers working with data and evidence, or they could be affected by the consequences of these risks. They may face problems such as inaccurate data, limited sample size in studies, uncertainty about the future, lack of reliable predictions, misinformation and lack of awareness, or biased results. These risks can have an impact on decision-making, measurement accuracy, data analysis, and overall understanding of contagious diseases spread in infrastructure.
