## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders describes a wide range of individuals involved in various aspects of work and employment. It includes laboratories and testing laboratories involved in scientific research and analysis. Workers of different types, such as animal rearing workers, essential workers, office workers, service workers, laborers, agricultural workers, and low-skilled workers, are also part of this category. Employers and employees, including those in retail, restaurants, and grocery stores, as well as displaced workers, unemployed workers, and job seekers, are part of this group. Labor unions and frontline workers, including airport workers and knowledge workers, are also included. Social workers and cultural workers, as well as individuals working in routine and manual tasks, such as gig economy workers and service industry workers, are part of the stakeholders in this category. Overall, this group includes a diverse set of individuals involved in different occupations and industries.
