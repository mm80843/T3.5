## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who are knowledgeable or actively involved in the realm of Internet of Things (IoT), a network of interconnected devices and objects that can interact and exchange data. These individuals may possess expertise in areas such as IoT development, implementation, security, or data analysis. With their understanding of how IoT functions and its potential applications, they are well-equipped to contribute to discussions and initiatives related to the integration of IoT technologies in green building infrastructure and the impact it may have on the spread of contagious diseases.
