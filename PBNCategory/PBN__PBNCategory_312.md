## Title of the category

This category of enablers describes individuals who possess expertise and knowledge in the field of Smart HVAC systems. They are experienced professionals who specialize in the design, installation, and maintenance of advanced Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) technologies. These enablers are essential in creating efficient and sustainable indoor environments, as they understand how to optimize HVAC systems through the use of smart controls, sensors, and data analysis. Their expertise allows them to enhance energy efficiency, improve indoor air quality, and minimize the potential spread of contagious diseases within green building neighborhoods.
