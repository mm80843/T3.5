## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who have expertise in using design and construction software, specifically Building Information Modeling (BIM). BIM is a digital representation of a building's physical and functional characteristics, which facilitates efficient collaboration and communication among architects, engineers, and contractors. These individuals are proficient in using BIM software to create detailed models, simulations, and visualizations of green buildings and their infrastructures. Their skills enable them to contribute to the design and construction process, ensuring that green buildings are built with sustainable materials and energy-efficient systems.
