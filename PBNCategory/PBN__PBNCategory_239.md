## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders describes individuals and organizations that play a role in public health, regulatory compliance, education, government, research, journalism, urban planning, conservation, and historical analysis. These stakeholders include public health authorities at different levels, health authorities, regulatory authorities, education authorities, national authorities, government and authorities, datum protection authorities, research institutions, local authorities, border control authorities, urban authorities, housing authorities, journalists and journal editors, city authorities, landscape architects, water authorities, conservation authorities, emergency authorities, immigration authorities, customs authorities, airport authorities, data protection authorities, and historians.
