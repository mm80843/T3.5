## Title of the category

This category of Technologies describes a range of lighting solutions and systems that utilize ultraviolet (UV) light, natural lighting, and energy-efficient fixtures. This includes UV germicidal irradiation technologies such as UV-C light and UVGI, as well as various types of lamps and lighting control systems. It also encompasses technologies that harness natural light, such as skylights, sun tubes, and daylighting systems, as well as smart lighting systems and LED lighting. In addition, this category includes technologies related to UV sterilization and germicidal UV lamps, as well as solar energy and solar panels. Overall, these Technologies are aimed at providing efficient and effective lighting solutions, utilizing both artificial and natural light sources, while also addressing health and sanitation concerns through the use of UV technology for disinfection and sterilization purposes.
