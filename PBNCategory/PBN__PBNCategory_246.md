## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders describes individuals who have been affected by domestic violence, specifically intimate partner violence, and those who are experiencing or have experienced violence in their households, particularly within residential buildings. It includes victims of domestic violence, victims of intimate partner violence, victims of violence in general, women experiencing intimate partner violence, and vulnerable households. It also encompasses domestic abuse victims and domestic abuse charities that provide support and assistance to those affected by domestic violence.
