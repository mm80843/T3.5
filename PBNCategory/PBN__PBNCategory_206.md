# __Sanitizing the atmosphere.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Disinfection of air](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Intervention/PBN__BP_Intervention_6.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes measures taken to eliminate pathogens and harmful substances from the air. It involves the use of various techniques, such as UV-C radiation, filtration systems, and chemical treatments, to sanitize the air and reduce the spread of contagious diseases. Individuals involved in these Interventions are focused on maintaining clean and healthy indoor air quality, ensuring that occupants of green buildings and surrounding areas are protected from airborne infections and contaminants.
