## Title of the category

This category of Technologies describes various tools and devices used for temperature screening and monitoring. It includes thermal cameras, thermographic cameras, thermometers, infrared thermometers, temperature scanners, temperature detection devices, and temperature sensors. These Technologies are employed for temperature screening in various settings such as green buildings, neighborhoods, and infrastructure. They can be used for monitoring the temperature of individuals, as well as for automated control of temperature in systems such as smart thermostats and temperature control devices. Additionally, they can be integrated into drones for thermal imaging and can be used in data analysis for temperature adjustments. Cold storage facilities and heat treatment devices are also part of this category. Overall, these Technologies play a crucial role in ensuring thermal comfort, maintaining safe temperatures, and preventing the spread of contagious diseases in infrastructure.
