## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents individuals and organizations involved in the real estate and housing sector. This includes real estate owners, housing providers, housing developers and landlords, real estate agents, home buyers/sellers, and real estate agencies. It also encompasses government housing agencies, housing associations, housing authorities/agencies, and property management associations. Additionally, housing developers, property developers, and housing industry professionals are part of this group. Overall, these Stakeholders play a key role in various aspects of the real estate and housing industry, including construction, property management, and housing provision.
