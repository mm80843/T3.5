# __Efficiency through technology.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Automation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Enabler_16.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers represents technologies and systems that reduce manual labor and increase efficiency through the use of automation. Individuals who are experts or work with automation technologies, such as robotic systems, AI algorithms, or process automation tools, belong to this group. They are skilled in designing, implementing, and maintaining automated processes and are able to optimize workflows, reduce human error, and improve productivity in various sectors, including manufacturing, logistics, and service industries.
