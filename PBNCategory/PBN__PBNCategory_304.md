# __Methods to eradicate microorganisms using ultraviolet light and germicidal irradiation.__

## Items covered in this category

* [UV/UVGI/Irradation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Enabler/PBN__BP_Enabler_15.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who are experts or practitioners in the field of ultraviolet (UV) technology, specifically UV germicidal irradiation (UVGI) or UV disinfection. They are knowledgeable about the use of UV light to kill or inactivate microorganisms, including viruses and bacteria, as a means of preventing the spread of contagious diseases. These individuals might specialize in designing and implementing UV disinfection systems in various infrastructures, including green buildings.
