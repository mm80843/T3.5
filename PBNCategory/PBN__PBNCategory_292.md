# __A pictorial representation of data or concepts.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Visualization](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Enabler_3.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who are skilled in creating visual representations and graphics that aid in the understanding and communication of complex information. They have the ability to transform data and concepts into visually appealing and easily comprehensible presentations, charts, diagrams, and infographics using various software and design techniques. These Enablers play a crucial role in effectively conveying information, ideas, and findings to a broad audience, making it easier for others to grasp and assimilate the information presented.
