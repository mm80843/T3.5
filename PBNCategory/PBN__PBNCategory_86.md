## Title of the category

This category of Risks describes the lack of proper access to water and sanitation, including inadequate facilities and conditions. It also includes poor hygiene practices and insufficient cleaning and sanitization. Individuals in this list may belong to a group that faces challenges related to hygiene maintenance, sanitation practices, and access to clean water and energy.
