## Title of the category

This category of technologies describes a wide range of devices and systems used for monitoring and surveillance in various contexts. These include IoT devices, motion sensors, pressure sensors, gastrointestinal monitoring tools, smartwatches, wearable sensors, Zigbee-based monitoring systems, occupancy sensors, energy monitoring systems, remote patient monitoring systems, monitoring tools, fitness tracking devices, remote inspections, sensors for contactless inspections, alert systems, monitoring devices, sensor integration and development, environmental sensors, real-time monitoring systems, touchless technologies for doors and faucets, environmental monitoring systems, biosensor technology, water sensors, remote sensing technology, and physical distancing tools. These technologies enable the collection of data on occupancy, air quality, building health conditions, and facility usage, among other things. They are used for early detection and response, remote monitoring and management, pollution control, calibration, social distancing monitoring, and overall surveillance.
