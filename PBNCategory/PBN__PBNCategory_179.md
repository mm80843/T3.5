## Title of the category

This category of Technologies describes various components and systems related to heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) and air quality monitoring in buildings. It includes technologies such as HVAC systems, air purifiers, air quality monitors, air circulation systems, humidity control systems, air pollution sensors, and smart sensors for monitoring air quality. These Technologies focus on improving indoor air quality, enhancing airflow and ventilation, and maintaining optimal temperature and humidity levels. They also involve the use of advanced monitoring and data analysis techniques, as well as proper maintenance and operation of HVAC systems. In addition, qualified HVAC professionals play a crucial role in implementing and upgrading these technologies.
