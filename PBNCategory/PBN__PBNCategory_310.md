## Title of the category

This category of Enablers represents the individuals who are involved in the development and implementation of augmented and virtual reality technologies. These individuals are skilled in creating immersive virtual experiences and enhancing real-world environments with digital elements. They possess expertise in various fields such as computer programming, graphic design, user interface design, and 3D modeling. These Enablers play a crucial role in the advancement of virtual reality applications, allowing users to interact with digital content in simulated environments. Additionally, they contribute to the development of augmented reality, which overlays digital information onto the real world, thereby enhancing user experiences in various domains such as gaming, education, healthcare, and architecture.
