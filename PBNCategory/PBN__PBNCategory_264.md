## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents a wide range of individuals and organizations involved in the production, supply, and use of various devices, materials, and equipment. They include manufacturers of devices such as medical equipment, wearables, IoT devices, and diagnostic tests, as well as suppliers of equipment. Manufacturers specifically mentioned include those producing vehicles, materials, PPE, and vaccines. The list also includes users of wearable devices and platforms, as well as suppliers and researchers in the sensor industry. Lastly, the category encompasses organizations in the fitness and telepsychiatry industries.
