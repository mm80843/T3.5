## Title of the category

This category of Benenefits describes the various risks that are reduced or mitigated by implementing green buildings and their infrastructure. These risks include importation risk, health risks, psychological risks, social risks, performance risks, time-related risks, and financial risks. By implementing green buildings, there is an enhanced risk management and cooperation, improved information systems and risk management, improved risk assessment and perception, and an early warning system for emerging risks. Additionally, green buildings help in preventing panic buying and provide an enhanced understanding of risks and impacts. Overall, these Benenefits contribute to creating a safer and healthier environment for the individuals in the neighbourhood.
