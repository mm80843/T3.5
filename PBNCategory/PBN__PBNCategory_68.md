## Title of the category

This category of Risks describes various limitations and challenges in terms of access to different resources and services. It includes technical limitations and limited connectivity, inequality in education access, lack of access to pharmaceutical interventions, limited access to remote work and telecommuting, limited access to outdoor environments and nature, limited access to care and essential services, rural-urban disparities in access to medical treatment, limited access to parks and physical activity environments, limited access to data and real-time information, legal problems and lack of access to legal representation, difficulties accessing finance and funding, unequal access to resources and opportunities, disparities in access to wearables and other technologies, limited site access and security concerns, limited access to testing and personal protective equipment, limited access to training and expert knowledge, limited access to fresh produce, and inequities in infrastructure access. Individuals in this list may belong to populations facing these challenges and limitations in accessing various resources, services, and opportunities.
