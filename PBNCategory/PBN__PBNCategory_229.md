# __Support for individuals in isolation or lockdown due to a communicable disease or contagious condition.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Quarantine support](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Intervention_30.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes measures to provide assistance and support to individuals who are placed in quarantine. These interventions may include delivering essential supplies such as food and medicine to individuals in quarantine, providing mental health support, connecting individuals with healthcare services, and ensuring access to appropriate medical care if needed. The individuals in this category are those who have been required to self-isolate or stay in quarantine due to potential exposure or confirmed infection with a contagious disease.
