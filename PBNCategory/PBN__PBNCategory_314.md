# __Protection against microbes.__

## Items covered in this category

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes protective coatings that have antimicrobial properties. These coatings are designed to prevent the growth and spread of microorganisms, such as bacteria and viruses, on various surfaces. Individuals in this category are likely to be experts in developing, applying, or researching antimicrobial coatings for use in infrastructure, including green buildings and their surroundings.
