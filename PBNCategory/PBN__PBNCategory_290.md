## Title of the category

This category of Enablers represents the individuals who facilitate the integration and use of digital technologies and platforms in green building neighborhoods. They possess knowledge and expertise in digital infrastructure and are skilled in implementing and managing digital platforms that enhance the sustainability and efficiency of these neighborhoods. These Enablers are essential in ensuring the smooth functioning of digital systems and technologies, which play a crucial role in monitoring energy consumption, optimizing resource utilization, promoting connectivity, and enhancing overall environmental performance in green buildings.
