# __Ensuring cleanliness and hygiene by eliminating harmful bacteria and viruses through thorough cleaning and disinfection.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Sanitizing](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Theme/PBN__BP_Theme_2.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Themes describes the importance of maintaining cleanliness and hygiene to prevent the spread of contagious diseases. Individuals in this group are focused on implementing and practicing proper sanitization measures, such as regular handwashing, disinfection of surfaces, and maintaining clean environments. They understand the significance of these practices in minimizing the risk of infections and promoting public health.
