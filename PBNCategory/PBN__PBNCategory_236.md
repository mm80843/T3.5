# __Enable open and clear exchange of information.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Facilitate transparent communication](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Intervention_37.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes measures that aim to improve communication within a group or organization by promoting transparency. These interventions aim to create an environment where information flows freely and openly, allowing for effective and clear communication between individuals. By implementing these measures, individuals will feel encouraged to share information, ideas, and feedback, ultimately enhancing collaboration and fostering a more cohesive and efficient group dynamic.
