## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents individuals and organizations involved in waste and wastewater management, water utilities, cleaning services, and research related to wastewater analysis. It includes private investors in wastewater treatment plants, waste management services, water management services, waste management authorities and agencies, water management authorities and agencies, water utilities and consumers, workers handling human waste and wastewater, wastewater treatment workers and operators, wastewater sampling personnel, waste collection workers, communities with poor waste management practices, researchers in wastewater analysis, the waste management industry, the Ministry of Electricity and Water, cleaning service providers, wastewater testing laboratories, water treatment plants, and building occupants in facilities with contaminated surfaces, faulty drainage systems, or high chemical pollutant levels.
