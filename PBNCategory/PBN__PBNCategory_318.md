# __Design inspired by and incorporating elements of nature.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Biophilic desing](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Enabler_30.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who are proponents and practitioners of biophilic design in green buildings. Biophilic design is an approach that incorporates natural elements, such as plants, natural materials, and views of nature, into the design of built environments. These individuals are passionate about creating spaces that connect people with nature, believing that this connection can have a positive impact on health, well-being, and productivity. They are likely to be architects, interior designers, or researchers who specialize in sustainable and healthy building design, and they actively promote the benefits of biophilic design in creating sustainable and resilient communities.
