# __Interconnected digital platforms facilitating communication and connection among individuals.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Social Networks](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Enabler_20.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers represents individuals who are connected and engaged within various social networks. These Enablers actively participate in social interactions, both online and offline, and value the importance of building and maintaining relationships. They possess strong communication skills and have a wide circle of acquaintances, friends, and colleagues. These individuals are adept at leveraging their social networks to access information, resources, and support, and are likely to have a significant influence on their peers.
