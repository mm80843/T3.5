# __Promote the utilization of safety gear.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Ensure use of personal protection equipment](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Intervention/PBN__BP_Intervention_23.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes actions taken to ensure the use of personal protective equipment. These measures include requiring individuals to wear masks, gloves, or other protective gear when in public spaces or interacting with others. These interventions aim to prevent the spread of contagious diseases by reducing the exposure and transmission of pathogens through direct contact or respiratory droplets.
