## Title of the category

This category of Benenefits describes the positive impacts and advantages related to the implementation of improved ventilation systems and other measures in green buildings, specifically in terms of addressing contagious diseases and improving overall health and well-being. These benefits include reduced carbon emissions, decreased respiratory illnesses in children, enhanced airflow and air circulation, improved ventilation effectiveness, heat and temperature reduction, ambient pollutant reduction, and increased air quality. Additionally, the category emphasizes the importance of natural ventilation, increased air change rate, and the use of physical barriers to reduce transmission risks. Lastly, the list highlights the potential increase in the supply of ventilators as a result of these improvements.
