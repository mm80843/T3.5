# __Devices that can be worn on the body to track health, provide information, or perform specific functions.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Wearables](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Enabler_11.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who utilize wearable technology for various purposes. Wearables refer to electronic devices that can be worn on the body, such as fitness trackers, smartwatches, or health monitoring devices. These individuals embrace technology that allows them to track and monitor their physical activity, sleep patterns, heart rate, and other health-related data. They prioritize the convenience and accessibility that wearables offer in improving their overall well-being and fitness levels.
