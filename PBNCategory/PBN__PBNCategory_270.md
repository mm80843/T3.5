## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents various community organizations, associations, and stakeholders that play a role in supporting and advocating for the community. Examples include community organizations, community associations, social organizations, and social justice organizations. It also includes community leaders and organizers who work towards the betterment of the community. Additionally, this category encompasses donors and blood donors who contribute to community welfare. Nonprofit and social service organizations, as well as social welfare organizations, are also part of this group. Furthermore, it includes organizations that provide community support networks and services, as well as those engaged in community outreach and engagement efforts.
