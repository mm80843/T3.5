## Title of the category

This category of benefits describes the positive impact of green buildings and infrastructure on individuals' mental health and well-being. It includes a reduction in fear, stress, overwhelm, anxiety, and symptoms of anxiety, depression, and stress. These benefits also contribute to a reduction in the burden individuals face, as well as improved stress management and decreased panic. Additionally, the list highlights the reduction in domestic violence and family conflict, as well as the alleviation of musculoskeletal pain and psychological distress. Overall, the individuals experiencing these benefits are likely to be part of a group who enjoy reduced stress levels and improved mental health.
