## Title of the category

This category of technologies describes a range of tools and methods used for the detection, monitoring, and prevention of contagious diseases. It includes advanced techniques in molecular biology for pathogen detection, rapid diagnostics for targeted therapy, and various strategies for vaccine development. This category also encompasses the development and research of antiviral drugs, as well as improved methods for virus concentration, identification, and testing. In addition, it includes technologies for disease surveillance, contact tracing, and the management of infectious agents. Other technologies in this category focus on the distribution and tracking of vaccines, including the development of vaccination campaigns, clinics, and reminder systems. Overall, this category represents a wide array of technologies aimed at addressing the spread of contagious diseases through detection, prevention, and treatment.
