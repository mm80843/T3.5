# __Combat misinformation and disinformation.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Prevent fake news](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Intervention/PBN__BP_Intervention_36.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions is focused on preventing the spread of fake news. It includes strategies and actions aimed at raising awareness, promoting critical thinking, and improving media literacy among individuals. These interventions are designed to combat the dissemination of false information and help individuals make informed decisions based on reliable sources.
