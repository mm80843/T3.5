# __Monitoring the spread or transmission of something.__

## Items covered in this category

## Title of the category

This category of Themes describes the monitoring of the spread or transmission of a contagious disease. It involves tracking and observing the movement and progression of the disease within a population or community. This may include monitoring the number of cases, identifying new infections, and studying the patterns and pathways of transmission. Propagation monitoring helps in understanding how a disease is spreading and can inform public health interventions to control its spread.
