## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents a wide range of individuals and organizations involved in ensuring personal safety and security, disaster management, health and safety, and emergency response. This includes disaster management agencies, health and safety officers, disaster response teams, emergency management agencies, crisis and disaster management teams, emergency responders, and emergency services and management personnel. Additionally, this category includes energy assistance programs aimed at providing support and aid during emergencies and assisting with energy-related issues. These Stakeholders work together to prevent and respond to crises, manage emergencies, ensure the safety of individuals and infrastructure, and provide necessary support and assistance in times of need.
