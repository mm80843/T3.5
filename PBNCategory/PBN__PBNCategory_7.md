## Title of the category

This category of Benenefits describes a range of positive impacts on mental, emotional, and physical health. These benefits include improved mental health outcomes, reduced health harming behaviors, enhanced psychological well-being, improved mood and productivity, improved physical health, increased physical activity, raised awareness of health gains, and reduced mental health problems. Additionally, these benefits involve improved mental well-being, enhanced emotional health and well-being, improved cardiovascular health, maintenance of physical fitness and health, reduction in sedentary behavior, and improved quality of life. This category also encompasses the promotion of mental health among healthcare providers, accessibility and standardization of mental health services, enhancement of social and emotional health, and increased motivation and adherence to exercise. Overall, this set of benefits highlights the positive impacts of green building neighborhoods on various aspects of individuals' health and well-being.
