# __Innovative methods of transporting goods safely and efficiently.__

## Items covered in this category

* [New delivery systems](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Enabler_21.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes innovative methods of delivering goods and services to individuals within a green buildings neighbourhood. These methods may include advanced technologies, such as drone deliveries or autonomous vehicles, as well as efficient and sustainable logistics systems. Individuals in this list may be involved in developing or implementing these new delivery systems to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of goods and services distribution within a green buildings neighbourhood.
