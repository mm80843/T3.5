## Title of the category

This category of Mitigations describes various strategies and actions related to data collection, analysis, sharing, and quality improvement. These include using digital techniques and technologies, implementing data-driven decision-making processes, ensuring reliable connectivity and data transmission, adopting standardized data collection methods, investing in data integration platforms and tools, implementing quality control measures for accurate and reliable data, enhancing data accuracy and real-time updates, improving data standardization and interoperability, and promoting data sharing and collaboration through open data initiatives and platforms. These Mitigations also involve developing guidelines and protocols for data collection and harmonization, establishing databases for knowledge sharing, coordinating and funding research efforts, and investing in scalable infrastructure for data storage and processing. Overall, these strategies aim to facilitate the efficient and effective use of data for informed decision-making and to promote collaboration and knowledge sharing among researchers and institutions.
