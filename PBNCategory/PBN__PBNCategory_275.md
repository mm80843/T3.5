## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents a diverse group of individuals and organizations, including small businesses, governments, private businesses, local industries, retail businesses, and online businesses. It encompasses small and medium-sized businesses, business owners, and business associations. These Stakeholders are involved in various sectors, such as retail, online, and local industries, and they play a crucial role in the economy at a local, regional, or national level. They have a stake in the development and growth of green building neighborhoods and are interested in understanding and mitigating the spread of contagious diseases within these infrastructures.
