## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents various individuals and organizations involved in the management and operation of buildings and infrastructure. It includes laboratory management, building managers, construction managers, facility managers, property managers, project managers, office managers, hospital management, waste management, water management, energy managers, environmental managers, supply chain managers, nursing managers, healthcare managers, crisis management, real estate managers, urban managers, public space managers, fund managers, data management, hiring managers, site managers, park managers, and operating room managers. These Stakeholders are responsible for overseeing and coordinating different aspects of building and infrastructure management such as construction, maintenance, facility operations, resource management, and overall governance.
