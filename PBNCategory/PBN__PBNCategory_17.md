## Title of the category

This category of Benenefits describes the various ways in which green buildings and infrastructure can contribute to reducing the risk and spread of infectious diseases. These benefits include measures to control and prevent infection, such as infection control measures, enhanced infection control, and improved infection control. They also encompass the containment and control of epidemics and disease outbreaks, with a focus on preventing mass gatherings and minimizing the transmission of diseases through shared facilities and activities. Additionally, these Benenefits highlight the importance of effective pandemic response and disease prevention, including the conversion of spaces into infection and isolation wards. Overall, these Benenefits emphasize the goal of creating a safe environment that reduces the risk of infection and transmission, mitigates disease spread, and protects individuals from various pathogens, including the coronavirus.
