## Title of the category

This group of stakeholders represents a wide range of individuals and groups who may be affected by contagious diseases, particularly in green buildings neighborhoods. It includes workers in global supply chains, high-risk industries, and the informal sector, as well as women experiencing economic hardship and individuals facing economic crisis and uncertainties. It also encompasses workers in various industries such as manufacturing, technology, and the service industry, as well as individuals and households facing job loss or economic instability. Additionally, it includes workers in close-proximity work environments, poorly ventilated work environments, and workplaces with air recirculation systems. Other stakeholders in this list are workers at live markets, individuals affected by the loss of loved ones or economic hardships, and individuals with comorbidities. It also includes small businesses and self-employed individuals, as well as workers affected by restructuring. Finally, it encompasses workers in the renewable energy sector and the gig economy.
