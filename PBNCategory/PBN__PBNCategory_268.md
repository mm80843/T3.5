## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents a diverse range of individuals including young people, both males and females, women and young children, vulnerable families, families with children, children of all ages, particularly young children, kindergartens and youth. It also includes parents, older adults, and older people, with specific concerns related to their age. Additionally, this category encompasses migrants and racial and ethnic minorities, as well as women and children in developing countries. It addresses the needs and concerns of newborns, individuals belonging to all age groups, including younger and older age groups. It also includes large families and highlights the specific needs and concerns of racial and ethnic minorities and children of immigrants. Overall, this category encompasses a wide range of stakeholders and considers age-specific concerns, particularly those related to children.
