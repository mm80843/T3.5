## Title of the category

This category of Benenefits describes the various ways in which individuals in a workforce are protected, supported, and provided with a conducive work environment during a contagious disease outbreak. These benefits range from improved job security and minimized exposure to enhanced worker safety and protection. It also encompasses strategies for work-life balance, mitigating job displacement, and ensuring sufficient labor at sites. Additionally, it addresses gender equality in the workforce, the sustainability of workplaces during a pandemic, and the protection of healthcare workers. Other benefits include employee safety, new staff onboarding, workplace adaptation, increased efficiency, and increased flexibility in work arrangements. Ultimately, these benefits aim to prioritize the safety and health of workers while minimizing job losses and maintaining low employee positivity rates.
