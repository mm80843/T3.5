# __Spreading information to increase knowledge or understanding.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Raising awareness](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Intervention_38.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes efforts to increase knowledge and understanding among individuals regarding a specific issue or topic. This can involve campaigns, educational programs, or public conversations aimed at informing and engaging people about a particular subject. People involved in raising awareness often seek to bring attention to important issues, promote behavioral changes, and encourage active participation from the community.
