## Title of the category

This category of Benenefits describes the various ways in which green buildings neighbourhoods improve accessibility and provide essential resources to individuals. It includes benefits such as increased accessibility, improved access to water and sanitation, stable internet access, local food supply and production, food and nutrition security, priority access to essential goods, improved access to healthy diets, improved access to patients, sustained access to necessary resources, and improved support accessibility. Other benefits in this category include increased access to care, ensured access to necessities, improved availability and access to wearables, improved access to information and communication, improved food availability and quality, improved infrastructure and increased connectivity, improved testing access, increased inclusivity and accessibility, ease in production, scalability and accessibility, enhanced citizen ownership, enhanced control over city access, and food security and sustainability.
