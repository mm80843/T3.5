## Title of the category

This category of technologies describes various methods and tools used for remote communication and access to healthcare services. It includes telehealth services, telemedicine, telehealth/telepresence, and telehealth technology. These technologies enable remote diagnostics, virtual therapy and counseling, and access to mental health support through telepsychiatry and teletherapy platforms. They also involve the use of telephones, teleconferencing tools, and teledevices for secure communication and conferencing. Additionally, telehealth platforms and software are utilized for virtual therapy and counseling, while telemedicine technologies enable remote healthcare and access to mobile health clinics. Teleoperation technology allows for remote operation and control. Television is also a part of this category, with its potential for disseminating healthcare information and education remotely.
