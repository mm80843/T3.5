## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents a diverse group of individuals who reside within a specific area or community. They include urban residents, public housing residents, local residents, community residents, city residents, nearby residents, Indonesian residents, apartment residents, gated community residents, non-gated community residents, public and social housing residents, and future residents. This group also encompasses surgical residents and residents with restricted access to resources. The term "resident" is used in various contexts such as urban resident, gated community resident, non-gated community resident, and community resident. Additionally, the category includes bipoc residents, referring to residents who identify as Black, Indigenous, and People of Color.
