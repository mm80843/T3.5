## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders describes individuals involved in the tourism and hospitality industries, including visitors, tourists, tourism destinations, tourism providers and operators, workers in the tourism industry, hospitality industry workers, hotel staff, and small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in the tourism and hospitality sectors. These stakeholders also include individuals who travel and book accommodations, as well as those in industries reliant on tourism and travel. It encompasses individuals in the tourism sector, including tourism boards. Specific groups within this category include travelers to/from China, pilgrims visiting Saudi Arabia for religious purposes, and visitors to underground spaces (UGS) and points of interest (POIs).
