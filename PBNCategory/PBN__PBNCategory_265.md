## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders describes individuals who are at higher risk or susceptible to contagious diseases, including those living in high-risk areas, individuals at risk or in close proximity to others, pedestrians and users of streets, individuals living alone or in isolation, people with disabilities or specific conditions, individuals attending events, those who rely heavily on social media for information, individuals in non-English speaking countries, persons in prison or detention, people in developing countries, men and women in South Africa, individuals with limited social connection or in close social or work settings, human resources personnel responsible for implementing preventive measures, people without internet access or limited digital literacy, individuals in multilingual environments, individuals practicing preventive measures, individuals belonging to different gender identities, occupants in crowded places or buildings, and individuals whose data is in the knowledge graph.
