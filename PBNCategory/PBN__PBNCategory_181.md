## Title of the category

This category of technologies describes a range of water and waste management systems that are commonly found in green buildings and neighborhoods. It includes technologies for water treatment, such as water treatment systems, water purification technology, and improved water treatment technologies. Waste management technologies, such as waste management systems, waste segregation systems, and waste disposal infrastructure, are also part of this category. Additionally, there are technologies focused on wastewater management, such as wastewater testing, wastewater treatment plants, and sewage monitoring. Other technologies in this category include rainwater storage and collection, smart water consumption technologies, and water-efficient fixtures. These technologies are essential for improving water and waste management, promoting sustainability, and ensuring the health and safety of individuals in green buildings and neighborhoods.
