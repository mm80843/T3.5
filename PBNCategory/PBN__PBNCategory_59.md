## Title of the category

This category of Risks describes various issues related to water and waste management in green building neighborhoods. It includes deteriorating and lost biodiversity, environmental contamination, and pollution. Poor surface water drainage, inadequate waste management, and insufficient filtration can lead to water contamination and reduced water quality. Increased water consumption and inadequate wastewater management are also potential risks. The category also includes challenges in wastewater analysis, limited availability of biosensors for detection, and the complexity of the wastewater matrix. In addition, there are risks associated with waste recycling and disposal. Overall, these Risks highlight the importance of proper water and waste management to ensure the health and safety of individuals in green building neighborhoods.
