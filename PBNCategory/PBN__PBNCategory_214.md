# __Material assortment.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Selection of materials](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Intervention/PBN__BP_Intervention_15.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes the process of choosing materials for construction or renovation projects. It encompasses the careful selection of materials based on their environmental impact, such as their energy efficiency, sustainability, and potential for off-gassing. This includes considering factors like the use of recycled or responsibly sourced materials, low VOC (volatile organic compound) emissions, and the potential for reducing waste throughout the building's lifecycle. By prioritizing the use of environmentally friendly materials, this intervention contributes to creating greener and healthier living spaces.
