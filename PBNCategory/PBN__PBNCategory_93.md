## Title of the category

This category of Risks describes the various issues related to inadequate ventilation in different settings, including hospitals, public buildings, indoor spaces, and existing buildings. It also includes risks associated with inefficient ventilation systems, poor air quality, and challenges in ventilation and air conditioning. This category further encompasses risks related to respiratory illnesses, shortages of ICU beds and ventilators, as well as challenges in ventilator production. Overall, individuals in this list may be those who face risks due to inadequate ventilation, poor air quality, and shortcomings in respiratory support systems.
