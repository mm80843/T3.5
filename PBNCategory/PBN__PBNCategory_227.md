# __Flexible and adaptable solutions for various purposes or configurations.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Introduce "multi-use" / modular designs](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Intervention/PBN__BP_Intervention_28.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes the implementation of "multi-use" or modular designs in infrastructure, such as green buildings neighbourhoods. These designs enable the flexibility of spaces and functions, allowing for the easy adaptation of the infrastructure to different needs and purposes. Individuals who are involved in projects that focus on developing and implementing these types of designs can be grouped together based on their interest and expertise in creating and promoting adaptable and flexible spaces within infrastructure.
