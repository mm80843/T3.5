# __Building water system upkeep and purification to ensure cleanliness and safety.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Maintenance / decontamination of building water sytems](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Intervention/PBN__BP_Intervention_9.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes activities aimed at maintaining and decontaminating the water systems within buildings. These interventions focus on preventing and addressing the spread of contagious diseases through water sources such as pipes, faucets, and water storage tanks. By regularly maintaining and decontaminating these systems, individuals in the list are taking steps to ensure the quality and safety of the water supply in their buildings, thereby reducing the risk of disease transmission within the green buildings neighborhood.
