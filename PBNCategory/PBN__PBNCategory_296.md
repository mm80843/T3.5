# __Devices that detect the presence or level of water.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Water sensors](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Enabler_7.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers represents individuals who utilize water sensors. These devices are designed to detect and monitor water flow, levels, and quality in various settings, including green buildings neighborhoods. These Enablers play a role in promoting sustainable water management practices by constantly monitoring and providing valuable data on water usage patterns. Their use contributes to the efficient use of water resources and the prevention of water-related issues.
