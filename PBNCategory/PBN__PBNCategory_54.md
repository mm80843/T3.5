## Title of the category

This category of Risks describes various aspects related to social distancing, social interactions, and social inequalities. It includes risks such as inconsistent or insufficient implementation of social distancing, lack of social interaction, psychological impacts of social isolation, disparities in social and economic contexts, and negative perception of certain housing residents. These risks highlight the importance of social distancing measures, the impact on well-being and mental health, and the implications of social inequalities on contagious disease spread. They also emphasize the need for social support, community engagement, and considerations for social and environmental factors in order to address these risks effectively.
