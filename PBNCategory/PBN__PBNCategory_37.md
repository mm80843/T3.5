## Title of the category

This category of benefits describes the various ways in which individuals can improve the quality of air in their indoor environments. These benefits include improved indoor air quality, enhanced air quality, and indoor environmental quality improvement. They also encompass specific measures such as air cleaning in specific areas, fresh air supply, and air filtration. Additionally, this category includes benefits related to the understanding of the indoor-outdoor relationship, improved outdoor air quality, and equitable indoor environments. The aim of these benefits is to create clean and healthier indoor spaces, preventing the spread of viruses and promoting comfort and well-being.
