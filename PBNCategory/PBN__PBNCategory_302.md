## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes the emerging technologies of robots, drones, and automated vehicles. These advanced machines have the capability to perform tasks autonomously or remotely, without human intervention. Robots are programmable machines that can carry out a wide range of functions, while drones are unmanned aerial vehicles that can fly and perform specific tasks. Automated vehicles, on the other hand, are self-driving cars or trucks that can navigate the roads without human control. The individuals mentioned in this list are likely associated with the development, deployment, or utilization of these technologies in the context of green building neighborhoods.
