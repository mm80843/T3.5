## Items covered in this category

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes a type of technology used in green buildings and other infrastructure to improve indoor air quality. HEPA filters are known for their high efficiency in removing airborne particles such as dust, pollen, and bacteria. They are commonly used in ventilation systems to filter and clean the air, reducing the spread of contaminants and promoting a healthier environment. Individuals associated with this category are likely to be professionals involved in the design, installation, and maintenance of ventilation systems in green buildings. They have expertise in ensuring the filtration systems are effective in reducing airborne pollutants and improving indoor air quality.
