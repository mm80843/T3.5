## Title of the category

This category of Benenefits describes the various ways in which green buildings and their infrastructure contribute to the management and prevention of the spread of contagious diseases, particularly Covid-19. These include early detection of Covid-19 cases, efficient crisis management, reduced transmission and spread of the virus, enhanced tracking and prediction of cases, improved remote monitoring and diagnosis, early detection of risks, minimize airborne transmission in workplaces, lower mortality rates, containment of spread, more accurate estimation of cases, mapping disease progression and identifying long Covid cases, increased awareness of risk, access to accurate and up-to-date data, reliable and accurate early detection of risks, reduction in infections, and treatment options for Covid-19.
