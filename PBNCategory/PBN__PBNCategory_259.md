## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents a diverse group of professionals and experts involved in various aspects of green building and infrastructure. They include policy advisors, risk assessors, design professionals, and technical experts who contribute to the planning and implementation of sustainable and resilient buildings. Other stakeholders in this category are data scientists, analysts, and experts who use data-driven approaches to inform decision-making and address challenges related to energy, technology, innovation, and indoor air quality. Additionally, the list includes legal advisors who provide guidance on legal aspects, communication experts who facilitate effective communication and outreach, and IT professionals involved in managing and securing data and technology. The stakeholders also encompass linguistic experts, meteorologists, and ontology experts who each bring their specialized knowledge and skills to support the development and operation of green buildings and infrastructure. Finally, this category includes residents with limited digital literacy to ensure inclusivity and address the needs and challenges of all community members.
