## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents a wide range of individuals who interact with or are affected by various types of public spaces, including public places, public spaces, interior public spaces, green spaces, urban green spaces, private green spaces, poorly maintained green spaces, and social spaces. It includes visitors, users, and individuals frequenting these spaces, as well as those with limited access to green spaces, especially those without access to private outdoor spaces. It also encompasses specific groups such as rural women diagnosed with ovarian cancer, individuals living near poorly maintained green spaces, and the general population in areas with underfunded green space. Overall, this category comprises people from different backgrounds and circumstances who are involved in or impacted by the use, accessibility, and conditions of public and green spaces.
