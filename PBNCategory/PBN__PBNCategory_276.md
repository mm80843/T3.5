## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents a wide range of individuals and entities involved in various aspects of development and technology. This includes software developers, app developers, mobile application developers, and technology developers who create and innovate digital tools, platforms, and applications. It also includes blockchain developers and ontology developers who specialize in creating decentralized systems and digital frameworks. Additionally, this category encompasses community developers and project developers who are involved in community building and project management. Apart from developers, there are users of digital tools and platforms, including app users and users of digital tools, who interact with these technologies. Lastly, this category includes energy modeling software developers and research institutions, as well as those involved in AI development.
