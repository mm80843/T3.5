## Title of the category

This category of Risks describes the impact of various factors on the workforce in green building neighborhoods. It includes job loss, remote work challenges, unemployment, and income decline. It also highlights the disruption of immigrant employment, concerns about job security, and uncertainties about job status. Additionally, it mentions workforce management problems, labor shortages, workplace safety issues, and exploitation of workers. The category further addresses changes in work schedules, poor work productivity, and blurring of work-life boundaries. Overall, these Risks indicate the potential vulnerabilities and challenges faced by individuals in the workforce within green building neighborhoods.
