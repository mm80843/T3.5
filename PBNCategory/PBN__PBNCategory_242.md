## Title of the category

This category of stakeholders represents various entities involved in the governance and policymaking processes at different levels, ranging from local to national and global. It includes policy-makers, policy bodies, government officials, governments, national and sub-national governments, state governments, city governments, government departments, governmental entities, government regulators, local and regional governments, democratically elected leaders, and officials/administrators of government sites. Additionally, it encompasses organizations supporting evidence-informed policy-making, political actors, and border control officials. The stakeholders also involve the public, citizens, users of public places, and the general public in need of specific services or support, such as police or psychological first aid. Overall, this category encapsulates the range of individuals and organizations involved in public governance, policy formation, regulation, and service provision.
