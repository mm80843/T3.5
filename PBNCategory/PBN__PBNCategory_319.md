# __Communication through electronic displays.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Digital signage](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Enabler/PBN__BP_Enabler_31.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes the use of digital signboards and displays in infrastructure, such as green buildings neighborhoods. Digitals signage refers to the technology and systems used to display and relay information through electronic displays and screens. These Enablers are involved in the implementation and maintenance of digital signage solutions in order to communicate important messages, advertisements, or other visual content to the occupants or visitors of the green buildings.
