## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents a variety of individuals and organizations involved in the infrastructure and services related to green buildings and technology. It includes energy providers, both general and specific to electricity, as well as essential service providers. Service providers, including internet service providers and network operators, are also part of this group. Data collectors, data providers, and data sharing partners play a role in information management and security. Technology providers, including those in the IoT industry, offer innovative solutions and support for green buildings. Users of IoT devices and mobile apps, as well as network administrators, are stakeholders who interact with these technologies. Support networks and IT support are also included. Overall, this category encompasses a broad range of stakeholders involved in the functioning and support of green buildings and related infrastructure.
