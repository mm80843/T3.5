# __Effective organization and control of data to facilitate efficient access and utilization.__

## Items covered in this category

## Title of the category

This category of Themes describes the management and organization of information. It includes topics such as data storage, retrieval, analysis, and protection. Individuals in this category are likely to have expertise in database management, data governance, information security, and data analytics. They are proficient in implementing effective information management strategies to ensure the availability, integrity, and confidentiality of data within an organization.
