# __Actions and patterns of individuals online.__

## Items covered in this category

* [User behavior](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Theme_4.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Themes describes the study and analysis of individual behavior and actions in various contexts, including their role in contagious disease spread within green building neighborhoods. It involves examining how individuals interact with their environment, follow or deviate from guidelines and regulations, and make decisions that may impact the transmission of diseases. Understanding user behavior is crucial in developing effective strategies and interventions to minimize the risk of contagion within these communities.
