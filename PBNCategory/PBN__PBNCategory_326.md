## Title of the category

This category of Themes describes the design and layout of physical spaces, with a focus on how they impact the spread of contagious diseases. It includes factors such as the arrangement of rooms, the flow of people within a space, the availability of natural light and ventilation, and the integration of green spaces. Individuals related to this theme are likely to be architects, interior designers, and urban planners who specialize in creating environments that minimize the risk of disease transmission.
