# __Extracting insights from information to inform decision-making.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Data Analytics](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Enabler/PBN__BP_Enabler_2.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers represents individuals who excel in the field of data analytics. They possess advanced skills and knowledge in data analysis techniques and tools, allowing them to gather, interpret, and derive meaningful insights from large sets of data. These individuals are proficient in statistical analysis, data modeling, and data visualization, and they can effectively use data to identify patterns, trends, and correlations. Their expertise in data analytics enables them to make informed decisions, solve complex problems, and optimize business processes.
