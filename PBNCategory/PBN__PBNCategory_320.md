# __Medical tools for identifying illnesses and conditions.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Disease Diagnostic Test Kits](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Enabler_32.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers represents individuals who manufacture or distribute disease diagnostic test kits. These kits are designed to detect and diagnose contagious diseases in individuals. These Enablers play a crucial role in the early detection and containment of diseases, as their test kits offer a quick and convenient way to identify infected individuals. Their contribution to public health is significant, as their products help to prevent the spread of diseases by enabling early treatment and isolation of infected individuals.
