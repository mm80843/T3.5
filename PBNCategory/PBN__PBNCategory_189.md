## Title of the category

This category of Technologys describes a wide range of tools, initiatives, and strategies aimed at promoting and maintaining green spaces in urban areas. These Technologys include various approaches to making green infrastructure more accessible, such as the creation of green corridors and the development of green spaces in residential areas. They also encompass technologies for designing and managing green spaces, such as green roofs, green walls, and green facades. Additionally, this category includes methodologies for assessing the quality of green spaces, as well as strategies for incorporating green spaces into urban planning and design. Overall, these Technologys represent a comprehensive set of solutions for enhancing the availability and functionality of green spaces in order to promote healthier and more sustainable urban environments.
