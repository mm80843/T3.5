# __Effective management of individuals to maintain safe distancing and protect vulnerable groups from potential infection.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Occupancy control (eg separating at/risk contaminated population)](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Intervention/PBN__BP_Intervention_18.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes measures that involve controlling the occupancy of a space or separating populations that are at risk or contaminated. These measures can include strategies such as isolating individuals who are infected or at a higher risk of getting infected in order to prevent the spread of contagious diseases within a green buildings neighborhood or other infrastructure.
