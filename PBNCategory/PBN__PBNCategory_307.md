# __Portable internet-enabled devices.__

## Items covered in this category

* [SmartPhones](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Enabler/PBN__BP_Enabler_19.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who possess or use smartphones. Smartphones are handheld devices that combine the function of a mobile phone with advanced computing capabilities and internet access. These individuals are likely to be connected to a wide range of online platforms, apps, and social media networks, allowing them to easily communicate and share information with others. They can access real-time information, news updates, and communicate instantly with others, making them potential carriers for the spread of contagious diseases in green building neighborhoods.
