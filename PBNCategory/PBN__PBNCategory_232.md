# __Enable remote sector rehabilitation assistance.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Support recovery of sectors via remote work](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP/PBN__BP_Intervention_33.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes efforts to facilitate the recovery of different sectors by promoting the adoption of remote work. These initiatives aim to mitigate the impact of contagious diseases on businesses and industries by enabling employees to work from home or other remote locations. By encouraging remote work, these interventions aim to limit the spread of diseases within physical workplaces and reduce the potential for new infections.
