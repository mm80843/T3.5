## Title of the category

This category of Stakeholders represents various individuals and groups who belong to low-income areas, households, communities, or neighborhoods. It includes low-income individuals, families, citizens, renters, earners, and workers. Specifically, it encompasses low-income individuals and families in urban areas, as well as individuals from low-income backgrounds and vulnerable groups such as minorities. Furthermore, it encompasses individuals with low well-being, particularly those in low-income countries. Overall, this group of Stakeholders comprises those who are economically disadvantaged and may face additional challenges in accessing resources and opportunities.
