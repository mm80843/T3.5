## Title of the category

This category of Benenefits describes the positive impacts of implementing strategies and measures to reduce the spread of contagious diseases in green buildings neighbourhoods. It emphasizes the reduction of bias in estimation, prevention of misinformation sharing, and noise reduction. It also highlights the effectiveness of policies and treatment strategies, as well as the decreased spread of fake news and misinformation. Furthermore, it emphasizes improved communication and dissemination of accurate information, combating misinformation, and prevention of false information spread. Additionally, this category mentions the improved understanding and analysis of public spaces and the prevention and reduction of misinformation dissemination.
