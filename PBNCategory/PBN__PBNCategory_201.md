## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes the measures taken to control environmental parameters in green building neighborhoods. These interventions aim to regulate factors such as temperature, humidity, air quality, and lighting levels to create a healthy and comfortable living environment. By implementing technologies like smart thermostats, ventilation systems, and air purifiers, individuals can ensure optimal conditions that promote well-being and reduce the risk of contagious diseases.
