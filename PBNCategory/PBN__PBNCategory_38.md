## Title of the category

This category of Benenefits describes the advantages related to the prevention and reduction of the spread of contagious diseases, specifically respiratory syndromes such as SARS and SARS-CoV-2. It includes benefits such as the prevention of severe respiratory syndromes, reduction in the transmission of SARS-CoV-2, and a reduced risk of contracting the virus. Additionally, it encompasses benefits like early detection of SARS-CoV-2 infection, rapid inactivation of the virus, and a reduced risk of transmission on various objects. Furthermore, it includes benefits related to confirming the presence and identity of SARS-CoV-2 in wastewater, enhancing recovery of the virus from wastewater, and a reduction in airborne transmission of SARS-CoV-2.
