## Title of the category

This category of Enablers describes individuals who are involved in the process of acquiring images and operating closed-circuit television (CCTV) systems. These individuals may include photographers, videographers, security personnel, or technicians who specialize in capturing visual footage for various purposes such as documenting events, monitoring surveillance systems, or analyzing image data. Their role is crucial in maintaining and safeguarding the security of a building or neighborhood, as well as gathering visual evidence for investigations or identifying potential threats or risks. These Enablers are skilled in operating advanced camera equipment, managing video feeds, and ensuring the quality and accuracy of the images obtained. They play a vital role in the overall infrastructure of green buildings neighborhoods by contributing to enhanced security and surveillance measures.
