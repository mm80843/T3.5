# __Implementing safeguards for the security and integrity of potable water systems.__

## Items covered in this category

* [Managing drinking water safety measures](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/BP_Intervention/PBN__BP_Intervention_8.md)

## Title of the category

This category of Interventions describes measures aimed at ensuring the safety of drinking water in a given infrastructure or neighborhood. These interventions involve implementing strategies and protocols to monitor and maintain the quality of drinking water, including regular testing, treatment processes, and adherence to safety standards. Individuals involved in managing drinking water safety measures would be responsible for overseeing the implementation of these interventions, ensuring compliance with regulations, and taking appropriate actions to address any issues or concerns related to the quality of the drinking water.
