* [Extended data collection process may expose interviewees and interviewers to multiple sources of contagion](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_9759.md)
* [Gathering individuals from different organizations and backgrounds increases the risk of exposure to different covid-19 variants and infection rates.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_9760.md)
* [In-person interviews may increase the risk of covid-19 transmission if proper safety measures are not followed.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20049.md)
