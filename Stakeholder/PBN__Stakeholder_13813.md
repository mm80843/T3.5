# __And communities near construction sites__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Changes in global wind patterns and increased rainfall can propagate the spread of malaria during the construction life cycle.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_17290.md)
* [Increased precipitation, relative humidity, and temperature conditions can create a more suitable habitat for the malaria vector.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_17291.md)
* [Pools of stagnant water developing in areas of intense excavation and earth removal work during the construction phase provide breeding grounds for malaria-causing mosquitoes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_17292.md)
