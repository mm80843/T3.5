# __Food truck operators__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Customers gathering around food trucks may facilitate the spread of contagious diseases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10911.md)
* [Enabling customers to interact with each other while respecting social distancing guidelines may lead to challenges in maintaining strict adherence to the guidelines, increasing the risk of contagious disease transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10914.md)
* [Fostering connections with other businesses](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10915.md)
* [Increased popularity of food trucks leading to higher consumer exposure to different cuisines](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10928.md)
* [Vicariously participating in everyday business and increasing perceived transparency may lead to complacency in following social distancing and health guidelines, risking the spread of contagious diseases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10976.md)

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Collaboration with a local store may lead to shared facilities and increased interaction among customers, raising the risk of contagious disease transmission](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10912.md)
* [Customers engaging in prosocial behavior](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10913.md)
* [Customers sharing food-related experiences in person and online may contribute to the spread of contagious diseases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10914.md)
* [Enabling customers to interact with each other while respecting social distancing guidelines may lead to challenges in maintaining strict adherence to the guidelines, increasing the risk of contagious disease transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10915.md)
* [Inconsistent delivery times](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10916.md)
* [Ineffective communication of health and safety measures](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10917.md)
* [Lack of clear communication about orders](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10918.md)
* [Lack of consistent food truck scheduling](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10927.md)
* [Lack of social distancing in the food truck environment](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10936.md)
* [Limited food truck capacity leading to scarcity of popular food items](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10937.md)
* [Limited physical locations may lead to overcrowding and congestion in the area, increasing the risk of contagious disease transmission](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10938.md)
* [Promotion of specific days and times for food truck visits may lead to a concentration of customers at those times, increasing the risk of contagious disease transmission](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10939.md)
* [Regularized schedules leading to high demand and competition for meals](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10940.md)
* [Reliance on social media for customer engagement](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10942.md)
* [Sharing information about food preparation and the people behind the business may lead to an increased risk of spreading contagious diseases if proper hygiene and safety practices are not followed.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10943.md)
* [Vicariously participating in everyday business and increasing perceived transparency may lead to complacency in following social distancing and health guidelines, risking the spread of contagious diseases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10944.md)
