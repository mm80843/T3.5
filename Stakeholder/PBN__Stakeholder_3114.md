# __Smart city administrators__

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Data collection from multiple sources in urban areas increases the risk of contagion spread through contact with surfaces or individuals carrying the virus.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_3830.md)
* [Dependence on smart city data network and terminal tracking systems may lead to privacy concerns and misuse of personal data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_3831.md)
* [Integration of diverse data sources in smart city networks increases the complexity of tracking and tracing potential infectious cases, leading to delays in containment efforts.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_17688.md)
