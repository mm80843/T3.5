# __Database administrators__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Extraction of virus taxonomy tree from ncbi and sars-cov-2 phylogeny kg construction may lead to unintentional release of sensitive genetic information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_3455.md)
* [Incomplete or inaccurate search queries may result in irrelevant or biased results](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_7289.md)
* [Integration of virus, human proteins, antiviral drugs, and diseases data increases the risk of unintentional release of sensitive information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_7343.md)
* [Misreporting in the si-vic database, leading to discrepancies in patient data](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_12109.md)
* [Potential bias in search results due to discipline limitations](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_12110.md)
* [Relying on a government database for hospitalization data introduces a risk of data accuracy and integrity issues.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_12111.md)
* [Retrieving 536 media reports from the database nexis could expose individuals to potentially contagious diseases if the database was accessed in a high-risk environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_17813.md)
* [Use of sqlite database to store covid-19 data](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_17931.md)
* [Use of virus data in ncbi database for kg construction may increase the risk of accidental release of sensitive information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20046.md)
