# __Visitors to the store__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Frequent changes to signs and their locations create potential confusion and inconsistency in communicating covid-related information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_18445.md)
* [Informal signs printed on paper with regular tape may contribute to the spread of contagious diseases if not properly handled or disposed.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_18446.md)
* [Use of traditional paper signs for covid-related information limits the ability to dynamically update and adapt messaging in real-time.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_18447.md)
