# __Data collection organizations__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Difficulty in obtaining data in a public health safety emergency, which usually features missing or incomplete coverage](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6518.md)
* [Lack of personal data on individuals infected by sars-cov-2](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20674.md)

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Limited data on built environment attributes in some cities may lead to incomplete understanding of the factors influencing disease spread in those areas.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_9524.md)
* [Limited dataset size for 2020 only dataset and 2018 vs. 2020 dataset](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_11767.md)
* [Response biases in self-reported compliance data](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14430.md)
* [Unequal distribution of housing units across states in the datasets](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15216.md)
* [Use of big data and ai for tracking people and infection spread](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_19307.md)
* [Use of digital data collection devices for monitoring public space usage raises legal and ethical concerns regarding privacy and surveillance.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_19971.md)
* [Use of mobile device location data for data collection may raise privacy concerns.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20326.md)
* [Utilization of pedestrian detection models may lead to increased interaction with potentially infected individuals during data collection.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20327.md)
