## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Concentration of individuals in gated communities may lead to rapid spread of contagious diseases if preventive measures are not strictly enforced.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4539.md)

* [Presence of gated communities may create a false sense of security and lead to decreased vigilance in disease prevention measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4541.md)
