# __University management and facilities management__

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Congested academic and administrative buildings may facilitate the spread of contagious diseases due to close proximity and high occupancy.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14088.md)
* [Inadequate parking facilities and overhanging structures may lead to congestion and close contact among vehicle occupants, increasing the risk of disease transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14089.md)
* [Inadequate workshop facilities and limited space may impact the ability to implement proper hygiene and distancing measures, increasing the risk of disease transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14090.md)
