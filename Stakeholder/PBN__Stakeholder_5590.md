# __Wearable technology manufacturers__

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Concerns about the battery life of smart wearables](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6631.md)
* [Dependence on wearables for health monitoring may lead to neglect of traditional healthcare practices and face-to-face medical consultations, resulting in delayed or inadequate medical care for non-covid-19 related conditions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6632.md)
* [Privacy and security risks associated with the transmission and storage of sensitive health data from wearables to cloud-based platforms.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6633.md)
* [Use of wearables to monitor covid-19 symptoms may lead to inaccurate readings or interpretations, resulting in misdiagnosis or inappropriate treatment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6644.md)
* [Wearable devices collecting and transmitting health data may pose privacy and security risks, potentially leading to unauthorized access or misuse of sensitive personal information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6761.md)
* [Wearable devices may lead to false alarms or misdiagnosis of covid-19 symptoms, leading to unnecessary panic or burden on healthcare systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6763.md)
