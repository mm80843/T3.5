# __And users of built environments__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Connecting to the local natural environment through incorporating regional features geomorphology and landscape in buildings could generate the ’sense of place’ and ’sense of community’ and realize personal identity, belonging, and cohesion.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10363.md)
* [Contact with natural features vegetation and water can generate a quick and positive psychological reaction, reducing negative emotions and fostering recovery from physiological stress and health problems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10364.md)
* [The emotional need for ’nature’ is explained as an inherited affection from the experience of choosing habitats and building dwellings, which can lead to a preference for natural characteristics in modern architectural spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10365.md)
