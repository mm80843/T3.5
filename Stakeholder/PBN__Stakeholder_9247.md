# __Retailers and store management__

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Inadequate space in stores can lead to crowded and congested environments, increasing the risk of disease transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_11259.md)
* [Increased risk of contagious disease transmission through physical contact with payment devices and surfaces in stores.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_11303.md)
* [Limited availability of products in stores may lead to increased physical interactions and longer shopping durations, contributing to disease transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_11304.md)
* [Physical store crowding and safety management](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_11305.md)
