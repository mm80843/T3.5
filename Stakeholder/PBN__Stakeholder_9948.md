# __Building management or owners__

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Contamination of indoor air with formaldehyde and toluene](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_12155.md)
* [Higher concentration of vocs in buildings can lead to skin, throat, and respiratory irritation, as well as broader set of illnesses including headaches, fatigue, and respiratory symptoms.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_12156.md)
* [Introduction of additional humidity by the air filtration system](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_12157.md)
* [Mixture of pollutants in indoor air can generate secondary pollutants that are more harmful than the primary ones.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15303.md)
* [Overserving of air filtration for a smaller space](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15304.md)
* [Presence of biological agents in indoor air can degrade overall air quality and cause respiratory diseases and allergic sensitization.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15305.md)
