# __Building management or facility maintenance team__

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Continuous cleaning and sterilization practices may not be effective in preventing the spread of contagious diseases if not done properly or if the cleaning products used are not effective against the specific pathogen.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_9007.md)
* [Limited occupancy measures may not be effectively enforced without proper control measures or technologies, leading to overcrowding and increased risk of physical interactions and disease transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_9009.md)
* [Measured energy consumption of refrigerators](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10216.md)
* [Reduced occupancy leading to continuous operation of systems](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10217.md)
* [Removal of shared and reusable resources may reduce the risk of surface contamination, but if not replaced with adequate alternatives, it may lead to dissatisfaction among building occupants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10218.md)
