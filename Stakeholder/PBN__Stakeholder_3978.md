# __And visitors in built environments__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Contagious disease propagation in the built environment](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4688.md)
* [Historical pandemics like cholera, typhoid, tuberculosis, and the third plague pandemic have influenced the design and infrastructure of the built environment, potentially contributing to the spread of contagious diseases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_13104.md)
* [Hypothesized social distance may not reflect practical conditions, especially in spaces with strong population mobility or irregular shapes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_13679.md)
* [Inadequate consideration of other significant paths of virus transmission, such as direct contact and droplet nuclei.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_13681.md)
* [Increased risk of contagious diseases in the built environment due to lack of worker protection and health-care facility concerns.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15013.md)
* [Lack of biophilic design and healing environments](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_18317.md)
