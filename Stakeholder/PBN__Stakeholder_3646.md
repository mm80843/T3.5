# __Interviewees__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Close proximity during interviews may increase the risk of disease transmission](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4349.md)
* [Emergent coding used to identify themes and ideas within the data transcribed from interviews](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4351.md)
* [Extended data collection process may expose interviewees and interviewers to multiple sources of contagion](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4353.md)
* [Gathering individuals from different organizations and backgrounds increases the risk of exposure to different covid-19 variants and infection rates.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_9758.md)
* [In-person interviews conducted at construction sites may expose researchers and interviewees to contagious diseases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_9759.md)
* [In-person interviews may increase the risk of covid-19 transmission if proper safety measures are not followed.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_9760.md)
* [Lack of availability for in-person interviews may affect the depth and quality of information gathered from interviewees.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_11219.md)
* [Potential spread of contagious diseases during in-person interviews and meetings](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_11221.md)
* [Potential spread of covid-19 due to extensive interviewing and interaction with people from various companies and organizations](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_12619.md)
* [Transmission of misinformation or incomplete information about workplace management practices and covid-19 response due to lack of validation and confirmation at the end of interviews.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_12621.md)
* [Unidentified health and safety protocols during interactions with interviewees from various companies and organizations](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20049.md)
* [Unsanitary interview conditions may promote the spread of contagious diseases](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20050.md)
* [Unstructured interviews may lead to inconsistent data collection and analysis.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20051.md)
* [Zoom video conferencing services may be vulnerable to security breaches and privacy concerns.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20052.md)
