# __Household occupants__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Close proximity and high density of occupants in housing units may lead to increased risk of contagious disease transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4733.md)
* [Data obtained from household survey may contain personal or sensitive information about occupants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_8177.md)
* [Inadequate handling and delivery system for goods and services may lead to potential exposure to contaminants during the transfer of items into the home.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_8178.md)
* [Increase in household energy use due to prolonged time spent at home during the covid-19 pandemic.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_8179.md)
* [Increased consumption of resources in homes during extended lockdowns, leading to environmental impacts and resource strain.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_13077.md)
* [Lack of measures to limit virus propagation in household wastewater](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_13096.md)
* [Lack of spatial organization in the home may lead to increased risk of disease transmission between different activities and areas within the home.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_13136.md)
* [Limited space and lack of dedicated entry zones for managing outdoor to indoor transitions may lead to an increased risk of bringing contaminants into the home.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20355.md)
* [Residential cooking as a substantial source of indoor air pollutants leading to respiratory problems](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20366.md)
* [Social distancing within the house](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20367.md)
* [Use of temperature data to analyze hvac use may reveal patterns of occupant behavior and routines.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20368.md)
