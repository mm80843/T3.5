# __Machine learning engineers__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Benchmarking tests and comparisons conducted on a single hardware and software platform](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_3531.md)
* [Dependence on convolutional neural network cnn architecture](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6920.md)
* [False negative predictions due to iou threshold](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14421.md)
* [Frequent cases of occlusions, overlaps, and crowded zones in the dataset](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14423.md)
* [Inefficient spatial pooling operations may impact the accuracy and efficiency of the model](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14426.md)
* [Manual selection of physiological characteristics for anomaly detection model development may lead to biased or incomplete feature sets, impacting the effectiveness of the models.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15742.md)
* [Noisy precision / recall curve may make it difficult to estimate model performance for object detection.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15743.md)
* [Overfitting may occur due to the use of specific techniques such as dropblock and class label smoothing.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15744.md)
* [Overfitting of the model due to prolonged training](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15747.md)
* [Risk of overfitting in the neural network](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15759.md)
* [Training models using python libraries](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15763.md)
* [Unequal condition for speed and generalisability testing on the otc dataset](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15764.md)
* [Use of multiple detection models may lead to varied performance results and difficulty in comparing model effectiveness.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_16062.md)
* [Use of selective search and region proposal for two-stage detectors may introduce potential biases in object detection](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_16074.md)
* [Using stochastic gradient descent sgd with warm restarts and a cosine annealing learning rate schedule may lead to challenges in hyperparameter tuning and model convergence, as well as the need for careful adjustment of learning rate values.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_16075.md)
