# __Sewage system operators__

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Endogenous biomarkers present in sewage systems can be used to estimate the real-time population served by the system, posing a risk of privacy invasion.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_5216.md)
* [Proteomic identification of disease biomarkers in sewage systems may lead to the unintentional tracking and monitoring of health conditions of different populations, potentially violating privacy and ethical standards.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_5217.md)
* [The identification and monitoring of disease biomarkers in sewage systems could lead to the unintentional disclosure of health information of entire populations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_5218.md)
