# __Property management companies__

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [High maintenance costs of copper materials.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_3792.md)
* [Inadequate ventilation and infiltration levels in residences, leading to the potential spread of contagious diseases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_9341.md)
* [Lack of effective health and safety management in residential apartment buildings](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_13088.md)
* [Lack of service or facility to assist victims of domestic violence in existing residential buildings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_13089.md)
* [Limited capacity to protect occupants from virus transmission through surfaces and by air in existing residential buildings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14859.md)
* [Limited control of tenants over indoor environmental quality may lead to suboptimal conditions for mitigating contagious diseases propagation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_16811.md)
* [Reliance on outdated prevention and control methods](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_16812.md)
* [Reluctance of building operators to relax control may hinder the implementation of measures to mitigate contagious diseases propagation in the built environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_16813.md)
* [Tardy adoption of new technologies and slow uptake in the buildings sector may result in inadequate measures to address contagious diseases propagation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_17637.md)
* [Technology-facilitated real-time residential management could lead to increased exposure to contagious diseases if not implemented with appropriate health and safety protocols.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_20551.md)
