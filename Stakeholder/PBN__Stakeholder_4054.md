# __Building scientists__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Collaboration needed between building scientists and health professionals](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4773.md)
* [Lack of common standards for building design to support health](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4778.md)
* [Lack of interdisciplinary approach to understanding health implications of buildings](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4911.md)
* [Limited generalizability and longitudinal studies in existing research on occupant health in buildings](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4912.md)
* [Limited research on ergonomics settings in buildings and its musculoskeletal consequences on occupants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4913.md)
* [Limited understanding of the link between building and occupant parameters and their impacts on physical, mental, and social health](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4924.md)
* [Siloed expertise in building science community and lack of integrated understanding of lighting, air, noise, and temperature impacts on occupants](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4925.md)
