# __Health care facilities__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Close physical interaction in service industries increases the risk of contagious diseases propagation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_11155.md)

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Inadequate protective equipment for health care workers can increase the risk of infection while interacting with covid-19 patients.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10426.md)
* [Manufacturing technology adaptation for producing medical equipment may pose contamination risks due to the introduction of new materials and production processes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_18121.md)
* [Pathogen-laden droplets expelled during medical procedures may travel further and reach deeper into the respiratory tract of susceptible persons](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_18122.md)
* [Reduced capacity of health care workers to manage increased workload and handle medical supplies, leading to potential errors and contamination risks.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_19036.md)
* [Transmission of infectious agents not solely correlated with the size of the microbes in droplet nuclei or larger droplets](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_19037.md)
