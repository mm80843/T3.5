# __Ontologists__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Dependency on existing ontologies and integration of terms from multiple sources may introduce inconsistencies and errors in the cido ontology.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_5405.md)
* [Development of cido-specific terms for covid-19 and other coronavirus diseases may lead to discrepancies in terminology and classification with existing ontologies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_5416.md)
* [Integration of ontologies and query interfaces could lead to unintended exposure of disease-related data](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_5417.md)
* [Synchronous development of ontologies with the appearance of new diseases may lead to gaps in coverage and understanding of specific disease aspects.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_9444.md)
* [Use of owl 2, a dl-based formalism and semantic web standard for knowledge representation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_16122.md)
