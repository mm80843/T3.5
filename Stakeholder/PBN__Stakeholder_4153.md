# __Apartment management__

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Balconies as areas for observation and leisure activities can lead to close proximity and interaction between individuals, increasing the risk of contagious disease transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4937.md)
* [Balconies as places for communication and social activities may facilitate the spread of contagious diseases between neighbors and residents.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4938.md)
* [Significant relationship between balcony size and changes made during the pandemic process suggests that smaller balconies may face challenges in implementing proper disease prevention measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4939.md)
* [Use of balconies for daily activities like drying laundry and storing items can potentially spread contaminants or pathogens in the air or on surfaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_4967.md)
