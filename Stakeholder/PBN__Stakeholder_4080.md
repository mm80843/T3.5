## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Assumption of independent random times for the incubation period and time between onset of symptoms and death](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6468.md)
* [Dependency on ai/ml/dl for critical healthcare decisions leading to potential errors or biases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_8767.md)
* [Inaccurate model predictions leading to ineffective interventions](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_8773.md)
* [Low levels of convergent validity in the data, indicating potential issues with the measurement model.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_10282.md)
* [Potential issues with discriminant validity, leading to inaccurate assessment of how different constructs in the model relate to each other.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_13792.md)
* [Potential threat of common method bias in the data analysis process.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14724.md)
* [Reliability and validity of publicly available data from countries with poor data infrastructure](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14891.md)
* [Reliance on machine learning and ai models for disease prediction may lead to inaccuracies and false predictions](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_19787.md)

* [Use of genetic algorithm to estimate parameters for dynamic model of covid-19 spread.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_19789.md)
