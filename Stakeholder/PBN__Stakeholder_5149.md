# __Users of wearables__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Lack of energy-efficient wearable technology leading to rapid battery drainage and reduced usability.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6060.md)
* [Misinterpretation of wearables data could lead to an overreliance on the technology for decision-making, neglecting other important public health measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6061.md)
* [Reliance on wearables for early detection of infection may lead to privacy concerns, as biometric data and gps information are utilized for tracking and alerting individuals.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_6062.md)
* [Use of wearables to detect covid-19 symptoms may lead to false positives or false negatives, resulting in incorrect diagnoses.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_12545.md)
