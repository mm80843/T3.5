# __Model developers__

## Risk affecting this stakeholder

* [Availability of source code and package may lead to unauthorized modifications or misuse of the simulation model.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_5960.md)
* [Complexity of the model and economic interactions can lead to improper consideration of disease transmission and risk management.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_7322.md)
* [Developing an agent-based model abm to simulate the dynamics of the covid-19 epidemic](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14323.md)
* [Errors in the overall error function calculation could lead to misleading assessment of model performance and predictions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14324.md)
* [Hard-coded social constructs and periodic routines may not accurately represent real-world behavior, leading to unrealistic simulation outcomes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14327.md)
* [Inconsistencies in defining and mapping variables within the numerical grid](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14352.md)
* [Influence of computational models on decision-making, leading to oversight of important real-world aspects](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14354.md)
* [Multinomial logistic regression model developed due to violation of proportional odds assumption in ordinal regression model.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_14420.md)
* [Overreliance on single-model performance without considering the context and limitations of other detection models](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_15421.md)
* [Reliance on a specific programming language and package for the agent-based model may limit accessibility and collaboration, leading to a lack of diverse perspectives and expertise in the development and validation process.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_16203.md)
* [Semi-arbitrary choice of 2 days for length of time in the protected class](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_17774.md)
* [Unclear prior for dynamics of b may lead to inaccuracies in model predictions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_18857.md)
