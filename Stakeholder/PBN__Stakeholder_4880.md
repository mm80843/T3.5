# __Engineering and design teams__

## Risks that can be mitigated by this stakeholder

* [Displacement ventilation performs worse when patients are lying sideways, leading to the persistence of exhalation jets over a long distance.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_5727.md)
* [Downward ventilation may not always protect people from cross-infection due to mixing ventilation and free convection around the human body.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_5728.md)
* [Increasing the throw of the supply jet from the floor diffuser can deteriorate inhaled air quality and promote resuspension of particles including those carrying respiratory pathogens.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Risk/PBN__Risk_5729.md)
