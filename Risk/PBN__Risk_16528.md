# __Inadequate thermal comfort in living spaces__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Peters_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_612.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper insulation, air sealing, and efficient heating, ventilation, and air conditioning hvac systems to maintain thermal comfort in living spaces. retrofit underperforming housing to address thermal discomfort and improve indoor environmental quality.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18473.md)

## Name of the risk

Poor thermal comfort conditions, including overcooling in the summer and overheating in the winter, can lead to discomfort, reduced productivity, and increased health issues among residents, especially vulnerable populations.
