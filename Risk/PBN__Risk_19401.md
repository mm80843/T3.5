# __Embedding contextual information in 3d building models may lead to inaccurate simulation of occupant behaviors and interactions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lee_designing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_672.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough validation and verification of simulated behaviors with real-world data, involve experts in human behavior and building design in the simulation process.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21379.md)

## Name of the risk

Misleading design analysis and planning, potential for gaps between design and use phases.
