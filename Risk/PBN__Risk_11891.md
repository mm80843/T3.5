# __Non-contact interaction activities may have varying degrees of impact on different types of service facilities within the community, leading to potential transmission of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_urban_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_517.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct targeted research on different types of facilities to understand the impact of non-contact interaction and optimize the configuration of community service facilities to minimize disease transmission risk.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13804.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission within the community due to the varying effects of non-contact interaction activities on different types of facilities.
