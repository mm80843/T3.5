# __Lack of understanding of health and living conditions in informal settlements may hinder the development of relevant intervention strategies.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Salama_coronavirus_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_580.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct comprehensive assessments of health and living conditions in informal settlements, involve community members in the planning process, and prioritize the development of infrastructure and healthcare services in these areas.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17004.md)

## Name of the risk

Without understanding the health and living conditions in informal settlements, architects and planners may struggle to implement effective intervention strategies, leading to continued health risks for the community.
