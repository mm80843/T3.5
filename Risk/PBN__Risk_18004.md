# __The optimization of occupant distribution patterns to minimize covid-19 infections could lead to unintended consequences such as reduced indoor air quality or increased transmission risk in certain areas of the building.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mokhtari_effect_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_643.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough indoor air quality assessments and ventilation system evaluations to ensure that changes in occupant distribution do not compromise indoor air quality. implement measures to ensure adequate ventilation and air purification.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19966.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of COVID-19 transmission and compromised indoor air quality, leading to higher infection rates and potential health issues for occupants.
