# __Participants being asked to rate retrospective health measures before and after covid-19 measures may introduce recall bias and subjective interpretation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Corley_home_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_445.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide clear and specific instructions for rating retrospective health measures and utilize additional objective measures where possible to validate self-reported data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9289.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate assessment of the change in perceived health following the pandemic, affecting the reliability of the study findings.
