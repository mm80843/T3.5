# __System failure when there is a problem in the network, leading to the inability to send information about violators via sms.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rahman_automated_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_600.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement redundancy in communication networks, utilize alternative communication channels , internet-based messaging , and develop offline storage and forwarding mechanisms for delayed transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18007.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to alert authorities and individuals about non-compliance with mask-wearing regulations, resulting in unaddressed violations and increased risk of contagious disease transmission.
