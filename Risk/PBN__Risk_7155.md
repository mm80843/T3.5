# __Inadequate protection of eyes from exposure to sars-cov-2 due to lack of safety goggles and visors__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cirrincione_covid-19_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_441.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure availability of certified protective goggles and visors, promote their use in close contact with infected individuals, and emphasize the importance of decontamination after use](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9036.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of SARS-CoV-2 transmission through the eyes, leading to potential infection
