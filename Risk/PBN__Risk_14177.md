# __Pure gps-based contact tracing solutions lack the accuracy to identify close contacts within a few meters.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Siriwardhana_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_563.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

Reduced effectiveness in identifying close contacts in the case of contagious disease transmission, leading to potential spread of the disease.
