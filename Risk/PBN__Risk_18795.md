# __Inadequate pressure regime in ventilation systems may lead to contaminated air escaping from isolation rooms.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Marinelli_emergency_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_658.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement a pressure regime to push air from clean areas to dirty areas and out of the building, create negative pressure rooms.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20766.md)

## Name of the risk

Contaminated air escaping from isolation rooms, increasing the risk of spreading contagious diseases to other areas of the building.
