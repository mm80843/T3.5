# __Technology-facilitated real-time residential management could lead to increased exposure to contagious diseases if not implemented with appropriate health and safety protocols.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kaklauskas_covid-19_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_693.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing health and safety protocols for technology use, providing virtual alternatives for in-person services, and promoting touchless technologies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22531.md)

## Name of the risk

Unregulated use of technology for residential management could contribute to the spread of contagious diseases through shared facilities and common areas.
