# __Lack of seamless integration of data from different sources may result in inaccurate or unreliable information being shared with travelers.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Umair_impact_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_531.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilize advanced data integration techniques, iot data validation processes, and real-time data quality monitoring to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the information shared with travelers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14448.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate information could lead to ineffective decision-making by travelers and increase the risk of exposure to contagious diseases during travel.
