# __Informal signs with general information initially provided to consumers may not effectively communicate specific social distancing rules__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop clear and specific social distancing guidelines for different settings and ensure they are effectively communicated to customers through appropriate signage and messaging](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20403.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of customers not understanding or following social distancing guidelines, leading to potential virus spread
