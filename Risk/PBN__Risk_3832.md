# __Use of wearable urban health sensors without specific virus outbreak tracking capabilities may result in delayed detection of early symptoms and contribute to the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Allam_coronavirus_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_388.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhance wearable sensor capabilities to include early symptom detection algorithms, integrate data with public health surveillance systems, and provide real-time alerts for potential outbreaks.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5705.md)

## Name of the risk

Delayed detection of symptoms and lack of targeted monitoring may facilitate the transmission of infectious diseases within urban populations.
