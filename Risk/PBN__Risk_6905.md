# __Minimal involvement from users and healthcare professionals in wearable technology may lead to reliance on technology and potential negligence of other diagnostic and preventive measures.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheong_wearable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_435.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Comprehensive education on the role of wearable technology as a complement to traditional methods, as well as continued emphasis on the importance of holistic approach to healthcare.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8781.md)

## Name of the risk

Overreliance on technology may result in overlooking traditional diagnostic methods and preventive measures, leading to undetected cases and increased transmission of contagious diseases.
