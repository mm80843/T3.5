# __Retrofitting existing crowd models may lead to errors in occupant exposure assessments during pandemics.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ronchi_exposed_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_587.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Thorough testing and validation of the retrofitted model, along with sensitivity analysis to understand potential errors and biases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17397.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate assessments may result in a higher risk of disease transmission within confined spaces such as buildings and transportation terminals.
