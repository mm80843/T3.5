# __Accumulation of extraneous nucleic acids during the concentration process of wastewater samples.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Westhaus_detection_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_514.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of filter units with appropriate cut-off sizes, regular quality control checks for concentration process.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13700.md)

## Name of the risk

Interference with PCR leading to false-positive results.
