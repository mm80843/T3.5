# __Association between residential density and covid-19 fatality rates__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Frank_treating_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_471.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement measures to address obesity in high-density residential areas to counteract the indirect effects on covid-19 fatality rates.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10748.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher residential density is associated with a decrease in COVID-19 fatality rates, but also with an increase in obesity, which in turn is linked to higher COVID-19 fatality rates.
