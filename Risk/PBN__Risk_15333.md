# __Inadequate ventilation systems in refurbishment alternatives leading to poor indoor air quality and high indoor temperatures.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saari_effect_2006](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_584.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure ventilation systems are properly designed and installed to provide adequate air exchange and temperature control.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17273.md)

## Name of the risk

Decrease in worker performance and increase in short-term sick leaves due to poor indoor air quality and high indoor temperatures.
