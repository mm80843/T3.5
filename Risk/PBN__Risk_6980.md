# __Involving a large number of individuals in the decision-making process may increase the risk of exposure to contagious diseases, especially during in-person meetings or consultations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheshmehzangi_revisiting_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_437.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilize virtual meetings and consultations to reduce the need for in-person gatherings, implement strict hygiene protocols for in-person meetings, and prioritize individual health screenings for participants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8856.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of contagious diseases among the participants, leading to illness and potential transmission to others.
