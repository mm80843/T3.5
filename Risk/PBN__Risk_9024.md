# __Uncertainty and variations in energy consumption may not be considered in the eui estimation model, leading to discrepancies between estimated and actual values.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Geraldi_addressing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_475.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Include a margin of error in the eui estimation to account for uncertainty and variations. use more comprehensive energy modeling techniques that consider real-world variability.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10921.md)

## Name of the risk

Misleading energy performance evaluations and inability to accurately pinpoint energy-related issues in buildings.
