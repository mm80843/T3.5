# __Perception of short-term closure led to lack of alternative methods of contact provided by small businesses.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure that businesses have alternative methods of contact email, phone number, social media, website readily available, regardless of the perceived duration of closure.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20410.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability for customers to access information or support from closed businesses, leading to dissatisfaction and potential loss of business.
