# __Physical interaction with ar installations and shared smartphone usage can increase the risk of contagious disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene protocols for shared devices, limit physical interaction with ar installations, and encourage the use of personal devices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13207.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of spread of contagious diseases such as COVID-19 among customers and staff.
