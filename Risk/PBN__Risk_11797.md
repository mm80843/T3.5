# __Testing sample materials during a pandemic carries the risk of incomplete or inaccurate detection of contagious viruses such as sars-cov-2.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Westhaus_detection_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_514.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of multiple, validated testing methods and comprehensive sample collection strategies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13709.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to detect hotspots and limit virus transmission, leading to increased public health risks.
