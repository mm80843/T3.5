# __Non-adjustment for vaccinations administered abroad in register-based vaccination rates__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Oecd_what_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_626.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Developing strategies to account for vaccinations administered abroad, improving data collection methods, and cross-referencing vaccination records](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19142.md)

## Name of the risk

Overstatement of differences in vaccination rates between native-born and foreign-born populations, leading to inaccurate data interpretation and policy decisions
