# __Transfer of the agent-based model to other contexts without corresponding data may lead to inaccurate predictions and recommendations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Silva_covid-abs_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_567.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validation of the model with local data before applying it to different contexts, and collaboration with local experts and stakeholders for context-specific parameterization.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16269.md)
