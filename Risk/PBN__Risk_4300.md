# __Air-handling systems may require more regular filter-maintenance protocols.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement regular maintenance schedules for air filters and invest in advanced filter-monitoring technology.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6171.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased air quality and increased risk of virus transmission if filters are not properly maintained.
