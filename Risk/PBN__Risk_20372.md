# __Gathering data from multiple housing units in different states increases the risk of cross-contamination of infectious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kawka_impacts_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_689.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene and disinfection protocols during data collection. use remote data collection methods where possible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22352.md)

## Name of the risk

Cross-contamination can lead to the spread of contagious diseases across different areas, increasing the risk of outbreaks.
