# __The adjustment coefficient c_ur for uv-c dose when the virus is suspended in air may be in a range 0.167-0.545.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Beggs_upper-room_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_413.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct extensive research to determine the accurate adjustment coefficient for different virus suspension scenarios.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7329.md)

## Name of the risk

Uncertainty in the adjustment coefficient may lead to improper UV-C dosing and ineffective virus disinfection.
