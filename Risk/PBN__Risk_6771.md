# __Use of respiratory rate monitoring for disease evaluation__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Education on correct usage and interpretation of respiratory rate readings, development of reliable sensor technology.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8645.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate measurement or misinterpretation of respiratory rate could lead to missed or delayed diagnosis of respiratory diseases, including contagious ones.
