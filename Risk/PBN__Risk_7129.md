# __Investigation of new drugs for covid-19 therapy, such as tocilizumab and ace2-targeted treatments, may have unknown side effects and long-term impacts.__

* [Cirrincione_covid-19_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_441.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
