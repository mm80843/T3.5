# __Undervaluation of greenspace as a resource for health may lead to underinvestment and underutilization of natural environments for promoting public health.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
