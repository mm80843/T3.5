# __Inefficiency and delays in logistics could lead to hoarding of supplies, social contact, and increased risk of exposure to contagious diseases during supply chain operations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Umair_impact_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_531.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Prioritize efficient and transparent supply chain operations, implement sustainable tracking tags and ultra-low-power tracing solutions, and adopt blockchain for trustworthy preservation of product histories.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14449.md)

## Name of the risk

Hoarding of supplies, social contact, and delays in logistics may contribute to shortages of essential pandemic supplies and increase the risk of transmission of contagious diseases in supply chain operations.
