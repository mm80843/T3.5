# __Potential sensitivity of bacteria in deionized water to uvgi compared to bacteria in droplet nuclei__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Memarzadeh_applications_2010](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_645.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough testing to understand the effectiveness of uvgi on bacteria in various conditions, and adjust installation and maintenance processes accordingly](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20031.md)

## Name of the risk

Inadequate UVGI sensitivity could lead to ineffective disinfection in environments with infectious droplet nuclei
