# __Lack of validation for human labeling__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_construction_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_521.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement validation processes for human labeling, cross-validation by multiple human labelers, and quality control measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14013.md)

## Name of the risk

Unvalidated human labeling may introduce errors in the dataset and affect the accuracy of the integrated knowledge graph.
