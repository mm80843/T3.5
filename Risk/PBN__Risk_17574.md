# __Lack of regular cleaning and maintenance of hvac systems can lead to the buildup of dirt, mold, and other contaminants, increasing the risk of spreading contagious diseases through the air.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Nembhard_ventilation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_634.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establish and adhere to a strict schedule for the regular cleaning and maintenance of hvac systems, including filter changes and thorough cleaning of components.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19533.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne transmission of contagious diseases within the building.
