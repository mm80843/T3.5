# __Use of humanoid service robots in retail environments may lead to increased physical interactions and close contact, contributing to the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Limit physical contact with robots, sanitize surfaces regularly, and provide clear instructions for safe interaction.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13226.md)

## Name of the risk

Elevated risk of disease transmission through close interactions with humanoid robots.
