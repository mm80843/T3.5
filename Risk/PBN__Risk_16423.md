# __Reliability of virtual model__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pilati_digital_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_609.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular validation and calibration of the virtual model with real-world data, and continuous improvement based on feedback and performance metrics.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18365.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate virtual models may result in suboptimal clinic configurations and inefficient resource allocation.
