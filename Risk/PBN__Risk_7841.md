# __Potential for spread of sars-cov-2 in buildings due to viral particles associated with larger particles that can potentially penetrate high efficiency filters.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dietz_2019_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_454.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhanced building hvac operational practices, proper filter installation, and maintenance.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9724.md)
