# __The emerging growth of mobile commerce and e-commerce may lead to an increase in physical interaction and foot traffic in retail spaces, leading to a higher risk of contagious disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tricarico_proximity_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_538.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing strict sanitation and hygiene protocols, managing crowd control, and promoting contactless transactions to reduce physical interaction.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14877.md)

## Name of the risk

If not mitigated, this could result in the spread of contagious diseases among shoppers and retail staff.
