# __Lack of differentiation in job retention schemes by nationality or country of birth, potentially leading to concentrated impact on immigrant workers in specific sectors.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Oecd_what_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_626.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement targeted support for sectors with high immigrant workforce representation, incorporate diversity awareness in scheme implementation, and provide cultural competence training for scheme administrators.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19127.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential clustering of immigrant workers in sectors with limited job retention support, increasing the risk of transmission and impacting the health of immigrant communities.
