# __Excessive use of it systems in housing units due to smart working, online lessons, and entertainment activities increases the risk of indoor contagious disease propagation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dalessandro_covid-19_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_448.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote ventilation and air circulation within the living spaces, encourage the use of outdoor spaces when possible, and practice good hygiene and sanitation measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9526.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased probability of contagious disease spread due to prolonged indoor activities and close contact among family members.
