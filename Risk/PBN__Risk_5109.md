# __Resuspension of particle matter from walking events in indoor spaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Azuma_environmental_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_406.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of walk-off mats, regular cleaning of carpets, and implementation of dust control measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6981.md)

## Name of the risk

Elevated presence of resuspended particles, including potential virus-laden particles, in the indoor environment.
