# __Inadequate response to the comfort category in some standards, particularly leed and well.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tleuken_readiness_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_542.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhance the inclusion of comfort and well-being indicators in the standards, focusing on noise management, urban farming, and local services.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15086.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced focus on providing comfort and well-being for building occupants, potentially impacting mental and physical health during a pandemic.
