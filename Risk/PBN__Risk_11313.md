# __Virtual reality and holographic technology may create an illusion of physical presence, leading to reduced adherence to physical distancing measures.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide clear guidelines for interaction, limit the duration of virtual interactions, and use visual cues to maintain physical distance.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13225.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of close proximity interactions and reduced awareness of personal space.
