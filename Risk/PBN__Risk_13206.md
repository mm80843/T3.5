# __Contamination from outdoor environment to indoors may result in higher levels of pollutants indoors, impacting the overall indoor air quality.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tleuken_readiness_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_542.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of walk-off mats, shoe storage, and regular cleaning to reduce the entry of outdoor contaminants into indoor spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15126.md)
