# __Neglecting the sink effect term in the mass balance equation may result in underestimation of contaminant levels, leading to inadequate mitigation measures.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_characterization_2011](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_522.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Include the sink effect term in the mass balance equation and implement continuous monitoring of contaminant levels.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14084.md)

## Name of the risk

Underestimation of contaminant levels can result in insufficient ventilation and filtration measures, increasing health risks for occupants.
