# __Introduction of gene drive mosquitoes could lead to unintended consequences in the ecosystem, such as the decline of other species or the emergence of new diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wilson_importance_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_510.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Thorough ecological impact assessments and strict regulatory frameworks should be established before releasing gene drive mosquitoes into the environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12798.md)

## Name of the risk

Uncontrolled spread of gene drive mosquitoes could disrupt the natural balance of the ecosystem and increase the risk of new disease outbreaks.
