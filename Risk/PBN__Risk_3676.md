# __Handling sars-cov-2 in wastewater samples increases the risk of exposure to the virus.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ahmed_first_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_383.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Strict adherence to safety protocols, use of appropriate personal protective equipment ppe , and conducting sample handling in a controlled environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5549.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential transmission of SARS-CoV-2 to individuals handling the samples and potential spread of the virus.
