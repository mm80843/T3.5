# __Potential risk of cross-contamination from stainless steel surfaces due to inadequate cleaning procedures and disinfectant application.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kampf_persistence_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_692.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement thorough cleaning and disinfection protocols for stainless steel surfaces, and ensure appropriate concentrations and contact times for disinfectants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22445.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased transmission of contagious diseases such as HCoV, TGEV, and MHV.
