# __Insufficient hvac standards for diluting indoor air to prevent airborne pathogen transmission in high-occupancy facilities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhang_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_500.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improvement of hvac standards, implementation of effective iaq control measures, and increased ventilation rates.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12371.md)

Increased risk of airborne pathogen transmission and potential disease outbreaks in high-occupancy facilities.
