# __Inadequate incorporation of nature-based elements in architecture may result in a lack of human-nature connection and reduced well-being for building occupants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhong_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_498.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Collaboration with environmental psychologists and biophilic design experts to ensure meaningful integration of natural elements; post-occupancy evaluations to assess the impact on occupants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12263.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased psychological and physiological health, impaired cognitive functionality and performance, diminished sense of well-being.
