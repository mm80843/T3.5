# __Misuse or improper maintenance of uvgi technology__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Proper training of maintenance staff, regular inspection and maintenance schedules, and clear guidelines for the use of uvgi technology.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10267.md)

## Name of the risk

Ineffective disinfection of air due to UVGI technology not functioning as intended, leading to the survival and spread of microorganisms.
