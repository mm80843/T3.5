# __Temperature and humidity control__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_review_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_478.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of hvac systems to control temperature and humidity within recommended ranges, regular monitoring of temperature and humidity levels.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11047.md)

## Name of the risk

Inappropriate temperature and humidity levels can impact the defensive efficacy of the oral mucous membrane, increasing the risk of virus transmission.
