# __Failure to observe social distancing guidelines in communal spaces, leading to potential transmission of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_residential_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_557.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enforce social distancing measures in communal spaces, such as hosting social occasions with adherence to recommended inter-personal distances. promote awareness of the importance of social distancing in minimizing the spread of contagious diseases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15764.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of transmission of contagious diseases, such as COVID-19, within communal spaces due to close contact and lack of social distancing measures.
