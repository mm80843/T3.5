# __Limited ability to collect geolocation data for studying disease transmission in the built environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kumar_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_680.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Development of privacy-respecting geolocation data collection methods, incentivizing citizen participation in data collection efforts, improving data sharing protocols between governments and research institutions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21903.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to accurately assess the impact of geographic factors on disease spread and implement targeted interventions in specific locations.
