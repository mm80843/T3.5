# __Ineffective communication leading to lack of updates and information for furloughed employees.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pamidimukkala_impacts_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_618.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Maintain regular communication with furloughed employees, provide updates on job status, and offer support and encouragement.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18873.md)
