# __Potential spread of contagious diseases through physical contact during the survey data collection process.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Santini_mental_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_577.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene protocols, provide protective equipment, and minimize physical contact during data collection.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16866.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of transmission of infectious diseases to the survey respondents and survey administrators.
