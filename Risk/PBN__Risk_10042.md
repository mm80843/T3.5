# __Environmental noise pollution impacting health and well-being__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibn-mohammed_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_494.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing noise reduction measures such as sound barriers, road surfacing, and traffic management; creating quiet zones in urban areas; improving building insulation; using low-noise technology for vehicles and transportation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11949.md)

## Name of the risk

Distorted sleep patterns, annoyance, negative impacts on metabolic and cardiovascular system, cognitive impairment in children, 48000 new cases of ischaemic heart disease per year, ~12000 premature deaths, chronic high annoyance, extreme high sleep disturbance, ~12500 schoolchildren suffering from reading impairment
