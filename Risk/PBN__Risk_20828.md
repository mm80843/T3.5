# __Potential for strain on healthcare infrastructure due to an increase in contagious diseases resulting from construction activities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Jallow_impact_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_697.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase healthcare capacity and resources, train healthcare workers for surge response, establish temporary medical facilities near construction zones, and prioritize healthcare services for affected areas.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22812.md)

## Name of the risk

Overwhelmed healthcare systems, shortage of medical resources, and increased pressure on healthcare workers. Risk of healthcare facilities becoming hotspots for disease transmission.
