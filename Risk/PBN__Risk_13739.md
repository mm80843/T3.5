# __Failure to conduct a self-health evaluation and implement preventive measures for workers in shared vehicles increases the risk of covid-19 transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sunandar_preventing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_554.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement self-health evaluation protocols, provide partitions/barriers, enforce mask-wearing, and disinfection after each use of the vehicle.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15664.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of COVID-19 transmission among workers in shared vehicles, leading to potential outbreaks within the workforce.
