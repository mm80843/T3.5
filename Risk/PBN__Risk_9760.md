# __Transmission of misinformation or incomplete information about workplace management practices and covid-19 response due to lack of validation and confirmation at the end of interviews.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hou_study_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_489.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing a validation process at the end of each interview to confirm accuracy and completeness of information, cross-referencing interview data with other sources for accuracy.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11666.md)

## Name of the risk

Dissemination of inaccurate information leading to ineffective workplace management strategies and increased risk of COVID-19 transmission in workplaces.
