# __Transmission of contagious diseases through contact with documents and articles.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Agade_exploring_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_379.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular sanitization of documents and articles, promotion of digital content over physical documents.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5403.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of diseases if infected documents are handled without proper precautions.
