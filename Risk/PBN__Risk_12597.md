# __Iot systems used to detect health symptoms may lead to false positives or false negatives, impacting the accuracy of health analyses and potentially causing unnecessary panic or neglect.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Umair_impact_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_531.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Cross-validation with traditional diagnostic methods, transparent reporting of accuracy rates, and user education on the limitations and potential errors of iot health systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14516.md)

## Name of the risk

Confusion, panic, unnecessary medical interventions, or neglect of actual health issues.
