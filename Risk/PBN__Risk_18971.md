# __Reliance on natural ventilation may lead to inadequate air exchange, allowing viruses and particles to accumulate indoors.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Maharani_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_663.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance of natural ventilation openings, use of air purifiers, and installation of monitoring systems for air quality.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20943.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of contagious disease transmission and poor indoor air quality.
