# __Exclusion of nonpeer-reviewed articles and studies in other languages with no english translation might lead to language and publication bias.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mbunge_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_653.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilize translation technologies and collaborate with international research networks to access non-english literature. consider the inclusion of preprint articles and non-peer-reviewed studies with rigorous review processes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20480.md)

## Name of the risk

Limited access to valuable insights and research findings from non-English sources, potentially missing crucial information on disease control and management.
