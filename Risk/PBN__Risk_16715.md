# __Inability to simultaneously heat and cool rooms may lead to inconsistent temperature control, creating discomfort for occupants and potentially impacting their immune response, thereby increasing the risk of contagious disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Invest in advanced hvac zoning systems and building automation controls to enable simultaneous heating and cooling based on occupancy and thermal load.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18662.md)

## Name of the risk

Inconsistent temperature control may compromise occupants' comfort and immunity, contributing to the spread of contagious diseases.
