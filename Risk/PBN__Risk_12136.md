# __Reduced outdoor air requirement from 560 m3 to 119 m3 per hour may impact indoor air quality and increase risk of indoor air pollution.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_characterization_2011](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_522.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular monitoring of indoor air quality, use of alternative air purification methods, and periodic ventilation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14051.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of indoor air pollution-related health issues such as respiratory problems and allergies.
