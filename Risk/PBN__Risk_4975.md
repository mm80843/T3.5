# __Lack of sufficient space on balconies may lead to a higher concentration of people in indoor communal areas, increasing the risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aydin_questioning_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_403.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Creation of designated balcony usage schedules, promotion of staggered balcony usage, and provision of alternative outdoor communal spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6848.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased likelihood of disease transmission in indoor communal areas due to overcrowding on balconies.
