# __In-person meetings and training pose a risk of contagious disease transmission due to close contact and shared spaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Limit meetings and conduct them virtually when possible. implement social distancing requirements and sanitization protocols for in-person meetings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13532.md)
