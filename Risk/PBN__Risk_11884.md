# __Impact on community planning model due to non-contact interaction activities under the influence of covid-19.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_urban_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_517.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Include pedestrian scale factors and spatial scale changes in community planning, utilize non-contact interaction technologies, and allocate community service facilities and public space more flexibly.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13797.md)

## Name of the risk

Reconfigured organization mechanisms of community space, affecting accessibility of facilities and service radius.
