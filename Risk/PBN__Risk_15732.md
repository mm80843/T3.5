# __Reliance on technology for identifying social distancing violations may lead to errors in detection and interpretation of human behaviors.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rezaei_deepsocial_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_593.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Combine technology-based detection with human validation, conduct regular calibration and validation of tracking systems, provide training on interpreting results.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17673.md)

## Name of the risk

Misidentification of social distancing violations, resulting in ineffective allocation of resources and misinformed policy decisions.
