# __Rapid deployment of tele-treatment technology may lead to lack of standardization and oversight, resulting in inadequately trained personnel and potential errors in treatment procedures.__

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
