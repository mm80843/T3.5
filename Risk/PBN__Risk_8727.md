# __Reliance on data from countries with more advanced epidemics and earlier interventions, such as italy and spain.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Flaxman_report_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_470.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporating data from countries with a range of epidemic stages and intervention timings to capture a broader picture of intervention impact.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10620.md)

## Name of the risk

Biased results that do not account for variations in epidemic trajectories and intervention effectiveness across different countries.
