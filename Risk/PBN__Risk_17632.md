# __Inconsistent implementation and maintenance of vertical greening systems may result in reduced air purification efficiency and potential structural damage to buildings.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Navaratnam_designing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_635.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop and implement guidelines for consistent installation and maintenance of vertical greening systems. conduct regular inspections of system integrity and plant health.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19590.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased air purification effectiveness and potential structural damage to buildings due to improper installation or maintenance.
