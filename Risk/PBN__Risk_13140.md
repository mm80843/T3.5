# __Monitoring air quality parameters at infrequent intervals__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tokazhanov_assessment_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_541.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Frequent real-time monitoring of air quality parameters and swift response to any deviations from standard values](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15060.md)
