# __Lack of awareness or consideration for contagious disease transmission during audio recording__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rogers_city_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_589.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Mandate adherence to public health guidelines during recording, provide disposable microphone covers, encourage individual recording in controlled environments](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17526.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of contagious diseases due to contributors being in close proximity or sharing recording equipment
