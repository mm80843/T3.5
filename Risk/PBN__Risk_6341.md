## Articles mentionning the risk

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Redesign crisis management protocols to treat disease outbreaks as critical emergencies from the onset, improve communication and coordination among healthcare institutions, and implement early warning systems](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8213.md)
