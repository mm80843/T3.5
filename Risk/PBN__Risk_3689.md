# __Initial evidence indicating that individuals can maintain and promote mental health through exercise during the pandemic may lead to over-reliance on exercise as a solution to mental health challenges__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ai_mental_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_384.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote a holistic approach to mental health that includes various interventions beyond exercise, such as therapy, counseling, and social support](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5563.md)

## Name of the risk

Neglect of other mental health interventions and overemphasis on exercise as the sole solution to mental health challenges
