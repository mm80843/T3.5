# __Wastewater-based epidemiology wbe studies that do not adjust monitoring results to temperature risk significant under- or over-estimation of the true temporal changes occurring in virus shedding within the community.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hart_computational_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_483.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Adjust monitoring results to account for the effects of temperature, particularly in long-term wbe studies across changing seasons and climate regions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11312.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate estimation of virus shedding trends and potential misinterpretation of public health data.
