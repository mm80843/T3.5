# __Transmission rate estimates may lead to over-estimation or under-estimation of the actual transmission rate, impacting the effectiveness of control measures.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Collin_using_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_443.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validate transmission rate estimates with multiple sources of data and incorporate uncertainty in the estimation process.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9210.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate transmission rate estimates can result in ineffective control and prevention of contagious diseases.
