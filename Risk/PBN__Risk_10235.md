# __Inadequate spatial configuration increases the risk of disease transmission during intensive exercise sessions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Install protective screens for treadmills and bikes, rearrange equipment to reduce crowding, and increase ventilation during peak training hours.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12143.md)

## Name of the risk
