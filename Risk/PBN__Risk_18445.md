# __Informal signs printed on paper with regular tape may contribute to the spread of contagious diseases if not properly handled or disposed.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of digital signs or professionally printed signs with easy-to-clean surfaces and touchless interaction.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20412.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission within the built environment.
