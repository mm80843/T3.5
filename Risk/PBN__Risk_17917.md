# __International collaboration in research may facilitate knowledge exchange, but it also carries the risk of introducing and spreading contagious diseases across borders.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Montoya-torres_mapping_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_642.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement rigorous health and safety protocols for international collaboration, promote virtual collaboration where possible, and conduct risk assessments of collaborative activities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19878.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to manage international collaboration effectively could lead to the global propagation of contagious diseases and increased mortality rates.
