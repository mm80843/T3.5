# __Inadequate quarantine facilities within homes for infected individuals, leading to increased risk of transmission within the household.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elrayies_prophylactic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_462.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Design and creation of dedicated quarantine rooms and private bathrooms, with separate ventilation and access to minimize exposure to other household members.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10001.md)
