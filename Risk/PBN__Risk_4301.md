# __Increased airflow rates that primarily improve recirculated indoor air delivery may raise transmission possibilities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Optimize airflow rates to prioritize fresh air intake and reduce recirculation, upgrade ventilation systems to improve air exchange rates.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6172.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher likelihood of virus transmission due to inefficient ventilation.
