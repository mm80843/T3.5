# __Covering the face with masks or face shields negatively impacts the ability of hard of hearing hoh individuals to listen and lip-read, affecting approximately 11 million people in the uk.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Adapting guidance to accommodate hoh individuals through the use of clear face masks or novel technologies such as captioning apps.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8009.md)

## Name of the risk

Social and communication challenges for HoH individuals, leading to potential isolation and difficulties in accessing essential services.
