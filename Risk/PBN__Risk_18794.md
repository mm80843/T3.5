# __Insufficient ventilation in makeshift hospitals may increase infection risk of opportunistic airborne transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Marinelli_emergency_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_658.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase ventilation rates to the maximum supported by the system, use air purifiers to reduce virus-laden aerosols.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20765.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher likelihood of opportunistic airborne transmission of contagious diseases, leading to increased infections among patients and medical staff.
