# __Exponential increase in the use of telemedicine technology and chatbots__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Madurai_elavarasan_restructured_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_664.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Training medical professionals on effective use of telemedicine technology and chatbots, and emphasizing the importance of in-person assessments for certain health conditions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21006.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased reliance on technology may lead to decreased in-person medical assessments and potential misdiagnosis of contagious diseases.
