# __The use of new customer-adapted delivery and collection services may involve direct interaction between delivery personnel and consumers, increasing the risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tricarico_proximity_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_538.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enforcing health and safety guidelines for delivery personnel, promoting contactless delivery options, and providing protective equipment for delivery personnel.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14878.md)

## Name of the risk

Without mitigation, this could lead to the spread of contagious diseases through delivery services.
