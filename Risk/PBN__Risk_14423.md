# __Use of multiple detection models may lead to varied performance results and difficulty in comparing model effectiveness.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Shorfuzzaman_towards_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_569.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Perform thorough comparative analysis and consider using ensemble methods to combine predictions from multiple models for improved accuracy.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16355.md)

## Name of the risk

Inconsistent assessment of object detection models, making it challenging to determine the best-performing model for a specific task.
