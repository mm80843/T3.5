# __Misinterpretation of wearables data could lead to an overreliance on the technology for decision-making, neglecting other important public health measures.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burki_wearable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_425.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Education on the limitations of wearables, integration of wearables data with traditional public health strategies, and clear communication of the role of wearables in conjunction with other measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7934.md)

## Name of the risk

Undermining public health efforts, overlooking manual contact tracing, and ignoring broader population health interventions.
