# __Lack of partition walls between parking bays allows for easy adaptation of large car parking areas into smaller educational spaces, potentially leading to overcrowding and congestion.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement clear zoning and partitioning strategies to create distinct spaces within the adapted areas, reducing congestion and improving air circulation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16003.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of congestion and overcrowding in adapted smaller educational spaces, leading to reduced air circulation and increased potential for contagion transmission.
