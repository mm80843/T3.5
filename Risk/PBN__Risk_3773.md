# __Frequent use of electric appliances leads to increased internal heat gain, potentially contributing to the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Al-mumin_occupants_2003](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_386.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encourage the use of energy-efficient appliances or implement cooling systems to offset the heat generated.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5646.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher internal heat gain can create a more favorable environment for the growth and spread of pathogens.
