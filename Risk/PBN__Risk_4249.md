# __Inadequate ventilation in buildings, especially hospitals, leading to increased risk of airborne infection__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure suitable ventilation rates, provide natural ventilation, use carbon dioxide tracer-gas techniques for analysis](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6120.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of nosocomial and other communicable infections, potential for cross-infection in public spaces and hospitals
