# __Increase in off-site construction may lead to a higher risk of contagious diseases transmission due to transportation of materials and workers from various locations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheshmehzangi_revisiting_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_437.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict health and safety protocols for workers, conduct regular testing, and enhance sanitation measures in construction sites and off-site facilities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8864.md)

## Name of the risk
