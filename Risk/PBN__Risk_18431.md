# __Lack of standardized signage in small businesses may result in inconsistent communication of covid-19 information to the public.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide support and resources to small businesses for creating and displaying standardized covid-19 signage. offer training on effective communication of health and safety information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20398.md)
