# __Modeling the economy within the agent-based model may introduce biases or oversimplifications that impact the accuracy of economic impact estimations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Silva_covid-abs_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_567.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Collaboration with economists and financial experts, validation of economic model against real-world economic data, sensitivity analysis of the economic model.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16281.md)
