# __Associations between greenspace and communicable diseases not considered__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct research to explore the associations between greenspace and communicable diseases, and integrate this information into public health and urban planning strategies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14787.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to explore the potential impact of greenspace on the propagation of communicable diseases, leading to a limited understanding of disease transmission and prevention in built environments.
