# __Lack of proper consideration for air circulation and ventilation in adapted parking spaces may lead to increased risk of contagion transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement ventilation assessments and design interventions to ensure adequate airflow and air exchange in the adapted spaces, considering the potential for contagion transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16005.md)

## Name of the risk

Inadequate air circulation and ventilation can lead to increased risk of contagion transmission within the adapted parking spaces, jeopardizing the health and safety of occupants.
