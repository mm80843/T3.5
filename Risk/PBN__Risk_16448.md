# __Employee preference for lower-levelled office buildings with sprawling staircases over tall and crowded buildings due to fear of virus infection.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Phapant_covid-19_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_610.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Redesign tall buildings to incorporate infection control features, improve elevator and stairwell systems, and communicate the implementation of safety measures to alleviate employee concerns.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18391.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased demand for tall and crowded office buildings, leading to potential financial losses for building owners and developers.
