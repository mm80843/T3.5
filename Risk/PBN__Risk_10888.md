# __Release of wolbachia-carrying mosquitoes could unintentionally introduce new pathogens or disrupt the balance of existing microbial communities in the environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wilson_importance_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_510.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Comprehensive risk assessments, monitoring of microbial communities, and strict containment measures should be in place before widespread release of wolbachia-carrying mosquitoes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12799.md)

## Name of the risk

Uncontrolled spread of Wolbachia-carrying mosquitoes may lead to the spread of new diseases or ecosystem imbalances.
