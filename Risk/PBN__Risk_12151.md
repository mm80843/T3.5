# __Dependence on specific pollutants for outdoor air ventilation rate determination__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_characterization_2011](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_522.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Diversify outdoor air ventilation rate determination methods, consider a wide range of indoor pollutants in ventilation rate calculations](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14066.md)

## Name of the risk

Inefficient ventilation strategies, potential neglect of other harmful pollutants, limited flexibility in ventilation system design
