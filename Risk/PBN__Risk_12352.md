# __Mismanagement of crisis communication on social media can lead to ineffective dissemination of accurate information and exacerbate public fear and confusion.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Vela_rol_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_526.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Training for social media moderators, establishment of crisis communication protocols, and regular updates from trusted health authorities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14267.md)

## Name of the risk

Ineffective communication leading to misunderstanding of health guidelines, failure to adopt safety measures, and increased anxiety among the public.
