# __Inadequate mental health support for healthcare providers during a contagious disease outbreak__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pfefferbaum_mental_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_611.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide mental health care, monitor stress reactions, offer professional mental health intervention, alter assignments and schedules, and create mechanisms to offer psychosocial support](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18455.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased stress, burnout, and decreased ability to perform professional responsibilities effectively, leading to compromised patient care
