# __Use of sensor nodes to measure gaseous pollutants may lead to potential contamination if not properly maintained.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saini_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_583.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance and calibration of sensor units, and verification of data accuracy through cross-validation with standard monitoring methods.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17221.md)
