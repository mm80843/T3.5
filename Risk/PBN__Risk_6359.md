# __High level of occupancy in extracurricular activities may lead to increased risk of contagious disease transmission among children.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cantarero_covid19-routes_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_428.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene protocols, social distancing measures, and regular disinfection of facilities. encourage vaccination of children and staff.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8231.md)

## Name of the risk
