# __Inadequate temperature control and fluctuations can weaken the immune systems of building occupants, increasing their susceptibility to contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Awada_occupant_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_401.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance of hvac systems, provision of individual temperature controls, and thermal comfort optimization.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6621.md)
