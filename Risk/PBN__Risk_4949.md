# __Lack of privacy on the balcony may lead to behaviors that increase the risk of disease transmission, such as talking loudly and playing loud music.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aydin_questioning_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_403.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement privacy screens or partitions to create private spaces on the balcony, and promote awareness of respectful behavior among users.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6822.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher likelihood of close interactions and increased exposure to potentially contagious activities.
