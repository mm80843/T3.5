# __Long-term detraining due to forced stop of physical activity__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Maugeri_impact_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_654.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encouraging safe physical activity at home, providing remote training programs, and promoting gradual return to exercise](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20529.md)

## Name of the risk

Marked decline in maximal oxygen consumption (VO2max), reduced endurance capacity, loss of muscle strength and mass, tendon ruptures, and reduced joint tribology

## People affected by this risk

* [General population](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_9.md)
* [Particularly those who had to stop regular physical activity due to quarantine measures](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_14526.md)
