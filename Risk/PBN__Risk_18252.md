# __Inadequate ventilation leading to poor indoor air quality and increased risk of airborne disease transmission__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Megahed_indoor_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_648.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of effective ventilation systems to dilute and remove airborne contaminants, regular maintenance of ventilation systems, and awareness of the importance of ventilation in disease control](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20220.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased spread of airborne diseases, potential for respiratory disorders, and discomfort for building occupants
