# __35 cohort studies and 69 cross-sectional studies, including 18 large scale ecological studies, may increase the risk of disease transmission due to the close contact and interactions among participants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene and sanitation measures during the conduct of the studies, including regular disinfection of study environments and proper ventilation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14820.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease spread in the population and potential outbreak of contagious diseases.
