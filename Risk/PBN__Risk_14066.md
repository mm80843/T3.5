# __Inadequate hvac services leading to different comfort zones and potential spread of contagious diseases in the built environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper hvac services are in place to maintain consistent and appropriate indoor air quality and temperature. implement air purification systems to remove pathogens from the air.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15994.md)
