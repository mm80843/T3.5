# __Relaxed budget policies may lead to a lack of financial resources for long-term public health infrastructure and preparedness, creating vulnerability to future disease outbreaks.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Allocate specific funds and resources for public health infrastructure and preparedness, prioritize long-term health investments, and ensure robust disease surveillance and response systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19461.md)
