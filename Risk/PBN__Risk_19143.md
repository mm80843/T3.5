# __Increased connectivity of urban green areas may lead to higher tick populations, increasing the risk of tick-borne infections in humans.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lohmus_making_2015](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_665.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing preventive measures such as awareness campaigns, guidance on avoiding tick attacks, and proper tick removal methods. additionally, targeted solutions to decrease the number of infected small mammals in the environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21115.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of human infections with tick-borne pathogens, potentially leading to illnesses such as Lyme disease.
