# __Subjective evaluations of indoor environments by occupants may not align with objective measures, leading to discrepancies in assessing indoor environmental quality.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Parkinson_continuous_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_615.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement a comprehensive approach that combines subjective evaluations with objective, instrumental measures to obtain a holistic understanding of indoor environmental quality.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18761.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate assessment of indoor environmental quality can lead to suboptimal building design and maintenance, potentially affecting occupant health and performance.
