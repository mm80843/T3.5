# __Potential for increased transmission of contagious diseases due to elevated airflow rates and recirculated indoor air delivery.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Balancing airflow rates with ventilation needs, implementing air purification technologies, and considering spatial configurations to minimize transmission possibilities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6168.md)

## Name of the risk

Heightened risk of disease transmission within the built environment, leading to potential outbreaks and public health concerns.
