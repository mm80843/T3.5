# __Lack of comprehensive force majeure clauses in construction contracts may lead to uncertainty in dealing with the impacts of the covid-19 pandemic.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Assaad_guidelines_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_399.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Review and update force majeure clauses in contracts to account for pandemics and other unforeseen events, seeking legal counsel if necessary.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6491.md)

## Name of the risk

Legal disputes, delayed projects, and financial losses due to ambiguous contract terms related to force majeure events like the COVID-19 pandemic.
