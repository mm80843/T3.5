# __Measurement error in the moisture content readings from the sensors__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_characterization_2011](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_522.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Calibrate and validate the accuracy of the sensors regularly, and cross-check readings with alternative moisture measurement methods](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14076.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate moisture content measurements could lead to incorrect conclusions about the filter bed performance
