# __Startups relying heavily on relational capabilities may lead to increased risk of disease propagation through close contact and interaction with partners and the startup community.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kuckertz_startups_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_682.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict health and safety protocols, encourage remote work where possible, and limit in-person interaction to essential activities. adhere to government health guidelines and restrictions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22049.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease spread within the startup community and partner networks, potential for outbreaks and increased transmission of contagious diseases.
