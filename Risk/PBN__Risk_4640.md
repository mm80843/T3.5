# __Failure to consider the concerns of workers related to pay, leave, safety, and health, leading to lower compliance with health and safety guidelines and increased risk of contagion.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Assaad_guidelines_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_399.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Take into account the concerns of workers by maintaining flexible policies related to pay, leave, safety, and health, and addressing their concerns through open communication and support.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6514.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced adherence to health and safety measures, leading to higher risk of contagious diseases spreading among construction workers and the wider community.
