# __Real-time ieq data assessment through the mobile interface creates an environment for potential virus exposure among the occupants if proper sanitization measures are not in place.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Geng_intelligent_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_474.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Frequent sanitization of mobile devices, promotion of individual device use, and regular cleaning of interface access points.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10830.md)
