# __Potential risk of false readings from the infrared thermometers leading to incorrect assessment of body temperatures.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Chamola_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_430.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and testing of the infrared thermometers to ensure accuracy. implementation of additional screening measures for individuals showing symptoms.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8600.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate assessment of body temperatures can result in infected individuals going undetected and spreading the disease.
