# __Failure to close recirculation dampers can lead to the distribution of airborne contaminants from one point to others connected to the same system.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dehghani_hierarchy_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_452.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict closure of recirculation dampers and use high efficiency filters in recirculation units.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9674.md)
