# __Conducting in-person surveys or interviews for data collection may pose a risk of exposure to contagious diseases for both data collectors and participants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Van_rooij_compliance_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_527.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilizing online surveys, virtual interviews, and contactless data collection methods to minimize physical interactions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14302.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential transmission of contagious diseases between data collectors and participants during in-person interactions.
