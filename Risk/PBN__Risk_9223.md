# __Exposure of preliminary research results on ipv and dv to the media prior to peer review.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Gurney_dangerous_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_479.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enforce ethical guidelines for research dissemination and establish strict embargoes on releasing research findings to the media.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11123.md)

## Name of the risk

Misrepresentation of research findings, premature public panic, and erosion of trust in academic research.
