# __Hospitals designed without containment spaces for walk-in patients and visitors, without internal containment options between wards, and without containment options in emergency departments__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_designing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_558.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate containment spaces for walk-in patients, consider spatial layout of wards, operating theatres, and emergency departments, and implement compartmentalized ventilation management with well-thought-out pressure differentials](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15799.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of rapid spread of contagious diseases within the hospital environment, leading to increased infection rates and overwhelmed healthcare facilities
