# __Shift in urban commute patterns away from public transit towards other modes of transportation , biking, walking, private vehicles .__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Yang_urban_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_504.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Investment in infrastructure for biking and walking, promotion of staggered work hours, incentivizing carpooling, and creating dedicated lanes for alternative commute modes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12620.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased congestion and crowding in alternative commute modes, leading to potential transmission of diseases. Decreased revenue and ridership for public transport systems, affecting their financial viability.
