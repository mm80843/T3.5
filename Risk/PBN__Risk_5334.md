# __Lack of community awareness about the relationships between vector-borne diseases vbds and social, environmental, and climatic determinants may hinder the effectiveness of community-based interventions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bardosh_addressing_2017](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_409.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement participatory awareness programs that engage local communities in understanding the connections between vbds and their social, environmental, and climatic determinants. promote community-based education and capacity-building initiatives.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7207.md)

## Name of the risk

Limited effectiveness of community-based interventions, increased vulnerability to vector-borne diseases, and challenges in controlling disease transmission within communities.
