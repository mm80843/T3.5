# __Involvement of non-essential guests during competitions or training sessions increases the risk__

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Limit or restrict non-essential guests, and enforce strict visitor protocols for entry and interaction](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12092.md)
