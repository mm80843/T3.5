## Description of the risk

Waste materials originating from households and quarantine facilities with COVID-19 patients may contain viable SARS-CoV-2, posing a risk of infection

* [Yes](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/ISO_Scale/PBN__ISO_Scale_1.md)
