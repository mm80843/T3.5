# __Potential spread of contagious diseases through the interaction with home energy management systems in a pandemic.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Chen_coronavirus_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_434.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene protocols for installation and maintenance of hems, promote contactless solutions and remote monitoring, and educate users on proper health and safety measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8725.md)
