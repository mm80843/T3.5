# __Unclear causation between poor quality housing and health outcomes creates uncertainty about the impact of living conditions on contagious disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Gurney_dangerous_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_479.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conducting longitudinal studies to determine the causal relationship between housing conditions and health outcomes, and implementing targeted interventions to improve housing quality in at-risk areas.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11084.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of overlooking potential health hazards and disease transmission within poor quality housing, leading to unaddressed public health risks.
