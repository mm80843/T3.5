# __Susceptibility of indoor air to environmental tobacco smoke may lead to exposure of occupants to harmful chemicals and increased health risks.__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict no-smoking policies and use air filtration systems to minimize tobacco smoke in indoor environments.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15125.md)
