# __Failure to reduce computational burden of sensing nodes may lead to shorter battery life of the monitoring system.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saini_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_583.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Optimize computational processes to reduce the burden on sensing nodes and extend battery life.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17217.md)
