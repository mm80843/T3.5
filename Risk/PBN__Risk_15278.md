# __Low-cost iaq monitor may have limited accuracy and reliability, leading to incorrect measurements of pollutants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saini_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_583.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and validation of the iaq monitor against scientific-grade instruments.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17218.md)

## Name of the risk

Incorrect measurements can result in a false sense of security and failure to address harmful pollutant levels.
