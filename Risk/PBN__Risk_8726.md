# __Assuming that each intervention has the same effect on the reproduction number across countries and over time.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Flaxman_report_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_470.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conducting separate analyses for each country to account for variations in the impact of interventions on transmission rates.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10619.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate estimation of the impact of interventions on transmission rates, leading to ineffective disease control measures.
