# __Contamination fear leading to lack of tactile input when shopping for products, both online and in-store.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Providing alternative ways for consumers to gather tactile input, such as virtual reality simulations or detailed product descriptions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13237.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased ability for consumers to make informed purchase decisions, lower satisfaction with products.

* [Consumers](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_146.md)
