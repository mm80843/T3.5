# __Reliability and quality of remote treatment may be compromised due to network connectivity issues, leading to ineffective or delayed treatment for infectors.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Redundancy in network connectivity, remote equipment maintenance, and training for medical staff to handle connectivity issues.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5958.md)
