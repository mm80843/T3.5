# __In-person interviews may increase the risk of covid-19 transmission if proper safety measures are not followed.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hou_study_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_489.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conducting interviews online to eliminate the risk of in-person transmission, implementing strict safety measures if in-person interviews are necessary.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11664.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of COVID-19 among interviewees and interviewers, leading to illness and possible long-term health effects.
