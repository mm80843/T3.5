# __Potential for inaccurate similarity computation leading to incorrect matches or nonmatches__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_construction_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_521.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular validation and verification of similarity computation results, employing multiple similarity measures, and setting threshold ranges for inference rules.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14011.md)

## Name of the risk

Incorrect matches or nonmatches could lead to incorrect data integration and analysis, impacting decision-making.
