# __Increased reliance on technology for essential social interactions may lead to decreased in-person social connections, which can impact mental health and well-being.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Umair_impact_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_531.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promotion of virtual social events, online support groups, and mental health resources, as well as encouragement of safe in-person gatherings where possible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14542.md)

## Name of the risk

Isolation, loneliness, and mental health issues due to lack of in-person social interactions.
