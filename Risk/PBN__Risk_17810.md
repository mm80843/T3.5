# __Testing the application with a small number of respondents may not represent the diversity of user experiences and needs.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mufid_risk_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_640.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct user testing with a diverse group of respondents to capture a wide range of user experiences and feedback.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19770.md)

## Name of the risk

The application may be optimized for a narrow range of users, leading to poor usability for others.
