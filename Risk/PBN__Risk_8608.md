# __Inclusion of measures that assess qualitative aspects of community composition is important to distinguish between urban exploiters, alien and invasive species, and native and sensitive species in green spaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Felappi_green_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_467.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of measures such as the urbanity index to differentiate habitat requirements for different species and the design of heterogeneous networks of green spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10501.md)

## Name of the risk

Unbalanced representation of species richness and abundance due to the dominance of urban exploiters and invasive species.
