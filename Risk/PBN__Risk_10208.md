# __Exposed and painted black air ducts may contribute to the spread of airborne contaminants and pathogens within the hall.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Install air purifiers and uv air disinfection systems to reduce airborne pathogens, and regularly clean and maintain the air ducts.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12116.md)

## Name of the risk

Heightened risk of airborne disease transmission among individuals in the hall.
