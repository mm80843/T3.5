# __Lack of filters in diffusers increases the risk of spreading contaminants through the supplied air.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Install filters in diffusers and conduct regular cleaning and maintenance of the air supply system.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12140.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher chances of indoor contamination and transmission of contagious diseases.
