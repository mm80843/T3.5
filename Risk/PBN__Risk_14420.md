# __Overreliance on single-model performance without considering the context and limitations of other detection models__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Shorfuzzaman_towards_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_569.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Comparative evaluation of multiple detection models, considering the unique strengths and weaknesses of each model, and incorporating ensemble techniques if necessary](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16351.md)

## Name of the risk

Neglecting potential advantages of other models and missing out on more accurate detection or monitoring possibilities
