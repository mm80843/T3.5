# __Promoting distance learning and telecommuting through synchronous communication and video conferences may lead to the transmission of contagious diseases if preventive measures are not implemented.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enforcing strict guidelines for virtual interactions, including adherence to health protocols, vaccination requirements, and regular health screenings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6014.md)
