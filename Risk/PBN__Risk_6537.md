# __Susceptibility of sars-cov-2 to heat and ultraviolet uv light may affect virus transmission under certain conditions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Chamola_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_430.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Education on comprehensive prevention measures, emphasizing the importance of multiple interventions in controlling virus transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8411.md)

## Name of the risk

Misinterpretation of heat and UV light as sufficient measures to control virus transmission, leading to complacency in implementing other vital control measures.
