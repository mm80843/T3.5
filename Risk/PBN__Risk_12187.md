# __Insufficient data on the effectiveness of botanical air filtration under realistic ventilation conditions__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_characterization_2011](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_522.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct further research and experimentation under realistic ventilation conditions to understand the true removal mechanisms and effectiveness of botanical air filtration](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14102.md)
