# __Neglecting to incorporate contextual information in the building model for human behaviour simulation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lee_designing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_672.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure that contextual information is embedded in the building model, such as environmental factors, social dynamics, and situational characteristics.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21433.md)
