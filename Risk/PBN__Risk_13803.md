# __Reliance on iot devices for early diagnosis may lead to inaccurate results and mismanagement of covid-19 cases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Subramanian_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_556.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and maintenance of iot devices, validation of results against traditional diagnostic methods, and providing training to healthcare professionals for interpreting iot device data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15728.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate diagnosis can lead to delayed or improper treatment, and the spread of the disease may not be effectively controlled.
