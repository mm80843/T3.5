# __Negative impact of covid-19 on physical and mental health of residents due to lack of access to urban green spaces__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sainz-santamaria_governance_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_582.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhance accessibility to urban green spaces, promote activities that can be performed in home environments, provide mental health support services](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17178.md)
