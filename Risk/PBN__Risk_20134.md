# __Reliance on twitter data may compromise privacy and consent of individuals sharing their thoughts on loneliness during the pandemic.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Koh_how_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_684.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper anonymization of social media data and obtain explicit consent for using public posts in research studies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22113.md)

## Name of the risk

Violation of privacy rights and potential harm to individuals who may not consent to their data being used for research purposes.
