# __Inaccurate parametrization of the relationship between relative humidity rh and droplet size could lead to misinterpretation of aerosol behavior and transmission dynamics.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Marr_mechanistic_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_656.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validate parametrization with experimental data, consider real-world aerosol behavior, and use multiple sources of data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20681.md)

## Name of the risk

Misinterpretation of aerosol behavior could lead to ineffective control measures and increased risk of disease transmission.
