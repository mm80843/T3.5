# __Inefficient thermal performance of building envelope__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhance insulation, improve window glazing, and implement energy-efficient hvac systems to maintain thermal comfort and reduce energy demand.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16014.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher energy consumption, indoor temperature fluctuations, and discomfort, leading to potential health issues and reduced resilience to temperature-related illnesses.
