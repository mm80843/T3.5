# __Use of multiple informal signs and different approaches by retailers can lead to inconsistency and potential confusion among customers.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Standardize signage and messaging, remove redundant signs, and use consistent communication approaches.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20401.md)

## Name of the risk

Customers may have difficulty understanding retailers' status and services, which could lead to decreased trust and satisfaction.
