# __Changes in control of distribution pumps and heat generators for energy efficiency may affect the thermal comfort of building occupants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Balancing energy efficiency with thermal comfort through advanced control systems and feedback from building occupants. regular temperature monitoring and adjustments.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18605.md)

Uncomfortable indoor temperatures can lead to stress, discomfort, and potential health issues for occupants.
