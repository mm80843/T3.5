# __The prolonged exposure to uvgi at dosages ranging from 1000 to 3000 mj/cm 2 may pose health risks to individuals if not used properly.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict safety guidelines and protocols for using uvgi, including proper shielding, control of exposure durations, and training for personnel.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10271.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential harm to human health, such as skin and eye damage, if exposed to high levels of UVGI.
