# __Generation and exposure of viral aerosols in an aerosol irradiation chamber.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Buonanno_far-uvc_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_424.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of sealed and controlled aerosol chambers, strict operational protocols, and regular monitoring of chamber conditions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7913.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of aerosolized virus particles escaping the chamber and posing a threat to individuals in the surrounding area.

## People affected by this risk

* [Operators of the aerosol chamber and individuals in the vicinity of the chamber](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_5133.md)

## Stakeholders who can mitigate this risk

* [Research facility management and biosafety officers](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_5134.md)
