# __High rate of virus positivity on surfaces frequently touched by medical staff or patients, such as computer mice, trash cans, handrails, and doorknobs.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_aerosol_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_477.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular disinfection of frequently touched surfaces, implementation of hand hygiene practices, and proper waste management protocols.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10996.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission through contact with contaminated surfaces.
