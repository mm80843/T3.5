# __Inadequate ventilation and air circulation in biophilic design buildings leading to poor indoor air quality and increased risk of airborne contagions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhong_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_498.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Installation of advanced ventilation systems, regular air quality monitoring, and use of air purification technologies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12282.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of respiratory infections and compromised health of building occupants.
