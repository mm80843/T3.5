# __Exposure of frontline health care practitioners to contagious pathogens through direct patient contact.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Yang_combating_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_505.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict infection control measures, provide adequate personal protective equipment, and prioritize testing and vaccination of health care practitioners.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12638.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of infection for health care practitioners and potential spread of contagious diseases within healthcare facilities.
