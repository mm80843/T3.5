# __Data collection from multiple sources in urban areas increases the risk of contagion spread through contact with surfaces or individuals carrying the virus.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Allam_coronavirus_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_388.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict sanitation protocols for data collection equipment and personnel, enforce physical distancing measures, and utilize contactless data collection methods where possible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5703.md)

## Name of the risk

Unmitigated data collection can lead to the unintentional spread of contagious diseases, amplifying outbreaks.
