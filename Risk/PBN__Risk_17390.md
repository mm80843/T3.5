# __Spatial analyses based solely on vector data models may limit the understanding of contagious disease spread through other data types such as raster models or network data.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Noszczyk_impact_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_630.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
