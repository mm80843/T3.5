# __Limited representation of non-residential processes in disease transmission models may impact the accuracy of environmental health impact assessments.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Brizuela_understanding_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_421.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate interaction kernels and spatially-dependent transmission parameters to capture non-residential processes in disease transmission models, refine the representation of local and remote environmental conditions, and validate the model outputs with real-world data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7816.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate representation of environmental health impacts can lead to ineffective policy interventions and resource allocation for disease prevention.
