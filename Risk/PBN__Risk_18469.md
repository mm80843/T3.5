# __Shortage of technical virtual reality experts to develop virtual reality applications suitable for medical education and healthcare.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mbunge_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_653.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Investment in training programs for virtual reality experts, collaboration between educational institutions and healthcare organizations to develop tailored virtual reality applications.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20436.md)

## Name of the risk

Limitation in the development of virtual reality applications tailored to medical education and training needs, hindering effective integration of virtual reality in healthcare settings.
