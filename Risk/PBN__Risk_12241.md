# __Electrical breakdowns caused by high humidity and condensation affecting fluorescent lamps__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Vranay_adaptation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_524.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of protective tubes made of quartz glass, implementation of ip 55 or higher protection, regular inspection and maintenance](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14157.md)
