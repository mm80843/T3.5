# __Enveloped viruses like mhv coronavirus are susceptible to inactivation by mechanical stress.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Walker_effect_2007](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_523.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Handle aerosolization and sampling of viruses with care to avoid unnecessary mechanical stress.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14122.md)
