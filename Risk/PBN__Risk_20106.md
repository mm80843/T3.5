# __Exclusion of non-english tweets may lead to findings that are more representative of english-speaking populations only.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Koh_how_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_684.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Include language diversity in the analysis by employing language translation tools and considering data from non-english tweets.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22085.md)

## Name of the risk

Limited generalizability and potential bias toward English-speaking populations in the findings.
