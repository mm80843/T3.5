# __Potential ineffectiveness of upper-room guv in killing or inactivating airborne microorganisms due to inadequate uv fluence rate or non-uniform uvgi distribution__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Morawska_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_641.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure that upper-room guv is designed to provide an average uv fluence rate within the recommended range and installed to provide uniform uvgi distribution](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19795.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne microorganism transmission and infection in the built environment
