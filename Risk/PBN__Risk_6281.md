# __Selection of lower-cost sensors for humidity and air quality measurements may compromise data accuracy and reliability.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Calvo_scalable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_427.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
