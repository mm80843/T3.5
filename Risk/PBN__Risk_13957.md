# __Effect modification by the current working mode__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spano_association_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_559.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct additional research to assess the influence of working mode on the association between green features and psychological health outcomes. develop tailored public health interventions based on the findings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15885.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to consider the impact of working mode on the relationship between green features and psychological health outcomes may lead to misinterpretation of the findings and ineffective public health interventions.
