# __Limitation of not measuring viral aerosol particle size directly.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Walker_effect_2007](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_523.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilize additional methods to assess viral aerosol particle size, such as microscopy or particle sizing instruments.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14108.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate assessment of viral aerosol behavior and effectiveness of UV-C disinfection.
