# __Rapid shelter construction and emergency constructions may lead to overcrowding and inadequate ventilation, increasing the risk of airborne contagious diseases transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheshmehzangi_revisiting_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_437.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Design emergency shelters with adequate ventilation systems and proper spacing, implement occupancy limits, and conduct regular air quality monitoring. ensure proper sanitation and hygiene facilities in crowded construction areas.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8866.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for the rapid spread of airborne contagious diseases in emergency shelters and crowded construction areas, leading to a higher risk of infection among occupants and construction workers.
