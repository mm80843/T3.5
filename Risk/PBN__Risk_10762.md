# __Testing viral enrichment process in sewage samples can pose a risk of exposure to viral contaminants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wu_sars-cov-2_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_507.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
