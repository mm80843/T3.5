## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enforce regulations for outdoor public spaces, promote responsible use of outdoor areas, and encourage the use of personal outdoor spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22512.md)
