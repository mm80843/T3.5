# __Neglecting the impact of temperature on degradation of biomarkers in wastewater can lead to error propagation into estimates of upstream discharge, consumption, exposure, and excretion.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hart_computational_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_483.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
