# __Photographing signs related to covid-19 inside retail establishments may increase the risk of exposure to the virus for the person taking the photographs.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of personal protective equipment ppe such as masks, gloves, and maintaining a safe distance while taking photographs.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20416.md)
