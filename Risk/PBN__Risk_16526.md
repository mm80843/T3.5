# __Lack of natural ventilation in bedrooms__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Peters_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_612.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Install operable windows in bedrooms to promote natural ventilation and reduce the risk of airborne disease transmission. implement regular cleaning and maintenance of ventilation systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18471.md)

## Name of the risk

Poor ventilation can lead to the accumulation of airborne contaminants and the spread of contagious diseases, impacting resident health.
