# __Potential for airborne cross-infection due to low air velocity and disturbance of boundary layer around individuals__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bolashikov_methods_2009](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_417.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
