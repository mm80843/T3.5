# __Inaccurate control of airflow and airspeed can lead to the transport of virus-carrying particles within indoor spaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular distribution of temperatures, control of the rate of airflow and airspeed, and use of natural ventilation to reduce the risk of infectious aerosols.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10211.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission from one part of the room to another due to improper airflow and airspeed control.
