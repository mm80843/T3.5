# __Potential transmission of contagious diseases through shared article downloads and access to online library resources.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheshmehzangi_10_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_436.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encourage the use of individual devices, provide hand sanitizers, and regularly disinfect shared surfaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8841.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission among users who access shared library resources.
