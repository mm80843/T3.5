# __Lack of quantification of simulated output leading to inability to score design options based on contamination or infection rate.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lee_designing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_672.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
