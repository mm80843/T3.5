# __Use of the surfacide whole-room uv-c disinfection system during mers outbreaks may prevent nosocomial spread of the virus and protect staff.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bedell_efficacy_2016](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_412.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote the use of uv-c disinfection as a complementary measure alongside other infection control strategies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7307.md)

## Name of the risk

Dependence solely on UV-C disinfection without other preventive measures may lead to incomplete protection against nosocomial spread.
