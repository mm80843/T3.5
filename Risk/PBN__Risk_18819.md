# __Online surveys may create a methodological bias by excluding individuals with limited internet access and computer skills.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Marconi_green_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_659.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use a combination of online and in-person surveys to ensure a more representative sample of the population.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20790.md)

## Name of the risk

Underrepresentation of certain demographic groups and occupations in the survey data, leading to skewed and inaccurate results.

## People affected by this risk

* [Demographic groups with limited internet access and computer skills](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_14673.md)
* [Such as elderly or low-income individuals](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_14674.md)

## Stakeholders who can mitigate this risk

* [Research team or survey administrators](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_14675.md)

## Technologies linked to the risk

* [Survey platforms with offline data collection capabilities to reach individuals without internet access](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Technology/PBN__Technology_21126.md)
