# __Inability to locate people without face masks in densely populated areas due to the limitations of the system.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rahman_automated_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_600.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Deploy additional surveillance and monitoring systems, integrate with crowd management technologies, and implement a multi-tiered approach to identify and alert individuals without face masks.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18006.md)

## Name of the risk
