# __Synchronous development of ontologies with the appearance of new diseases may lead to gaps in coverage and understanding of specific disease aspects.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bayoudhi_overview_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_411.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular updates and revisions of ontologies to reflect new knowledge and understanding of diseases, as well as collaborative efforts to ensure comprehensive coverage.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7289.md)

## Name of the risk

Incomplete or inaccurate modeling of infectious diseases, hampering research and intervention efforts.
