# __Informal signs in retail stores may not effectively communicate social distancing and hygiene measures, leading to increased risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement clear and standardized signage that clearly communicates social distancing and hygiene measures, provide staff training on signage placement, and regularly update signage to reflect current guidelines.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20388.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease spread within retail environments, potentially leading to outbreaks and public health concerns.
