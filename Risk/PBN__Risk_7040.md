# __Dependence on open-source implementation for pgp encryption may introduce vulnerabilities and security risks.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Christodoulou_health_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_439.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular security updates and patches, independent security reviews of the encryption implementation, and adoption of additional encryption layers for enhanced security.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8918.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential exploitation of security vulnerabilities, exposure of medical data to unauthorized parties, and compromise of patient confidentiality.
