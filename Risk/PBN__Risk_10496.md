# __Ineffective placement of pacs leading to uneven air purification and particle removal in the built environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhang_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_500.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement a systematic approach to determine the optimal placement of pacs based on airflow dynamics and occupancy patterns. regularly assess and adjust the placement.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12407.md)

## Name of the risk

Uneven air quality and particle distribution, increasing the risk of contagious disease transmission.
