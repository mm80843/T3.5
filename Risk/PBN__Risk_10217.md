# __Removal of shared and reusable resources may reduce the risk of surface contamination, but if not replaced with adequate alternatives, it may lead to dissatisfaction among building occupants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide alternative resources or amenities that are single-use or easily sanitized. communicate the reasons for the changes to building occupants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12125.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced convenience for building occupants, potential dissatisfaction, and non-compliance with the measures.
