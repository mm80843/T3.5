# __Implicit lag in time affecting intervention impact on deaths__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Flaxman_report_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_470.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement real-time monitoring of intervention impact and consider adjusting for the time lag in data analysis.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10663.md)

## Name of the risk

Weak manifestation of recent interventions in current deaths, leading to delayed understanding of intervention impact.
