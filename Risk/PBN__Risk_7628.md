# __Transmission of contagious diseases through ventilation systems and air extractors.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dalessandro_covid-19_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_448.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and maintenance of air extractors, use of high-quality air filters, and implementation of window-based natural ventilation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9509.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of disease transmission, especially in confined spaces without adequate ventilation.
