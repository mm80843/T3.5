# __Potential ineffectiveness of guv ’in-duct’ application in disinfecting contaminated extracts or ventilation ducts__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Morawska_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_641.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Consider alternative disinfection methods or ensure that guv ’in-duct’ application is effectively designed and implemented](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19797.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of continued recirculation of ventilation flows contaminated with microorganisms, leading to the spread of airborne pathogens
