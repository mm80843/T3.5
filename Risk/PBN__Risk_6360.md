# __Fluctuations in occupancy levels in hospitals can impact the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cantarero_covid19-routes_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_428.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement continuous sanitation practices, promote awareness of hygiene among staff and visitors, and maintain a consistent level of vigilance regardless of occupancy.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8232.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher occupancy during medical consultation and visiting hours may increase the risk of disease transmission. Lower occupancy at weekends may lead to reduced vigilance and readiness for potential outbreaks.
