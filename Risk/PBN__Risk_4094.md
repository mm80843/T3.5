# __Reliance on ai algorithm for temperature measurement__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and validation of the ai algorithm, manual temperature checks as a backup.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5966.md)
