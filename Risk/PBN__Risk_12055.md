# __Potential risks of ozone disinfection in wastewater treatment, including high operation costs and hazardous byproducts with chemical reactions. ozone over-dose can cause bad smell and secondary pollution.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_disinfection_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_520.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular monitoring and control of ozone dosage, proper disposal of hazardous byproducts, and ensuring efficient ventilation to prevent secondary pollution.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13969.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased operation costs, hazardous byproducts affecting human health, and potential for secondary pollution.
