# __Dependence on wearables for health monitoring may lead to neglect of traditional healthcare practices and face-to-face medical consultations, resulting in delayed or inadequate medical care for non-covid-19 related conditions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Chamola_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_430.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Emphasize the complementary role of wearables alongside traditional healthcare, promote regular medical check-ups, and provide education on the limitations of wearable technology.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8507.md)

## Name of the risk

Prolonged illnesses, worsening of chronic conditions, and reduced access to essential medical interventions.
