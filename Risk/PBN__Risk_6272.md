# __Reliance on wifi connectivity for ieq measurements may lead to data transmission issues and interruptions in monitoring.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Calvo_scalable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_427.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement a redundant data transmission system using wired connections or alternative wireless technologies to ensure continuous data flow. regularly test and troubleshoot the wifi network for reliability.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8144.md)

## Name of the risk

Data transmission issues can result in gaps in IEQ monitoring data, leading to incomplete and unreliable analysis of indoor environmental quality.
