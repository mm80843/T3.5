# __Informal settlements and slums being underrepresented in discussions and media reports could lead to higher infection rates and transmission of diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Salama_coronavirus_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_580.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase awareness and representation of informal settlements and slums in discussions and media reports to ensure that interventions are targeted and resources are allocated for disease prevention and control.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17003.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher infection rates and transmission in informal settlements and slums can lead to a greater spread of contagious diseases, impacting the health and well-being of the communities.
