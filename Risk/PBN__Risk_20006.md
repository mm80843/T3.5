# __Measurement of capacity to comply with covid-19 measures involves subjective assessments that may not accurately reflect actual behavior and adherence.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kuiper_intelligent_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_681.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Supplement self-reported measures with objective indicators of compliance , mobility data, observational studies to validate the reported capacity to comply.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21985.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate assessments of capacity to comply could lead to misinformed decision-making and ineffective allocation of resources for public health interventions.
