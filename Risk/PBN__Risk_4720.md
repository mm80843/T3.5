# __Algorithmic decision-making process reliant on sensor data could be influenced by external factors such as travel, diet, and other health conditions, leading to inaccurate diagnostic outcomes.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ates_wearable_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_400.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and validation of the algorithms using diverse and representative datasets, as well as periodic review and adjustment of the decision-making process based on clinical outcomes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6593.md)

## Name of the risk

Unmitigated, this could result in misdiagnosis and inappropriate treatments for contagious diseases.
