# __Lack of channels for managers who do not participate in associations or events to access different strategies or experiences.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sainz-santamaria_governance_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_582.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop channels for non-participating managers to access information and resources, such as online platforms or local knowledge sharing events.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17163.md)

## Name of the risk

Managers may miss out on valuable knowledge and resources, leading to less effective park management.
