# __High level of under-ascertainment of infections due to focus on testing in hospital settings rather than in the community.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Flaxman_report_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_470.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase testing in the community and develop antibody tests for representative population surveys.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10641.md)

## Name of the risk

Underestimation of infections could lead to inadequate control measures and increased transmission of the virus.
