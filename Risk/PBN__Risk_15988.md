# __Risk of unauthorized data disclosure in discoverable mode of bluetooth devices.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Raza_mobile_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_596.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enable non-discoverable mode by default, limit the information disclosed during device discovery, and ensure user consent for data exchange.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17929.md)

## Name of the risk

Disclosure of personal information, including name and address, leading to privacy breaches and potential exploitation of sensitive data.
