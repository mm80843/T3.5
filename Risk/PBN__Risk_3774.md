# __Occupants habitually leaving lights on in unoccupied rooms may lead to poor ventilation and air quality, increasing the risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Al-mumin_occupants_2003](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_386.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Install occupancy sensors to automatically turn off lights in unoccupied rooms, and educate occupants on the importance of ventilation and air circulation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5647.md)

## Name of the risk

Poor ventilation increases the concentration of airborne pathogens, potentially leading to higher transmission rates of contagious diseases.
