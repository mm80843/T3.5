# __Deployment of 5g-based intelligent service robots may result in reduced human interaction, potentially impacting social connections and mental health of patients and visitors.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Introduce guidelines for balanced use of robots and human interaction, implement social support programs for patients, and provide training for staff on supporting patient well-being.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5975.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced human interaction, potential social isolation, and impact on mental health for patients and visitors.
