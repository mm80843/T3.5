# __Estimation of transmission rates using population-based kalman filters may result in inaccurate data if not properly validated__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Collin_using_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_443.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validate kalman filter data with real-world observations, combine data from multiple sources to cross-verify transmission rates, and implement transparency in the methodology used for estimation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9209.md)

## Name of the risk

Miscalculation of disease transmission trends, misinformed policy decisions, and delayed public health responses
