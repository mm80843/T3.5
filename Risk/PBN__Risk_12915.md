# __Publication bias or selective reporting in meta-analysis may lead to an inaccurate estimation of the association between greenspace and contagious diseases in the built environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct sensitivity analyses and explore potential sources of bias, such as unpublished studies or unpublished data, to assess the robustness of the findings. transparently report the methods used to assess publication bias and the impact on the meta-analysis results.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14834.md)

## Name of the risk

Misleading conclusions and recommendations may result from an inaccurate estimation of the association, leading to suboptimal strategies for mitigating contagious diseases in the built environment.
