# __Collaboration with outsiders may introduce new contagious diseases to the local community or staff members.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict health screenings for all outsiders before they interact with the local community or staff. provide health education and resources for preventing the spread of diseases. enforce quarantine protocols if necessary.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12906.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of contagious diseases within the community and among staff members, leading to illness and disruption of operations.
