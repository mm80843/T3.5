# __Increased reliance on iot for monitoring and tracking of infected individuals can lead to data accuracy and reliability issues.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Malliga_comprehensive_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_661.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and maintenance of iot devices, data validation checks, and redundant monitoring systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20880.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate tracking of high-risk patients, leading to potential spread of the disease and ineffective quarantine measures.
