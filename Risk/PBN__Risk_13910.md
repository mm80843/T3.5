# __Narrow supermarket aisles make it difficult for shoppers to maintain social distancing, increasing the risk of infection while passing in the aisles.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_designing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_558.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement one-way aisle systems, designate specific entry and exit points for aisles, and provide clear signage to guide shoppers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15837.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of infection due to close contact in narrow supermarket aisles.
