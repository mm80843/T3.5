# __Targeting aggressive attack measures to source communities and implementing sustainable measures in sink regions is essential for reducing malaria transmission in namibia.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tatem_integrating_2014](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_548.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement aggressive case management, vector control, and surveillance in source regions, and sustainable measures to reduce receptivity in sink regions. monitor and adjust interventions based on ongoing data analysis.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15434.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to target source and sink regions effectively may result in continued transmission and spread of malaria, undermining elimination efforts.
