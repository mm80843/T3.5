# __Inadequate ventilation and airflow control leading to poor air quality and increased risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance of ventilation systems, implementation of air purifiers, and installation of hepa filters.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18672.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne contagious diseases spreading within the building.
