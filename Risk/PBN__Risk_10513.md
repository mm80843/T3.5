# __No rules for periodically monitoring indoor air quality in a facility.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhang_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_500.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop and implement guidelines for regular indoor air quality monitoring and establish thresholds for airborne virus concentration.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12424.md)

## Name of the risk

Lack of monitoring indoor air quality may lead to undetected increases in airborne virus concentration, posing a risk to building occupants.
