# __Potential exposure to infected surfaces due to high-traffic areas and shared spaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tokazhanov_assessment_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_541.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of touchless technologies and self-cleaning spaces to reduce contact with potentially infected surfaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15077.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of virus transmission and infection for individuals who come into contact with infected surfaces.
