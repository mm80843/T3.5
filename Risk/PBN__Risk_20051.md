# __Extended data collection process may expose interviewees and interviewers to multiple sources of contagion__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kuckertz_startups_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_682.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Minimize the duration of interviews, rotate interview locations, and conduct health screenings for participants](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22030.md)

## Name of the risk
