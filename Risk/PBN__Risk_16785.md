# __Samba firmware warming up period of 10 minutes may lead to delays in accessing sensor data, impacting real-time monitoring and response__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Parkinson_continuous_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_615.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Optimize firmware to reduce warm-up period without compromising sensor stability, or use additional sensors to provide initial measurements during warm-up](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18732.md)

## Name of the risk

Delayed access to sensor data may result in inaccurate or outdated measurements, leading to ineffective disease propagation monitoring
