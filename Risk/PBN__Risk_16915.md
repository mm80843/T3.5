# __Exposure to contagious diseases through literature search and review of covid-19 related materials.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Panneer_health_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_617.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure strict adherence to hygiene protocols, including regular handwashing and sanitization of materials. implement remote access to literature where possible to minimize physical handling of materials.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18863.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of contracting COVID-19 or other contagious diseases through handling or reading materials that may have been contaminated.
