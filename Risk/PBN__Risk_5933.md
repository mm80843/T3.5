# __Calculating the reproductive number in the context of disease transmission__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Brizuela_understanding_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_421.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use accurate and up-to-date data for variables and parameters in the calculation, consider the impact of spatial and demographic factors.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7803.md)

Inaccurate calculation may underestimate the contagiousness of a disease, leading to ineffective control measures.
