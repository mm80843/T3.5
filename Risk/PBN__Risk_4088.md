# __Reliance on 5g technology for remote medical procedures may result in communication failures or network interruptions, leading to delays or errors in treatment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement redundant network systems, backup communication channels, and failover mechanisms to ensure continuous connectivity. regular testing and maintenance of network equipment to identify and address potential failures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5961.md)

## Name of the risk

Misdiagnosis, delayed treatment, or incomplete medical procedures due to interrupted communication and loss of real-time data transmission.
