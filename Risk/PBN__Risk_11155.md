# __Close physical interaction in service industries increases the risk of contagious diseases propagation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing strict hygiene and sanitation protocols, promoting use of personal protective equipment, and redesigning service delivery processes to minimize physical interaction.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13066.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased spread of contagious diseases and higher risk of infection for both service providers and customers.
