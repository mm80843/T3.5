# __Data security and privacy risks associated with the large amount of simulation data stored in a sqlite database.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lee_designing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_672.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strong encryption and access controls for the sqlite database. regularly audit and monitor database access to detect and prevent unauthorized activity.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21409.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential unauthorized access to sensitive simulation data, compromising privacy and leading to misuse of the data for malicious purposes.
