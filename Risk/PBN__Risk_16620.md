# __Incomplete or delayed implementation of npis in specific regions of saudi arabia.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Perez-saez_effect_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_613.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhanced coordination and monitoring of npi implementation across all regions, targeted support to regions with delayed implementation, and continuous evaluation of the effectiveness of npis in each region.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18566.md)

## Name of the risk

Uneven or delayed reduction in SARS-CoV-2 transmission, leading to pockets of high transmission and hindering overall control of the disease.
