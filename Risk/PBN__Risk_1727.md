## Description of the risk

Insufficient or ineffective air filtration systems can lead to the accumulation and recirculation of airborne pollutants and pathogens.

* [Yes](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/ISO/PBN__ISO_Scale_1.md)
