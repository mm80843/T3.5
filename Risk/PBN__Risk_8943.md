# __Co-location of ibem devices with reference sensors in an office environment may lead to potential contamination and spread of contagious diseases if proper hygiene and cleaning protocols are not followed.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Geng_intelligent_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_474.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene protocols for handling and cleaning the devices, including regular disinfection and handwashing. provide training to personnel on proper cleaning procedures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10839.md)

## Name of the risk

Unmitigated spread of contagious diseases in the office, leading to illness and reduced productivity.
