# __Lack of consideration for the impact of social and behavioral factors on contagious disease propagation in the built environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Koh_how_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_684.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporating social and behavioral data into topic modeling, conducting surveys or ethnographic studies, and integrating human factors research into disease propagation models.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22107.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to capture the influence of human behavior and interactions on disease transmission, leading to incomplete risk assessment.
