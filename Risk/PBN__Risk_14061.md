# __High cost and technical complexity of transforming existing buildings for pandemic response can limit the feasibility of design interventions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Invest in adaptive building technologies, streamline regulatory processes for building adaptations, and provide financial incentives for building transformations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15989.md)

## Name of the risk

Limited ability to transform and adapt buildings to address public health needs during pandemics, leading to potential transmission risks and insufficient facilities for disease control.
