# __Presence of flushable material such as toilet wipes and protective masks in wastewater may cause clogging of sewer systems and pumping stations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Poch_when_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_606.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Education on proper disposal of flushable materials, development of biodegradable alternatives, and installation of wastewater filtration systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18169.md)

## Name of the risk

Disruption of wastewater flow, potential overflow, and environmental contamination.
