# __Ahus including in-duct applications.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Memarzadeh_applications_2010](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_645.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper maintenance of ahus, including in-duct uvgi installations, and regular inspection of uvgi system effectiveness.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20060.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for air handling units to become sources of contamination if not properly maintained or if UVGI systems are not effectively implemented.
