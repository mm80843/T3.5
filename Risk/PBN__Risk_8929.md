# __Interference from heat dissipation affecting accuracy of air temperature and humidity sensors__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Geng_intelligent_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_474.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Control power of modules, place sensors at windward position, and increase distance between heat sources and sensors](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10825.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate measurement of environmental conditions, leading to potential mismanagement of air quality and disease transmission
