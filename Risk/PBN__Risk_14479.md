# __Data analysis involved close interaction with the respondents, increasing the risk of disease transmission during the research process.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Shibani_effects_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_570.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote strict hygiene practices, limit the duration of in-person interactions, and consider remote data analysis where feasible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16411.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of disease transmission among researchers and respondents due to close and prolonged interaction during data analysis.
