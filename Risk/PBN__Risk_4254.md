# __Need for suitable ventilation rates and open-ended corridors in hospital buildings__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
