# __Potential misinterpretation of sequencing data due to errors in sequence processing and analysis.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kembel_architectural_2014](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_687.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Adopt standardized bioinformatic pipelines and quality control measures to ensure accurate sequence processing. use reference databases and validation techniques to confirm taxonomic assignments. perform sensitivity analysis and cross-validation to validate results.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22234.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate taxonomic assignments and abundance estimations, leading to incorrect interpretation of microbial community composition and diversity.
