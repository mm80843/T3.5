# __Potential transmission of contagious diseases in the built environment due to close proximity and association among stakeholders.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Araya_influence_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_395.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enforce strict social distancing measures and regular sanitation practices within construction sites and built environments. implement regular health screenings for stakeholders and adopt remote work where possible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6243.md)

## Name of the risk

Increase in the spread of contagious diseases such as COVID-19 within construction sites and built environments.
