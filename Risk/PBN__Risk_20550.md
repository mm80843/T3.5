# __Blurring lines between work and home life could lead to increased transmission of contagious diseases within households.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kaklauskas_covid-19_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_693.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establishing clear guidelines for hygiene and sanitation in multipurpose homes, promoting vaccination, and providing remote healthcare services.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22530.md)

## Name of the risk

Unmitigated transmission of contagious diseases at home could lead to prolonged illness, spread to vulnerable individuals, and strain on healthcare systems.
