# __Limited occupancy measures may not be effectively enforced without proper control measures or technologies, leading to overcrowding and increased risk of physical interactions and disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement automated occupancy monitoring systems, use electronic counters, and enforce strict occupancy limits through access control systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12126.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of overcrowding, physical interactions, and disease transmission.
