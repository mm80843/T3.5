# __Humidification of air using water vapor may create an environment conducive to the growth and spread of airborne pathogens.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Vranay_adaptation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_524.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular monitoring and control of indoor humidity levels, use of hygienic humidification systems, and maintenance of air handling units to prevent microbial growth.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14146.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne diseases due to the presence of moisture and potential microbial growth in the HVAC system.
