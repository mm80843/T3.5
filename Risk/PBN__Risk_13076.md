# __Lack of access to private outdoor space for people suffering from lung diseases, leading to potential health decline.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tokazhanov_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_540.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Providing alternatives such as balconies and terraces in apartment blocks, promoting natural ventilation, and advocating for outdoor space availability.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14996.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased stress levels, limited ability to manage respiratory conditions, and higher risk of disease transmission.
