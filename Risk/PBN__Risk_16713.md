# __Lack of reported information on ev charging capability may lead to unmonitored and unregulated usage, increasing the risk of contagious disease spread through surface contact on ev charging stations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure clear communication and education to occupants about the availability and use of ev charging stations, including guidelines for hygiene and sanitation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18660.md)

## Name of the risk

Unregulated EV charging may contribute to the spread of contagious diseases among occupants and visitors.
