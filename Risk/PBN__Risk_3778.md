# __Regular use of vacuum cleaners can lead to the dispersion and circulation of dust, allergens, and pathogens within indoor environments.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Al-mumin_occupants_2003](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_386.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use vacuum cleaners with hepa filters, schedule cleaning when occupants are out, and promote thorough ventilation after cleaning.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5651.md)

## Name of the risk

Aggravation of respiratory conditions and potential transmission of airborne diseases.
