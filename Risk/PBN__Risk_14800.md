# __Inadequate consideration of human and behavioral factors in wnv transmission risk assessment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Semenza_prototype_2015](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_575.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate demographic, behavioral, and socio-economic data into risk assessments. collaborate with social scientists and public health experts to integrate human factors into the analysis.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16736.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to account for human behavior and population dynamics could lead to incomplete risk assessments and ineffective mitigation strategies.
