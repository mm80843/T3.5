# __Major focus on office buildings in research studies and practice-based efforts may neglect other types of buildings that also impact occupant health.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Awada_occupant_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_401.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct research studies and implement practice-based efforts in educational, healthcare, and other building types to broaden understanding of occupant health.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6697.md)

## Name of the risk

Limited understanding of occupant health in non-office buildings, potentially leading to unchecked spread of contagious diseases.
