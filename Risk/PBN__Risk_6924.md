# __Inconsistent accuracy results and heterogenous specificity and sensitivity values in wearable technology for covid-19 detection, leading to uncertainty in the performance of the technology.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheong_wearable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_435.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Standardize accuracy measures, establish clear criteria for sensitivity and specificity values, and ensure transparency in reporting accuracy results.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8800.md)

## Name of the risk

Uncertainty and lack of confidence in the accuracy of wearable technology to detect COVID-19 infections, potentially leading to false reassurance or unnecessary interventions.
