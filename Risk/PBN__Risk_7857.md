# __Uvc and uvgi light disinfectant strategies for buildings may have unintended consequences if not implemented with appropriate precaution.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dietz_2019_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_454.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct further research to verify the margin of safety before implementation, and implement with appropriate precaution.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9740.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of unintended consequences and potential harm to occupants if UVC and UVGI treatment is not implemented safely.
