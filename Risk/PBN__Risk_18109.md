# __Effectiveness of uvgi depends strongly on the type of virus nucleic acid, leading to varying susceptibility to uv inactivation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Memarzadeh_applications_2010](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_645.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct regular studies on virus susceptibility to uvgi to ensure effectiveness, and utilize supplemental control measures in addition to uvgi.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20073.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential ineffectiveness of UVGI in inactivating certain viruses, increasing the risk of viral transmission.
