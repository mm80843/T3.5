# __High demand and production volume may lead to insufficient monitoring of hygiene and cleanliness in the production process, increasing the risk of contamination and spread of contagious diseases through the shoes.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement regular hygiene audits, increase staffing for quality control, and invest in automated cleaning and disinfection systems for production facilities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13342.md)

## Name of the risk

Inadequate monitoring can result in the distribution of unhygienic shoes, posing a risk of spreading contagious diseases among consumers.
