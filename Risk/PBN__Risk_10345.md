# __Diverse nature attributes and their classification in architectural design__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhong_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_498.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop a standardized classification system for nature attributes in architectural design and provide clear guidelines for their usage.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12253.md)

## Name of the risk

Inadequate classification can lead to misunderstanding and misinterpretation of the role of nature in architectural design, leading to poor design choices.
