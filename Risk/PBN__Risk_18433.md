# __Informal signs printed in small font result in visually messy and unorganized presentation of information, increasing cognitive load on consumers.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Design and print larger, professionally made signs to consolidate information and reduce cognitive load on consumers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20400.md)

## Name of the risk

Customers may experience confusion and stress, leading to decreased satisfaction and potential loss of business for retailers.
