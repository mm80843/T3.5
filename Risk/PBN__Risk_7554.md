# __Crowdfunding campaigns may lead to increased public gatherings and physical interactions, contributing to the spread of contagious diseases like covid-19.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cumming_building_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_447.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict social distancing measures, enhance sanitation practices, and promote virtual participation in crowdfunding initiatives.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9437.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of transmission and spread of contagious diseases within the public due to gatherings and interactions during crowdfunding campaigns.
