# __Monitoring devices that track contact with infected people may raise privacy concerns and result in user resistance.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cantarero_covid19-routes_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_428.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strong data protection measures, obtain user consent, and provide transparent information about data usage and storage.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8215.md)
