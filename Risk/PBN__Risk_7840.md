# __Ineffective handwashing and hand sanitizing protocols__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dietz_2019_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_454.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Post signage about the effectiveness of handwashing, provide access to alcohol-based hand sanitizer, ensure soap dispensers are full, and implement routine surface cleaning protocols](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9723.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of microbial transmission and spread of undesirable pathogens
