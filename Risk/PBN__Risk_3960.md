# __Potential transmission of contagious diseases through shared holographic displays__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Allam_future_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_390.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular disinfection of the holographic displays, implementation of touchless interaction technology, and enforcement of hygiene practices for users](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5833.md)

## Name of the risk

Spread of illnesses and viruses among individuals who come into contact with the holographic displays
