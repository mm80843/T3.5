# __Lack of reflection of temperature and rh effects from the botanical filter in the simulation may lead to inaccurate assessment of energy consumption and thermal comfort.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_characterization_2011](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_522.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conducting field studies to measure actual temperature and humidity effects, and integrating a botanical filter model in the energyplus software.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14052.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate energy consumption predictions and potential discomfort due to unaccounted temperature and humidity effects.
