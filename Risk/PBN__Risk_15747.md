# __Using stochastic gradient descent sgd with warm restarts and a cosine annealing learning rate schedule may lead to challenges in hyperparameter tuning and model convergence, as well as the need for careful adjustment of learning rate values.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rezaei_deepsocial_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_593.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Hyperparameter search techniques such as grid search or random search, and meticulous validation of learning rate schedules to ensure model convergence and performance.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17688.md)

## Name of the risk

Difficulty in finding the optimal hyperparameters and learning rate values, leading to suboptimal model performance and longer training times.
