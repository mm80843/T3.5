# __Default values for virus properties may not reflect the characteristics of a specific virus, leading to inaccurate simulation results.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lee_designing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_672.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Allow users to input custom virus properties and provide guidance on obtaining accurate virus data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21417.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate simulation may lead to underestimation or overestimation of virus transmission risk in the built environment.
