# __Wearable sensor technology may provide a false sense of security, leading to decreased vigilance in following other necessary precautions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
