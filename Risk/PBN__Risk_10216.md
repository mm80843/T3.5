# __Continuous cleaning and sterilization practices may not be effective in preventing the spread of contagious diseases if not done properly or if the cleaning products used are not effective against the specific pathogen.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide proper training to janitorial staff on effective cleaning and sterilization practices. use cleaning products that are known to be effective against the specific pathogen.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12124.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission and contamination of surfaces.
