# __Impact of noise on exercise rates__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibn-mohammed_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_494.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promoting active transportation and public transit to reduce traffic congestion, creating more green spaces and pedestrian-friendly areas, implementing noise reduction measures in urban environments](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11950.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased exercise rates due to reduced traffic congestion and pleasant atmosphere, with average, moderate, and passive athletes increasing frequency of exercise regime by 88%, 38%, and 156% respectively
