# __Living in small apartments with several occupants in highly populated neighbourhoods predisposes the afro-american community to acquire sars cov-2.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Marconi_green_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_659.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improving living conditions, increasing access to green spaces, and providing mental health support.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20783.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of SARS CoV-2 transmission and associated health issues. Also, social confinement and distancing can lead to feelings of loneliness, depression, and other mental health issues.
