# __Conversion of relative estimates into absolute activity numbers may introduce errors due to calibration and sampling limitations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Venter_urban_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_525.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate error margins and confidence intervals into the analysis, validate absolute counts with other data sources, and consider the limitations of calibration methods.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14226.md)

## Name of the risk

Misinterpretation of recreational activity levels, leading to inaccurate city-wide counts and trends.
