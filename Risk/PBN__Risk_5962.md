# __Reduction in public transport ridership__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Budd_responsible_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_422.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Investing in safe and effective public transportation and promoting alternative modes of transportation such as cycling and walking.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7833.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased access to public transportation increases the use of private vehicles, leading to higher vehicle emissions and air pollution.
