# __Use of improperly adhered surgical disposable masks leading to reduced protection from sars-cov-2__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cirrincione_covid-19_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_441.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhance awareness about the limitations of disposable masks, promote use of properly adhered masks, and consider using additional ppe for added protection](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9035.md)
