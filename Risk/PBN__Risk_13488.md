# __High movement rates drive parasite flows across the region, creating areas that are simultaneously both major sources and sinks of parasites.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement cross-border collaboration and information sharing to coordinate malaria control efforts. conduct targeted vector control and surveillance at border areas to prevent parasite importation and exportation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15412.md)
