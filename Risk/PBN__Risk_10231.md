# __Replacement of air duct types from aluminum to fabric could lead to the spread of contaminants if not managed properly.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict disposal or washability procedures for fabric ducts, and regular cleaning and maintenance to prevent buildup of contaminants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12139.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of indoor contamination and transmission of contagious diseases.
