# __Use of induction units and fan coils with local circulation may lead to the resuspension of virus particles.__

* [Dehghani_hierarchy_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_452.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
