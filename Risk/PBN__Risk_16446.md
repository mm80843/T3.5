# __Inadequate ventilation in office environments increases the risk of airborne virus transmission due to the presence of micro respiratory droplets.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Phapant_covid-19_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_610.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase outdoor airflow rates, utilize air cleaners and filters, and maintain healthy temperature and relative humidity levels to minimize the spread of airborne viruses.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18389.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of contagious diseases within the office environment, leading to increased illness and absenteeism.
