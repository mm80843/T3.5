# __Close interactions and discussions during the consensus process for quality assessments may pose a risk of disease transmission among the individuals involved.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct consensus discussions in well-ventilated areas with limited physical proximity, use personal protective equipment, and encourage regular testing for contagious diseases among the individuals involved.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14822.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased likelihood of disease spread among the individuals conducting consensus discussions, potentially leading to the transmission of contagious diseases.
