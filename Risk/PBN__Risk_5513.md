# __Research involving viral titers may pose a risk of accidental exposure and transmission of the virus.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Behzadinasab_surface_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_414.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict biosafety protocols, use proper personal protective equipment, and conduct research in a controlled environment such as a biosafety level 2 or higher laboratory.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7386.md)

## Name of the risk

Unmitigated exposure can lead to the spread of the virus and potential infection of researchers and lab personnel.
