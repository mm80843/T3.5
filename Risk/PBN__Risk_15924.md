# __Increased reliance on telemedicine and mobile technology for healthcare consultations may result in inadequate physical examination and assessment of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Raza_mobile_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_596.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Developing standardized protocols for telemedicine consultations including physical assessment techniques and criteria for in-person visits. use of additional tools for remote assessment , wearable devices for vital sign monitoring .](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17866.md)

## Name of the risk

Missed or delayed diagnosis of contagious diseases, leading to delayed treatment and increased spread of the disease.
