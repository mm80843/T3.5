# __Rapid proliferation of wearable technology in the healthcare sector may lead to concerns regarding data privacy and security, leading to potential misuse or unauthorized access to sensitive health information.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheong_wearable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_435.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of robust data privacy and security measures, adherence to regulatory guidelines, and transparent communication with users about data protection and consent.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8782.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential breaches of data privacy and security can compromise the confidentiality and integrity of individuals' health data, as well as erode public trust in healthcare technology.
