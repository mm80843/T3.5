# __The susceptibility of coronavirus to uv irradiation, while effective, may not eliminate the risk of disease transmission through other means, such as surface contamination and airborne particles.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Combine uv irradiation with comprehensive cleaning and disinfection strategies for surfaces, and integrate it with air purification and filtration systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10272.md)

## Name of the risk

Incomplete mitigation of disease transmission, leading to potential infections despite UV irradiation.
