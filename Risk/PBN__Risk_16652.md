# __Increase in energy efficiency leading to a reduction in energy consumption__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance and cleaning of ventilation systems, implementation of air purification technologies, and monitoring of indoor air quality.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18599.md)
