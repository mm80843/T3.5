## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing crowd management strategies, scheduling appointments to reduce wait times, and creating spatial configurations that allow for social distancing.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18366.md)

## Name of the risk

Overcrowding at the vaccination clinic may increase the risk of disease transmission, leading to potential outbreaks and spread of contagious diseases.
