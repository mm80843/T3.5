# __Inclusion of covariates such as age, gender, education level, working mode, income decrease, presence of other people, and presence of pets in the regression models.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spano_association_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_559.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Consider the potential interaction effects between covariates and green features in the models to disentangle direct and indirect effects.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15893.md)

## Name of the risk

Over-reliance on covariates for explaining psychological health outcomes, potentially overlooking the direct impact of green features.
