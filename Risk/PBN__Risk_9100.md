# __Lack of partition in cubicles allowing direct transmission of aerosols between occupants__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_aerosol_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_477.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Install partitions or barriers between cubicles to prevent direct transmission of aerosols](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10998.md)

## Name of the risk
