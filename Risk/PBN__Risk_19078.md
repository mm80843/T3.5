# __Reduced air pollution from lockdown may lead to complacency and a return to high pollution levels post-lockdown.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Madurai_elavarasan_restructured_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_664.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Continued monitoring of air pollution levels and implementation of strict regulations to control industrial pollution post-lockdown.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21049.md)

## Name of the risk

If not mitigated, a return to high levels of air pollution could lead to respiratory problems and other health issues for the public.
