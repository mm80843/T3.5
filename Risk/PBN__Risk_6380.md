## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement restrictions on movement in the identified areas or prioritize safer routes to minimize exposure to contagious diseases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8252.md)

## Name of the risk
