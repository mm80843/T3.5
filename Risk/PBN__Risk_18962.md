# __Insufficient separation of wet and living spaces may lead to increased risk of cross-contamination and spread of pathogens.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Maharani_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_663.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement clear separation of wet and living spaces, use effective cleaning and disinfection protocols, provide designated areas for cleaning before entering living spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20934.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of infectious diseases spreading within the household.
