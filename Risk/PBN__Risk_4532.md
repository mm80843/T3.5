# __Conducting an in-person survey may pose a risk of disease transmission to participants and survey staff.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Asfour_housing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_398.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Prioritize virtual survey methods, implement strict health and safety protocols for in-person surveys, and provide necessary protective measures for staff and participants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6406.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential contraction of contagious diseases by survey participants and staff, leading to community spread and increased infection rates.
