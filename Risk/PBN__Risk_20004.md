# __Measurement of negative emotions related to covid-19 on a 7-point likert scale could lead to underreporting or misinterpretation of emotional impact.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kuiper_intelligent_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_681.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Include open-ended qualitative assessments in addition to likert scale ratings to capture a wider range of emotional experiences.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21983.md)

## Name of the risk

Underreporting of negative emotions may hinder the understanding of the true psychological toll of the pandemic, leading to inadequate support and intervention.
