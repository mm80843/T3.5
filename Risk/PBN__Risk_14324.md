# __Availability of source code and package may lead to unauthorized modifications or misuse of the simulation model.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Silva_covid-abs_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_567.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict version control and access restrictions to the source code and package. provide clear guidelines for authorized use and modification of the model.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16254.md)

## Name of the risk

Unauthorized modifications or misuse of the simulation model can lead to unreliable results and misinformed decision-making for disease control.
