# __Exhaust vents positioned in the lower part of the room may lead to air contamination and increased risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Escombe_upper-room_2009](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_464.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Repositioning exhaust vents to ensure proper air circulation and ventilation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10294.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne disease transmission and exposure to contaminants for room occupants and guinea pigs.
