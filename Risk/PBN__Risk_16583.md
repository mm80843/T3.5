# __Crowded shared spaces such as lobbies, elevators, and laundry rooms can facilitate the spread of contagious diseases among residents.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Peters_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_612.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement capacity limits, promote mask-wearing, and enhance ventilation in enclosed shared spaces. utilize touchless technologies for entry and exit points.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18529.md)

## Name of the risk

Elevated risk of disease transmission and potential outbreaks, leading to compromised public health within the apartment housing.
