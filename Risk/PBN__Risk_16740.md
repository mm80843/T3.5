# __Inadequate ventilation and air circulation systems in healthcare facilities__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Upgrade ventilation and air circulation systems, conduct regular maintenance and inspections, implement air purification technologies](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18687.md)

## Name of the risk
