## Articles mentionning the risk

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement limits on the quantity of essential supplies that can be purchased, provide clear and consistent communication to the public about the availability of essential supplies, and encourage online shopping and home delivery](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13424.md)
