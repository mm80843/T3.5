# __Continuous monitoring of indoor thermal performance throughout the year may increase the risk of spreading contagious diseases within the building.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Geng_intelligent_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_474.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene protocols and ventilation measures to reduce the spread of airborne contaminants. ensure regular cleaning and disinfection of monitoring equipment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10852.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of airborne or respiratory contagious diseases spreading among occupants if the indoor monitoring is not managed properly.
