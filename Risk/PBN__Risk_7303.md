* [Collin_using_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_443.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Clear and comprehensive communication of the interpretation and limitations of the iptcc index by experts.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9184.md)

## Name of the risk

Misinterpretation of the IPTCC index could lead to mistaken conclusions about the risk of virus transmission based on temperature and humidity.
