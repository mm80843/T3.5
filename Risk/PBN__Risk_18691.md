# __Caution against drawing conclusions about virus survival under real-world conditions based on laboratory-based studies.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Marr_mechanistic_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_656.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct studies that closely mimic real-world conditions, validate laboratory findings with real-world data, and consider epidemiological evidence.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20661.md)

## Name of the risk

Use of inaccurate findings to inform real-world conditions may result in inappropriate public health interventions and increased disease transmission risk.
