# __Insufficient ventilation rates in high-occupancy buildings leading to increased risk of airborne disease transmission__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhang_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_500.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
