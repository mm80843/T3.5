# __Potential spread of contagious diseases through wearable devices and environmental sensors in the home hospitalization system.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and sterilization of wearable devices and environmental sensors, strict hygiene protocols for healthcare staff and family members, and use of antimicrobial materials in device construction.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8667.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of COVID-19 transmission and other contagious diseases among patients, healthcare staff, and family members.
