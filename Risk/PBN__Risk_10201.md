# __Proximity of lockers, toilets, and change booths may increase the risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Redesigning the layout to separate high-traffic areas and improve ventilation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12109.md)
