# __Boundaries and property lines defining and protecting private ownership can constrain the expansion of professional responsibilities and practices in the built environment, hindering the integration of cooperative networked infrastructure.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cole_navigating_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_442.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Reevaluate and redefine property boundaries to allow for the integration of buildings into cooperative networked systems. encourage stakeholder engagement and commitment in regenerative development projects.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9142.md)

## Name of the risk

Limiting the role of buildings as potential resource nodes within a cooperative networked infrastructure, inhibiting regenerative development and design.
