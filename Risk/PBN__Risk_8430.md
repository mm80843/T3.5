# __Lack of safety measures leading to adverse uv effects on patients and staff__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Escombe_upper-room_2009](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_464.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Appropriate positioning and installation of modern, shielded uv fixtures. checking of uv fields to ensure no excess uv light is reflected downwards. use of personal uv meters to measure exposures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10320.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential harm to patients and staff due to UV over-exposure
