# __The use of non-peer-reviewed data for clinical practice may result in incorrect or harmful medical decisions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wu_open-source_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_508.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Emphasize the non-peer-reviewed nature of the data, and use it as an aid to informed decision-making rather than a sole basis for clinical practice.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12682.md)

## Name of the risk

Incorrect diagnosis, treatment, or prevention strategies, leading to patient harm.
