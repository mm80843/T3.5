# __Lack of clear guidance on non-essential travel may result in employees traveling to high-risk areas and bringing contagious diseases back to the workplace.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Strict definition of essential travel, adherence to health protection agency guidelines, and implementation of return-to-work protocols for employees visiting high-risk locations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13531.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease spread within the workplace, potential exposure of coworkers, and potential disruption of business operations due to employee illness or quarantine.
