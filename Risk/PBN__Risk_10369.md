# __Lack of ventilation and natural light in built environment leading to poor indoor air quality and mental health issues.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhong_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_498.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Installation of ventilation systems, air purifiers, and incorporation of biophilic design elements for natural light and green spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12277.md)
