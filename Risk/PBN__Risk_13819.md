# __Rear extensions do not allow for a ready installation of a containment space without sacrificing room functionality or garden space.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_residential_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_557.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Careful consideration of spatial layout and allocation, and potentially rethinking the design of rear extensions to accommodate a containment space.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15744.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to establish a containment space may hinder disease control and management within the built environment.
