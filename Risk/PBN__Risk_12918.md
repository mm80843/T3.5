# __Limited scope may result in overlooking the impact of greenspace on certain health outcomes, leading to incomplete evidence for decision-making.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Expand the scope of the review to include a broader range of health outcomes related to greenspace exposure and its potential benefits.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14837.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to consider all relevant health outcomes associated with greenspace exposure may result in suboptimal planning and resource allocation for public health interventions and urban development.
