# __Use of ineffective or inappropriate filtration and cleaning methods in hvac systems.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_review_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_478.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and replacement of hvac filters, avoidance of chemical disinfection, and proper operation of indoor air cleaners.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11052.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne disease transmission and compromised health of occupants.
