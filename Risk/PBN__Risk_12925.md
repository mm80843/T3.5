# __Living in a greener neighborhood is linked with stronger greenspace-health associations and lower income-related health inequalities__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Equitable distribution of greenspaces across neighborhoods, ensuring accessibility and affordability of greenspace amenities for all residents, and promoting the health benefits of greenspaces through community education](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14844.md)
