# __Lack of valid and reliable tools for assessing participant outcomes related to green space exposure and contagious diseases.__

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
