# __Inadequate open and closed spaces for gatherings and social interaction__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bojovic_towards_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_416.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Design and implement open spaces, outdoor spaces, and ventilation systems to facilitate safe social interaction and gatherings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7497.md)

## Name of the risk

Limited space for social interaction and gatherings, leading to higher risk of indoor transmission of communicable diseases.
