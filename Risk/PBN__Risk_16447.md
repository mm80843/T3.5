# __Office buildings were not designed with sufficient consideration for infectious disease control, leading to an increased risk of viral spread within the workplace.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Phapant_covid-19_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_610.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Redesign office buildings to incorporate infectious disease control features, such as improved ventilation, air filtration, and layout modifications to reduce crowding.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18390.md)

## Name of the risk

Lack of infectious disease control features in building design increases the vulnerability of office workers to contagious diseases and reduces workplace productivity.
