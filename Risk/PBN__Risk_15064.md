# __Historical and contemporary discussions of urban mobility and spatial structure may overlook the potential impact of contagious diseases on population movement and city networks.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Salama_coronavirus_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_580.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

## Name of the risk
