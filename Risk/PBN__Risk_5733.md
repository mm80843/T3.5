# __Dependence of air supply diffuser performance on user position, angle, clothing insulation, and ambient air temperature.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bolashikov_methods_2009](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_417.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilize technology to monitor user position and clothing insulation, and adjust diffuser angle accordingly. also, regulate ambient air temperature.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7606.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced air quality for the user if these factors are not optimized.
