# __Increased use of balconies as a result of the pandemic may lead to crowding and close contact between individuals, increasing the risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aydin_questioning_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_403.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing guidelines for maximum occupancy on balconies, promoting social distancing, and encouraging the use of masks when using shared balcony spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6846.md)
