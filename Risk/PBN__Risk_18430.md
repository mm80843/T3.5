# __Informal signage and communication strategies in retail environments could lead to ineffective messaging about covid-19 precautions and symptoms.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement standardized, visually appealing signs with clear messaging about covid-19 precautions and symptoms. provide training to employees on effective communication of health and safety information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20397.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for customers to miss important COVID-19 information, leading to a higher risk of transmission within the retail environment.
