# __Inadequate hygiene and sanitization practices in the workplace.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bou_hatoum_analysis_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_419.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene and sanitization practices, provide necessary supplies, and enforce regular cleaning protocols. also, adopt a record-keeping system for tracking sanitization activities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7715.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of virus transmission due to poor hygiene, leading to potential outbreaks and employee illness.
