# __Lack of transparency in parameter certainty levels may lead to distrust in the system and customization with lower confidence.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lee_designing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_672.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide a system that displays the certainty level of each parameter, allowing users to make informed decisions and build trust in the system.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21395.md)

## Name of the risk

Users may not trust the system and may not be able to customize it with confidence, leading to decreased user adoption and satisfaction.
