# __Inadequate space planning and design in buildings can lead to inefficient utilization of space and hinder the ability to accommodate public health measures during pandemics.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Re-evaluate building design standards to include flexibility for transformation and pandemic response, prioritize efficient space utilization, and consider retrofitting existing buildings for adaptability.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15988.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission due to overcrowding and inadequate space for necessary public health measures.
