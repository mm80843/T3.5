# __Presence of antiviral drugs and their metabolized forms in wastewater streams contribute to environmental contamination and potential long-term impacts on microbial resistance.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sims_future_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_566.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop advanced wastewater treatment processes specifically designed to remove antiviral drugs and their metabolites from wastewater, and increase monitoring and research on their environmental impact.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16184.md)
