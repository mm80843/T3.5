# __Mechanically ventilated rooms may have significantly varying ventilation rates depending on their location and layout.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhang_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_500.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of uniform ventilation system designs and regular testing and maintenance of ventilation rates.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12416.md)

## Name of the risk

Uneven ventilation rates can lead to areas with inadequate ventilation, increasing the risk of airborne disease transmission.
