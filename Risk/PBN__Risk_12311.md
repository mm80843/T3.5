# __Detrending the time series data to account for the long-term trend in strava data may oversimplify the analysis and mask important variations in activity patterns.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Venter_urban_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_525.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use detrending methods cautiously, consider the impact of detrending on data interpretation, and explore alternative approaches to capture long-term trends without losing short-term variations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14227.md)

## Name of the risk

Loss of nuance in understanding recreational activity trends, leading to misrepresentation of true activity dynamics.
