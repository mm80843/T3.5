# __Unsubstantiated marketing claims suggesting that uvgi systems will protect against emerging diseases such as sars cov, influenza, m tuberculosis, and bioterrorism agents.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Memarzadeh_applications_2010](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_645.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conducting rigorous studies to assess the effectiveness of uvgi systems in protecting against specific diseases, and regulating marketing claims to ensure accuracy and evidence-based advertising.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20024.md)

## Name of the risk

False sense of security and potential misallocation of resources based on unsubstantiated marketing claims.
