## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Oni_implications_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_623.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Distributed fresh food supplies closer to neighborhoods, introduced community-based cashless farmers markets, implemented fruit preservation techniques, and created new space for walking and cycling](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19061.md)
