# __Refrigerators are commonly used in kuwaiti residences for storing food, and the presence of multiple units in each residence may lead to cross-contamination if not maintained properly.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Al-mumin_occupants_2003](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_386.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement regular cleaning and maintenance schedules for refrigerators, educate occupants on food safety practices, and monitor refrigerator temperature and cleanliness.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5653.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of foodborne illness and spoilage due to improper storage and maintenance of refrigerators.
