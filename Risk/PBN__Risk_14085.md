# __Inadequate spacing and allocation for car parking__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Redesign parking area layout to increase spacing, implement digital parking systems to reduce contact, and improve ventilation and air quality in the parking area.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16013.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased congestion and close contact between individuals in the parking area, leading to potential disease transmission.
