# __Sharing ventilators between 2 patients increases the risk of cross-contamination and spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhao_supporting_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_499.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict cleaning and sterilization protocols between patient use, and consider alternative solutions to address ventilator shortages.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12322.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of infectious diseases to multiple patients, leading to higher infection rates and potential severe health complications.

## People affected by this risk

* [Patients sharing ventilators and healthcare workers attending to these patients](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_8499.md)

## Stakeholders who can mitigate this risk

* [Healthcare facilities and medical equipment manufacturers](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_8500.md)

## Technologies linked to the risk

* [Advanced sterilization technologies](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Technology/PBN__Technology_6083.md)
* [And development of low-cost ventilators for individual patient use](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Technology/PBN__Technology_12265.md)
