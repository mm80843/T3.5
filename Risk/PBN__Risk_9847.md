# __Rapid tracer decay or ’whoosh’ observed after opening a window, leading to a short-lived, significant dilution of existing tracer gas in the immediate air volume.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Howard-reed_effect_2002](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_490.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing controlled ventilation strategies to minimize the impact of rapid tracer decay, such as adjusting window opening size and duration.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11754.md)

## Name of the risk

Temporary decrease in air quality and potential exposure to outdoor contaminants during the 'whoosh' period.
