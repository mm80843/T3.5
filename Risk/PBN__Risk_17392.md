# __Preference for online questionnaires among younger respondents may result in overrepresentation of this age group in the survey data, leading to age bias in the analysis and conclusions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Noszczyk_impact_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_630.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Employ sampling and weighting techniques to account for age bias and validate the survey findings through supplementary methods.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19347.md)

## Name of the risk

Age bias in the survey data may lead to inaccurate insights into the impact of contagious diseases on different age groups, affecting the efficacy of public health interventions targeted at specific age demographics.
