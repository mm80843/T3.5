# __Failure to maintain pressure differentials leading to inadequate cleanliness control__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_review_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_478.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular monitoring and maintenance of pressure differentials, use of mechanical ventilation systems to maintain airflow direction](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11061.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of contaminated air spreading from low-cleanliness areas to high-cleanliness areas
