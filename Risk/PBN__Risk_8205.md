# __Lack of evidence on the resistance of antimicrobial materials to viral loads may lead to misconceptions about their effectiveness.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elrayies_prophylactic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_462.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct scientific studies and testing to provide clear evidence of the ability of antimicrobial materials to resist viral contamination.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10091.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate belief in the ability of materials to resist viral contamination, leading to a false sense of security.
