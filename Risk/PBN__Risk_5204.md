# __Complex microfluidic systems with interconnected networks may be prone to contamination and spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Barcelo_wastewater-based_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_408.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and maintenance of the microfluidic elements, proper disposal of contaminated materials, and implementation of strict infection control protocols.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7078.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of inaccurate diagnostic results and potential transmission of diseases to operators and patients.
