# __Increased health harming behaviors during lockdown, including smoking, alcohol consumption, and poor diet.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Gurney_dangerous_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_479.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promotion of healthy behaviors through education, support services, and access to nutritious food options.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11139.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of non-communicable diseases (NCDs) such as coronary heart disease, stroke, hypertension, type 2 diabetes, and some forms of cancer.
