# __Inadequate positioning or installation of uv fixtures leading to occupant over-exposure__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Escombe_upper-room_2009](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_464.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Expert installation and post-installation checking of uv fields to ensure no excess uv light is reflected downwards. use of shielded uv fixtures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10319.md)

## Name of the risk
