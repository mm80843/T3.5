# __Potential influence of the funding bodies on the dissemination and interpretation of the research findings, despite lack of involvement in the study.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Li_association_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_669.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establish clear policies on the independence of research findings from funding bodies. engage in transparent and independent dissemination of research results.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21220.md)
