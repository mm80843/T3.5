# __Reliance on wireless communication using zigbee module may introduce interference and communication disruptions in iaq monitoring system.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saini_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_583.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement frequency hopping and signal strength optimization to minimize interference, conduct thorough site survey for optimal network placement.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17231.md)

## Name of the risk

Data loss, delay in real-time monitoring, and missed critical IAQ events, potentially compromising the effectiveness of the monitoring system.
