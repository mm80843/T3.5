# __Proposing a constrained optimization problem for physical space design without considering all relevant factors and constraints.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ugail_social_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_534.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct a thorough analysis of all relevant factors and constraints, including but not limited to physical distancing, air circulation, door and window positions, humidity, and lighting levels. incorporate expert knowledge and public health guidelines into the design process.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14717.md)

## Name of the risk

Ineffective social distancing arrangements, increased risk of disease transmission, and potential non-compliance with public health guidelines.
