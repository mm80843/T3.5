# __Travel time by public transport to activity centers may impact the spread of epidemics within cities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Li_association_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_669.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement targeted public health measures at activity centers, improve public transport sanitation and ventilation, and regulate capacity to reduce crowding.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21266.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of epidemic spread if travel time by public transport facilitates the movement of infected individuals to activity centers.
