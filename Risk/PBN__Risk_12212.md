# __Potential leakage of viral aerosols due to inadequate sealing of chamber parts.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Walker_effect_2007](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_523.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper sealing of chamber parts and use gaskets to prevent leaks.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14128.md)

## Name of the risk

Uncontrolled release of viral aerosols into the environment, increasing the risk of disease transmission.
