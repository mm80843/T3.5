# __Indicators of green space quality are influenced by outcomes in bird assemblages, leading to potential bias in wildlife outcome assessments.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Felappi_green_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_467.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Inclusion of measures that assess qualitative aspects of community composition, addressing less studied animal groups, and using standard metrics for comparison.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10500.md)

## Name of the risk

Misinterpretation of green space quality and its impact on wildlife due to biased indicators.
