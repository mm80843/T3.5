# __Ai-enabled technology dependency for diagnosis may lead to misdiagnosis or missed diagnoses due to technological errors or limitations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sharifi_contributions_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_573.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular validation and verification of the ai algorithms, training of healthcare professionals to actively verify ai-enabled diagnoses, and having alternate diagnostic methods in place.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16619.md)

## Name of the risk

Misdiagnosis or missed diagnoses can lead to delayed treatment or improper management of contagious diseases, leading to increased spread and severity of the disease.
