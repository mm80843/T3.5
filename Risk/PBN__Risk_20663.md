# __Insufficient consideration of green housing policies and guidelines in the context of pandemic-related risks.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kaklauskas_covid-19_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_693.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop and implement green housing policies that consider pandemic-related risks and prioritize measures to reduce disease transmission. integrate public health expertise into green housing research and policy development.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22645.md)

## Name of the risk

Lack of appropriate policies may result in increased transmission of contagious diseases in built environments, risking public health.
