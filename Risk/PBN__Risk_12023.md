# __Improper disinfection of hospital wastes may lead to the persistence of infectious materials.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_disinfection_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_520.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Adoption of effective disinfection methods for different types of hospital wastes and regular monitoring of disinfection performance.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13937.md)

## Name of the risk

Continued presence of infectious materials may pose a risk of transmission and contribute to the spread of contagious diseases.
