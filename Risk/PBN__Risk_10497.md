# __Ineffective operation and maintenance of hepa filtration systems leading to reduced efficiency in particle removal and air purification.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhang_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_500.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establish a comprehensive maintenance protocol including regular filter replacement, airflow monitoring, and sealing inspections. utilize advanced filtration monitoring systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12408.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased filtration efficiency, allowing particles to circulate and accumulate in the indoor environment, increasing the risk of disease transmission.
