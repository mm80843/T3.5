# __Sending food to healthcare workers in various hospitals may contribute to the spread of contagious diseases if proper hygiene measures are not taken in the food preparation and delivery process.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene protocols for food preparation and delivery, including regular sanitization of facilities and equipment, and training for staff on safe food handling practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13264.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of spreading contagious diseases among healthcare workers and other stakeholders in hospitals.
