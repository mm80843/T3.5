## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict health and sanitation protocols, provide education on contagious disease prevention, and establish monitoring and reporting mechanisms for symptoms.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12950.md)
