# __Ai in treatment may lead to over-reliance on automated systems, potentially overlooking critical patient factors and individualized treatment needs.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Madurai_elavarasan_restructured_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_664.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Training healthcare professionals to interpret ai recommendations critically, integrating ai as a support tool rather than a sole decision-maker, and promoting patient-centered care with emphasis on individualized treatment plans.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21023.md)

## Name of the risk

Patients may receive standardized treatment without considering unique medical histories, personal preferences, or other relevant factors.
