# __Cross-infection due to the supply of recirculated air in buildings with centralized air-conditioning systems__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_review_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_478.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Install hepa filters in centralized air-conditioning systems and maintain proper filter size and efficiency](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11027.md)

## Name of the risk

Increases the risk of infectious aerosol transmission within the building
