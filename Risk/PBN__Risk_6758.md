# __Wearable devices and unobtrusive sensing technologies may not accurately monitor early symptoms of covid-19 or common health conditions, leading to misdiagnosis or delayed treatment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validation of the accuracy and reliability of wearable devices through clinical trials and research studies, regular updates and improvements to the sensing technologies, education and training for users on the limitations of wearables.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8632.md)

## Name of the risk

Mismanagement of early symptoms of COVID-19 or other health conditions, potential spread of contagious diseases.

* [Healthcare professionals relying on wearable data for diagnosis and treatment](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_5682.md)
