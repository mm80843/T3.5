# __Inefficiency or ineffectiveness of chlorine dioxide as a disinfectant__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_disinfection_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_520.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Proper dosing and monitoring of chlorine dioxide levels, regular maintenance of dosing systems, and adherence to recommended usage guidelines](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13975.md)

Inadequate disinfection of wastewater, leading to the spread of contagious diseases and microbial contamination
