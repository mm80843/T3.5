# __Contact with natural features vegetation and water can generate a quick and positive psychological reaction, reducing negative emotions and fostering recovery from physiological stress and health problems.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhong_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_498.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporating natural features such as vegetation and water bodies within built environments, creating green spaces and parks, and promoting access to natural landscapes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12272.md)

## Name of the risk

If access to natural features is limited, it may lead to higher levels of stress, negatively impacting mental and physiological well-being.
