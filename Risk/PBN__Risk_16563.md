# __Inadequate research on flexible apartment design systems may lead to unsatisfactory living spaces in postcovid-19 housing.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Peters_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_612.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct research on flexible design systems that provide adaptability and convenience for various activities within the apartment. additionally, ensure that the design is user-friendly and meets the diverse needs of residents.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18508.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased liveability and adaptability of apartment housing, potential dissatisfaction and discomfort for residents.
