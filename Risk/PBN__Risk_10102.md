# __Refurbishment and renovation activities in the built environment can contribute to the spread of contagious diseases if proper hygiene and sanitation measures are not implemented.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibn-mohammed_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_494.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Adopt stringent cleaning and sanitation practices, promote regular handwashing and disinfection, and implement social distancing measures during renovation projects.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12009.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential transmission of infectious diseases among workers and occupants of refurbished buildings, leading to public health risks.
