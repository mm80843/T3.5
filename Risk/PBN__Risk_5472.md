# __Poor room air mixing and stagnant regions__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Beggs_upper-room_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_413.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Supplement upper-room uvgi systems with ceiling mounted fans or other devices to promote necessary air movement](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7345.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of larger aerosol particles remaining suspended in room air for many minutes and being inhaled by room occupants
