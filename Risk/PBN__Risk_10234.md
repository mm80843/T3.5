# __Infrequent cleaning of air diffusers and lack of complete sterilization sessions increase the risk of indoor contamination.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase frequency of air diffuser cleaning, incorporate complete sterilization sessions into preventive maintenance schedules, and use antibacterial sprays to disinfect air after use.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12142.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher likelihood of disease transmission within the facility.
