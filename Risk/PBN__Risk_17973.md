# __Inaccurate validation of the mathematical model for estimating the number of infected people due to limited consideration of infection mechanisms.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mokhtari_effect_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_643.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
