# __Use of antibacterial fabrics and finishes may lead to a false sense of security and reduce adherence to proper hygiene practices.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Awada_ten_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_402.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Education on hygiene best practices, regular communication on the limitations of antibacterial materials.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6791.md)
