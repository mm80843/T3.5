# __Utilization of low-cost micro gas sensors and arduino microcontroller may result in inaccurate iaq monitoring and data quality issues.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saini_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_583.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Calibrate and validate the sensor data regularly, employ redundancy and data validation techniques to cross-verify sensor readings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17230.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate IAQ readings, unreliable data for decision-making, potential health risks due to false information.
