# __Informal signs with handwritten and typed messages were used to communicate store closures and operating status.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establish clear and formal communication channels, such as email, phone numbers, social media, or websites, to provide accurate and timely information to customers and the community.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20409.md)

## Name of the risk

Lack of formal communication may lead to confusion and misunderstandings among customers and the community, potentially affecting public health and safety.
