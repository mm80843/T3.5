# __Connecting to the local natural environment through incorporating regional features geomorphology and landscape in buildings could generate the ’sense of place’ and ’sense of community’ and realize personal identity, belonging, and cohesion.__

* [Zhong_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_498.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporating regional features, preserving natural landscapes, and designing buildings to reflect the local natural environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12273.md)

## Name of the risk

If regional features are not considered in building design, it may lead to a lack of connection to the local natural environment, impacting the sense of place, personal identity, and community cohesion.
