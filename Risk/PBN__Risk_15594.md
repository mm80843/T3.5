# __Collection of audio material from multiple locations around the world may introduce contagious diseases from different regions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rogers_city_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_589.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict sanitation protocols for handling and sharing audio material, including disinfection and quarantine procedures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17534.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of contagious diseases to individuals handling the audio material or those interacting with the material in various locations.
