# __Use of voice assistants and social robots may facilitate the spread of contagious diseases by allowing interactions without physical contact.__

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene measures, enforce physical distancing, and limit the number of simultaneous interactions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13224.md)
