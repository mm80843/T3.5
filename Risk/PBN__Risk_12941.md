# __Operation of a research laboratory, store, and food/agriculture laboratory may create environments conducive to disease transmission if proper health and safety measures are not observed.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tricarico_proximity_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_538.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enforce strict sanitation protocols, provide personal protective equipment, and promote vaccination among staff and customers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14860.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease outbreak among employees, researchers, and customers, leading to public health concerns and reputational damage.
