# __Comparing the bentley and oxford stringency indices in simulations may lead to inconsistent r0 calculations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mukerjee_mitigation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_639.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
