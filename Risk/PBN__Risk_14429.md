# __Use of the mouse click event for selecting roi may introduce the risk of contamination through shared surfaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Shorfuzzaman_towards_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_569.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use touchless or voice-activated input methods for selecting roi, or regularly sanitize the mouse and surrounding area.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16361.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission due to shared surfaces, leading to potential spread of contagious diseases.

## People affected by this risk

* [As well as those in contact with the mouse and surrounding area](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_11716.md)
* [Individuals using the mouse for roi selection](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_11717.md)
