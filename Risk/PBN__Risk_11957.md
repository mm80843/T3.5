# __Reliance on non-contact interaction activities during the covid-19 pandemic__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_urban_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_517.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote hybrid interaction models that combine non-contact activities with safe physical engagement; prioritize mental health support and resources for isolated individuals](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13870.md)

## Name of the risk

Dependency on non-contact activities may lead to reduced physical interaction and community engagement, impacting mental health and social well-being
