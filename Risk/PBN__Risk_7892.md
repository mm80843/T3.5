# __Detection of positive samples in the toilet area and potential presence of sars-cov-2 in patient stool samples present an increased risk of viral spread in the built environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ding_toilets_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_455.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular testing and monitoring of toilet surfaces and patient stool samples, implementation of strict hygiene protocols, and isolation of affected individuals.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9775.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for increased transmission of SARS-CoV-2 within the built environment due to the presence of the virus in the toilet area and patient stool samples.
