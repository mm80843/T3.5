# __Deployment of ai and ml for disease prediction and diagnosis may lead to biases in decision-making and inaccurate assessments, impacting treatment and resource allocation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Subramanian_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_556.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular assessment of algorithm biases, diversification of training data, and transparency in ai decision-making processes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15696.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of misdiagnosis, unequal treatment based on demographic factors, and inefficiencies in healthcare delivery.
