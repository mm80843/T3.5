# __Major decline in fixed office internet access leading to increased reliance on shared public wi-fi networks.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Allam_future_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_390.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement secure and encrypted wi-fi networks, promote the use of vpns for secure connections, and raise awareness about the risks of using public wi-fi for sensitive activities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5802.md)
