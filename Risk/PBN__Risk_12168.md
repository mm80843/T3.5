# __Quasi-steady state assumption may lead to inaccurate estimation of emission rates, causing potential misinterpretation of data.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_characterization_2011](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_522.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct regular validation of emission estimation methods and consider transient effects in the mass balance equation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14083.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate estimation of emission rates can lead to incorrect conclusions about the level of air contamination in the built environment.
