# __Lack of quantitative data reported on the impacts of covid-19 on energy use of lighting and hvac loads in individual buildings, leading to uncertainty and potential inefficiencies in energy management strategies.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kawka_impacts_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_689.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct detailed energy audits, collect and analyze data on energy use before and during the pandemic, and develop adaptive energy management strategies based on data-driven insights.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22362.md)
