# __Potential leakage of exhaust air back into the air supply due to energy recovery ventilation systems.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Nembhard_ventilation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_634.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Bypassing energy recovery ventilation systems that have potential leaks and ensuring proper exhaust air management.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19522.md)
