# __Use of virtual reality for healthcare education may not accurately replicate real-world medical scenarios, leading to gaps in knowledge and skills.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Singh_significant_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_564.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Supplement virtual reality training with real-world practical experience and mentorship, validate virtual reality simulations against real-world scenarios.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16143.md)

## Name of the risk

Ineffective training of medical staff, potential misdiagnosis or mistreatment of patients, compromised patient safety.
