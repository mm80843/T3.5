# __Limited availability of historical data through the dashboard may hinder comprehensive analysis of building performance, including the tracking of long-term trends and patterns in indoor environmental quality.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Parkinson_continuous_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_615.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of a secure and user-friendly system for accessing archival data, with appropriate access controls and data visualization tools for comprehensive analysis.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18722.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to identify prolonged indoor environmental quality issues and address them effectively, leading to potential health risks and decreased occupant satisfaction.
