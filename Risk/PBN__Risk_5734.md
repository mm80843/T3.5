# __Draught felt by people at high air flow rates from personalized air supply nozzles.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bolashikov_methods_2009](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_417.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use technology to adjust air flow rates dynamically based on user feedback and environmental conditions, and design nozzles to minimize draught effects.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7607.md)

## Name of the risk

Discomfort and dissatisfaction with the air supply system, potentially reducing its effectiveness.
