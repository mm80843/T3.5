# __Inadequate infrastructure and facilities for managing contagious disease outbreaks in public venues, leading to compromised patient care and increased risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Harris_respiratory_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_482.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensuring appropriate layout design, electricity, waste management, and ventilation systems in public venues, as well as strategic location and accessibility.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11276.md)

## Name of the risk
