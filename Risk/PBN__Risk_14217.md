# __Potential ethical challenges in the use of virtual reality for medical education, such as privacy concerns and appropriate use of patient data in simulations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Singh_significant_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_564.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict data security measures, obtain informed consent for use of patient data in virtual reality simulations, adhere to ethical guidelines and regulations for use of patient information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16145.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of unauthorized access to patient data, violation of patient privacy, ethical issues related to the use of patient information in simulations.
