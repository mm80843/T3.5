# __Potential privacy concerns with the collection and storage of personal health data through mobile applications.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Whitelaw_applications_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_513.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of robust data security measures, user consent for data collection, and adherence to data protection regulations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13670.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of data breaches, unauthorized access, or misuse of personal health data, leading to privacy violations and loss of trust in the application.
