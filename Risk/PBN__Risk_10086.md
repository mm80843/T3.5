# __Cross-contamination of medical accessories in factories re-purposed for different products.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibn-mohammed_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_494.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict sanitization protocols and sterilization procedures before and after repurposing factory floors.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11993.md)
