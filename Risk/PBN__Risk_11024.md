# __Relying on communal support for renovation work may pose health risks during contagious disease outbreaks.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement health and safety guidelines for communal activities, provide protective equipment, and promote awareness of disease prevention measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12934.md)

## Name of the risk
