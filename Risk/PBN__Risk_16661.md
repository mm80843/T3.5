# __Introduction of demand-based control and automated functions may lead to an increase in aerosol transmission of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement air filtration systems, uv sterilization, and regular cleaning and maintenance of automated systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18608.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of aerosol transmission due to automation and reduced manual control.
