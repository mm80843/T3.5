# __Relying solely on 3d representation for understanding complex behaviors and virus situations may lead to limited insights and understanding of the overall environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lee_designing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_672.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide training and guidance on interpreting 3d visualizations, and incorporate additional data representations , statistical summaries, heatmaps to complement the 3d visualization.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21397.md)

## Name of the risk

Users may miss important patterns and interactions that are not easily observable in 3D representation alone, leading to potential oversights in mitigating contagious disease transmission.
