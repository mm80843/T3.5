# __Smart home devices may not accurately diagnose or assess the health status of individuals, leading to potential misdiagnoses or delayed medical interventions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Umair_impact_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_531.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular validation and testing of health-related features, clear disclaimers about the limitations of the technology, and user education on the proper use of health-related features.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14515.md)

## Name of the risk

Misdiagnoses, delayed medical treatment, and potential worsening of health conditions.
