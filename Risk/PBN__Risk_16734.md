# __Lack of individual room control for cooling may lead to uneven temperature distribution and poor ventilation in certain areas, increasing the risk of airborne disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement zoning and smart ventilation control systems to ensure uniform temperature distribution and adequate ventilation in all areas.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18681.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased likelihood of airborne disease transmission in areas with poor ventilation and temperature control.
