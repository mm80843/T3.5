# __Use of uv light devices for surface disinfection may not reach all surfaces or provide consistent coverage, leading to incomplete disinfection.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Yang_combating_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_505.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular manual inspection and cleaning of areas not reached by uv light devices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12635.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of surface contamination and potential disease transmission.
