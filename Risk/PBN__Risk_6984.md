# __Inaccurate weighting of factors against primary cbe areas may result in misaligned development priorities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheshmehzangi_revisiting_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_437.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilize industry-specific experts to verify the relevance and importance of each factor against primary cbe areas. perform sensitivity analyses to assess the impact of different factor weightings on development priorities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8860.md)

## Name of the risk

Misallocation of resources and efforts towards development changes and paradigm shifts that may not address the most critical factors in primary CBE areas.
