# __Standard-62.1 and standard-90.1 focus on thermal comfort, ventilation, and energy efficiency without considering pandemics, potentially leading to inadequate measures for controlling contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Awada_ten_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_402.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Integration of pandemic-specific design guidelines and operation protocols into existing standards and regulations to address contagious disease transmission in built environments.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6763.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of contagion and disease transmission in built environments due to the lack of proactive pandemic-related design and operation guidelines.
