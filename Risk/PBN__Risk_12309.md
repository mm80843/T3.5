# __Reliance on strava data as a representation of recreational activity may not capture the full spectrum of users, potentially skewing the results.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Venter_urban_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_525.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use multiple data sources and validation methods to cross-check strava data, and consider the limitations of strava user data in interpretations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14225.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate representation of recreational activity levels, leading to flawed analysis and decision-making.
