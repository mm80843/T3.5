# __Increased risk of surface transmission within apartment buildings due to high-touch surfaces and communal spaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Peters_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_612.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increased cleaning and disinfection of high-touch surfaces, closure of communal spaces, and promotion of hygiene practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18485.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of surface transmission of COVID-19 and other contagious diseases, leading to potential outbreaks within buildings.
