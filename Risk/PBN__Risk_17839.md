# __Potential reliance on upper-room germicidal uv guv systems as the primary control measure, leading to neglect of other essential infection control strategies.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Morawska_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_641.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement a comprehensive infection control strategy that includes a combination of ventilation optimization, air filtration, and other proven infection control measures in addition to guv systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19800.md)

## Name of the risk

Neglect of other essential infection control strategies due to overreliance on GUV systems, potentially leading to incomplete protection against contagious diseases.
