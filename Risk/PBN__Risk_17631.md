# __Inadequate research and regulation of air-purifying plants may lead to inconsistent efficacy and potential health risks from plant-related allergens or toxins.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Navaratnam_designing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_635.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough research and establish standards for air-purifying plants. monitor indoor air quality and conduct regular assessments of plant performance.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19589.md)
