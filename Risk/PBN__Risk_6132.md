# __Inefficient use of face shields for blocking transmission of cough-generated aerosol particles, leading to increased risk of virus transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encourage the use of more effective face coverings and masks for source control, and provide guidelines on the appropriate use of face shields in conjunction with other protective measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8004.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced protection against contagious disease transmission from cough-generated aerosol particles.
