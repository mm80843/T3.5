# __Inadequate data privacy and security measures in telemedicine platforms may lead to unauthorized access to sensitive health information and potential exposure of contagious disease status.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sharifi_contributions_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_573.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of strong data security measures, encryption of patient data, regular security audits, and patient education on data privacy.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16621.md)

## Name of the risk

Unauthorized access to health information can lead to breaches of patient confidentiality, stigmatization, and discrimination based on contagious disease status.
