# __Potential risk of transmitting covid-19 through shared browsing interfaces or search interfaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_construction_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_521.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene protocols and use of disinfectants for shared devices. encourage users to use their personal devices for browsing and searching.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13996.md)

## Name of the risk

Increase in the spread of COVID-19 due to potential exposure through shared interfaces.
