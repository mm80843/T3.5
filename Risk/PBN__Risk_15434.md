# __Assuming 80% efficacy of personal protective measures against mosquitos may lead to underestimation of transmission rates and disease spread.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Roosa_general_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_586.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct studies to accurately assess the efficacy of personal protective measures against mosquitos. educate and inform the population about the actual effectiveness of different protective measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17373.md)

## Name of the risk
