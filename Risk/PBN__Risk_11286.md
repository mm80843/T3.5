# __Use of virtual conversational agents and avatars in e-commerce may create a false sense of security regarding disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide clear guidance on health and safety measures, and continuously educate users about the limitations of virtual interactions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13198.md)

## Name of the risk

Without proper precautions, this may lead to reduced adherence to health and safety guidelines, increasing the risk of disease transmission.
