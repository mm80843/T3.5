# __Aerosol transmission via central air supply or drainage system in confined spaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_review_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_478.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance of central air systems, use of air disinfection technologies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11012.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential aerosol transmission of SARS-CoV-2 in indoor environments with central air supply or drainage systems.
