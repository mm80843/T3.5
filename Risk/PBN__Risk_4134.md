# __Potential risk of exposure to infectious diseases in tele-surgery due to the involvement of remote doctors.__

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of strict infection control measures, use of advanced sterilization techniques, and regular testing of remote doctors.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6006.md)
