# __Using a modeling framework to estimate region-specific parameters for epidemic seeding dates, basic reproduction number, and npi effectiveness may lead to inaccuracies in the estimation of these parameters and their impact on epidemic trajectories.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Perez-saez_effect_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_613.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular validation and calibration of the modeling framework using real-time data to ensure accuracy of parameter estimation. incorporation of uncertainty analysis to account for variances in parameter estimates.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18556.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccuracies in parameter estimation could result in ineffective implementation of NPIs, leading to uncontrolled spread of contagious diseases.
