# __Traveling for work-related activities increases the risk of contagious disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Budd_responsible_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_422.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilize video calls and digital means for meetings/events, and limit physical travel to essential purposes only.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7860.md)
