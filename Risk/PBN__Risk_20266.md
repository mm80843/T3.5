# __Poor sanitation and hygiene practices in restrooms and building support areas can facilitate the transmission of infectious agents through surface contact.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kembel_architectural_2014](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_687.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhancing cleaning protocols, promoting proper hand hygiene, and installing touchless fixtures and surfaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22245.md)
