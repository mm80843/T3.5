# __Widespread use of loc-based techniques for viral detection may create false positives or false negatives in diagnostic results.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Barcelo_wastewater-based_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_408.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validation of loc-based techniques with traditional testing methods, regular calibration of detection systems, and continuous training of operators.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7080.md)

## Name of the risk

Misdiagnosis of viral diseases, delayed treatment, and potential spread of contagious viruses due to incorrect diagnosis.
