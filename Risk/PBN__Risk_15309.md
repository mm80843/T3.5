# __Routine exposure to non-liquid petroleum gas fuel linked to acute infections in the lower respiratory tract, including pneumonia and severe pneumonia.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saini_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_583.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promotion of clean fuel usage, establishment of regulations for fuel usage, and education on the health risks of non-clean fuels.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17249.md)

## Name of the risk

High rate of acute lower respiratory tract infections, pneumonia, and severe diseases in affected individuals.
