# __Inadequate home entry area design may contribute to the spread of contagious diseases by failing to provide effective decontamination and hygiene measures.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elrayies_prophylactic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_462.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement proper decontamination and hygiene protocols, including designated entry spaces, sanitation stations, and decontamination chambers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10061.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of virus transmission within the home environment, leading to potential illness and the spread of contagious diseases among inhabitants.
