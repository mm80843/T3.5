# __Narrow control clauses in commercial office tenancy agreements may lead to suboptimal indoor temperature management, potentially impacting occupant comfort and energy efficiency.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Parkinson_continuous_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_615.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Adopt adaptive temperature control strategies based on occupant preferences and behavior, and align indoor temperature setpoints with international thermal comfort standards.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18762.md)

## Name of the risk

Inflexible temperature control measures may result in discomfort for occupants and unnecessary energy consumption, leading to increased environmental and climate impacts.
