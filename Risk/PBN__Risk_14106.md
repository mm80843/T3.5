# __Deployment of secondary research approach from multiple countries increases the risk of introducing infectious disease-related guidelines or standards from high-risk areas.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct a comprehensive review of the guidelines or standards and integrate infectious disease control measures into the design principles.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16034.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential adoption of guidelines or standards that do not account for contagious diseases, leading to increased risk of disease propagation in parking facilities.
