# __Sharing of communal spaces, such as toilets, in households where individuals are advised to isolate at home can increase the risk of virus transmission among family members.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhao_supporting_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_499.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing measures to minimize contact and sharing of communal spaces, such as using dedicated toilets and disinfection protocols.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12335.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher likelihood of intra-household transmission and infection, leading to increased community spread of the virus.
