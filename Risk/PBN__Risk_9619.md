# __Resilience metric that does not consider time to recover, leading to inaccurate assessment__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hosseini_review_2016](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_487.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Modify the resilience metric to incorporate the time to recover, providing a more accurate assessment of system resilience](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11521.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to account for the duration of recovery, potential underestimation of resilience, and inadequate preparedness for extended recovery periods
