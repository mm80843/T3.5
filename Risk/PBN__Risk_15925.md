# __Use of mobile technology for counseling and education may result in misinformation and inadequate guidance on self-isolation and protection from contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Raza_mobile_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_596.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Developing and disseminating accurate and up-to-date educational materials through mobile technology. training healthcare professionals in providing reliable counseling and education through digital platforms.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17867.md)
