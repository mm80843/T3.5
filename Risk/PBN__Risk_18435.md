# __Handmade signs with haphazard appearance may convey unprofessionalism and lack of attention to detail.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Create visually appealing handmade signs or invest in professionally designed signage to convey professionalism and attract customers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20402.md)

## Name of the risk

Customers may perceive the retailer as untrustworthy or disorganized, leading to decreased patronage and negative brand perception.
