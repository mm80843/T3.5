# __Switch from in-person to remote consultation methods in healthcare.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Robbins_covid-19_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_591.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide training for healthcare providers on effective remote consultation practices and ensure clear communication with patients about the limitations of remote consultations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17606.md)

## Name of the risk

Loss of in-person interaction may impact patient-provider relationships and increase the risk of misdiagnosis or missed symptoms.
