# __Reentrainment of exhausted effluents into the hvac system due to engineering problems can result in the spread of contaminants and increase the risk of airborne transmission of diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Nembhard_ventilation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_634.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct regular inspections and maintenance of hvac systems to prevent or identify reentrainment issues. implement engineering changes if necessary to mitigate reentrainment risks.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19535.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher chances of airborne transmission of contaminants and contagious diseases within the building.
