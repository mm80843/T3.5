## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Maugeri_impact_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_654.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Providing mental health support, promoting physical activity, and encouraging social support networks](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20528.md)

## Name of the risk

Psychological effects such as post-traumatic stress symptoms, confusion, and anxiety, as well as adverse health changes such as premature ageing, obesity, cardiovascular vulnerability, muscle atrophy, and decreased aerobic capacity
