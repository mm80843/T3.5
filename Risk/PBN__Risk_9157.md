# __Ineffective water system maintenance leading to potential contamination and transmission of waterborne diseases__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_review_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_478.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular inspection of drainage and water systems, unobstructed uplift ventilation pipe, use of sterilizers and equipment to ensure water safety](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11056.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of waterborne disease transmission, including SARS-CoV-2 if present in water supply
