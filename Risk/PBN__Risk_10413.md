# __Use of improvised breathing aids and devices, such as snood-type masks, without proper filtration and purification systems may lead to inadequate protection against airborne viruses.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhao_supporting_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_499.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement effective air filtration and purification systems in the design of breathing aids, and provide training on proper usage and maintenance to healthcare workers and patients.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12323.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of exposure to contaminated air and inhalation of virus particles, contributing to higher infection rates among users.
