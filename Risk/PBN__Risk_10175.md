# __Inadequate ventilation and confinement of indoor spaces leading to efficient spread of covid-19 particles in buildings.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase outdoor ventilation, improve air filters, maintain hvac systems, use portable air cleaners, adopt ultraviolet germicidal irradiation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12083.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of respiratory diseases transmission and COVID-19 spread in built facilities.
