# __Potential bias in the survey data due to exclusion of individuals over the age of 70 who were strongly advised to stay at home during lockdown.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Corley_home_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_445.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct separate outreach and data collection efforts for the older population, ensuring their experiences are captured and analyzed separately.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9285.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate representation of the impact of lockdown on the older population, leading to skewed conclusions and recommendations.
