# __Exclusion of mental health from the review may neglect the potential mental health benefits of greenspace, impacting overall well-being and quality of life.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate a review of the literature on the association between greenspace and mental health to comprehensively evaluate the health benefits and inform policy decisions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14836.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to account for the mental health benefits of greenspace may lead to incomplete assessments of its overall impact on health outcomes.
