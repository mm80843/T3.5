# __Inclusive design for a diversified range of activities can lead to challenges in maintaining hygiene and preventing disease transmission across different areas of the health club facilities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Developing specific hygiene protocols for each activity area, providing adequate sanitation facilities, and implementing regular cleaning and disinfection schedules.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12071.md)

Increased complexity in managing hygiene and disease prevention efforts across diverse activity areas.
