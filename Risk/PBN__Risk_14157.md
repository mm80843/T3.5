# __Legal issues arising from the complex environment of 5g-enabled smart devices for e-health.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Siriwardhana_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_563.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Extending product liability to cover the correct functioning of network and services in e-health solutions, implementing root-cause analysis techniques, and ensuring compliance with 5g regulations and standards.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16085.md)

Increased liability for manufacturers, service companies, insurers, and potential legal challenges for consumers.
