# __Overreliance on smartwatch data for covid-19 detection may lead to overmedicalization and unnecessary interventions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ates_wearable_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_400.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use smartwatch data as a supplementary tool to clinical diagnosis, emphasize the interpretation of data by qualified healthcare professionals.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6588.md)

## Name of the risk

Unwarranted medical treatments, increased healthcare costs, and potential psychological distress for individuals.
