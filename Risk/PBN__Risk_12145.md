# __Potential spread of indoor microbial pollution due to the use of biofiltration system for degradation of formaldehyde and vocs in the air.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_characterization_2011](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_522.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular monitoring and testing for microbial pollution in the indoor air. use of additional filtration and purification methods to eliminate any potential release of microorganisms.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14060.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of respiratory infections and allergic reactions for building occupants.

## People affected by this risk

* [Building occupants](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_97.md)
* [Especially those with respiratory conditions or allergies](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_648.md)

## Stakeholders who can mitigate this risk

* [Building management and facilities maintenance staff](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_9944.md)

## Technologies linked to the risk

* [Advanced air purification systems with hepa filters to capture microorganisms. uv-c light treatment to sterilize the air](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Technology/PBN__Technology_14285.md)
