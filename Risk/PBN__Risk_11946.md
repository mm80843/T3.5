# __Non-contact interaction activities and virtual community spaces may impact the spatial structure of urban communities and the behavioral activities of residents, potentially leading to the neglect of other influencing factors.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_urban_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_517.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct comprehensive research and data collection to understand the full impact of non-contact interaction activities on urban community service facilities and incorporate the findings into planning and development strategies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13859.md)
