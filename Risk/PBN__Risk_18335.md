# __Inconsistent quantification of sars-cov-2 in sewage samples__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Medema_presence_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_650.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and validation of quantification methods, use of reference materials for qrt-pcr quantification](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20303.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate quantification can lead to misinterpretation of the level of virus presence in the sewage, impacting public health decisions and interventions
