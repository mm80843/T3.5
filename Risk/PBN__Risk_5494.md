# __Contamination risk from unattached polymer chains during film fabrication__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Behzadinasab_surface_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_414.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement thorough rinsing and drying processes to remove unattached polymer chains, and use appropriate waste disposal methods.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7367.md)

## Name of the risk

Presence of unattached polymer chains can lead to contamination of the environment and potentially cause health hazards if inhaled or ingested.
