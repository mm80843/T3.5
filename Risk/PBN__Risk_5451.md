# __Reliance on published literature and studies for uv-c doses and log reductions without independent validation introduces risk of using potentially inaccurate data__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Beggs_upper-room_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_413.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Verify and validate published literature through independent studies and experiments. emphasize peer-reviewed, well-documented research for benchmarking and evaluation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7324.md)

## Name of the risk

Miscalculation of required UV-C doses and log reductions, leading to ineffective viral disinfection
