# __Prominent in-store signs and displays play a role in shaping consumer behavior and prompting particular actions. if used incorrectly, they may inadvertently encourage behaviors that contribute to the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Strategic design and placement of signs to promote health-conscious behaviors, use of clear and consistent messaging on hygiene and safety practices, and regular monitoring of customer behavior.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20427.md)

## Name of the risk

Encouragement of behaviors that contribute to the spread of contagious diseases, such as crowding, inadequate social distancing, or poor hygiene practices.
