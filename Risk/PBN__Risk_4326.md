# __Architects and engineers lacking training in public health may lead to designs with inadequate sunlight access impacting indoor viral communities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide training in public health for architects and engineers engaged in hospital design to ensure adequate sunlight access and minimize indoor viral communities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6197.md)
