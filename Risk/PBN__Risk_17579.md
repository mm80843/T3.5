# __Unclean or inadequate maintenance of ventilation systems can lead to reduced system efficiency and accumulation of contaminants, contributing to indoor air pollution.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Nembhard_ventilation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_634.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and maintenance of ventilation systems, use of high-efficiency filters, and proper disposal of collected contaminants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19538.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased air quality, potential spread of dust, allergens, and microbial contaminants, and compromised respiratory health of occupants.
