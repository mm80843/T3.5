## Mitigations of this risk

* [Developing and implementing clear policies for park management, investing in sanitation and hygiene infrastructure, and providing training for park staff on disease prevention measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17093.md)
