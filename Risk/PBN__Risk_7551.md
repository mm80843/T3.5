# __Dependence on cyclical funding sources, such as seasonal tourism, may lead to financial instability and funding gaps.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cumming_building_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_447.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Diversify funding sources to include long-term, dependable funding and explore alternative revenue streams not tied to seasonal fluctuations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9434.md)

## Name of the risk

Financial instability and uncertain funding for ongoing management and salaries, leading to reduced capacity to respond to contagious disease outbreaks.
