# __Limited applicability of the proposed method to large-scale buildings__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Li_building_2016](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_668.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Research and development of scaling techniques for the proposed method, integration of building design features that facilitate people flow monitoring and occupancy estimation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21208.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to accurately estimate people occupancy in high-rise buildings may result in inadequate emergency preparedness and response to contagious disease outbreaks.
