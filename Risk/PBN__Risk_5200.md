# __Potential risk of undetected sars-cov-2 in wastewater due to limitations in concentration and detection methods.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Barcelo_wastewater-based_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_408.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Standardize and optimize concentration and detection methods to improve sensitivity and accuracy. implement quality control measures to ensure reliable results.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7074.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to detect the virus in wastewater may result in delayed identification of virus spread in communities.
