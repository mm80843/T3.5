# __Inadequate bathroom infrastructure in residential buildings for preventing virus transmission between occupants during quarantine.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rahim_impact_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_601.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Equipping each bedroom with a separate bathroom, ensuring external bathroom access, and implementing proper sewage containment measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18043.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of virus transmission due to shared bathroom use and lack of separate facilities for each bedroom.
