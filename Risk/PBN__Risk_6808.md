# __Rise in e-learning and use of iot in smart learning increases the risk of cyber attacks and data breaches.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strong cybersecurity measures, conduct regular security audits, and provide training on cybersecurity best practices for educators and students.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8683.md)

Potential disruption of e-learning platforms, unauthorized access to student and teacher data, and compromise of educational resources.
