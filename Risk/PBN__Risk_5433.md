# __Whole-room uv-c system effectiveness against rna viruses like mhv-a59, mers-cov, and sars-cov.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bedell_efficacy_2016](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_412.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Highlight that uv-c disinfection should be an adjunct to standard terminal cleaning of hard surfaces, not a replacement for it.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7306.md)

## Name of the risk

Overreliance on UV-C disinfection without proper cleaning may lead to inadequate control of disease spread.
