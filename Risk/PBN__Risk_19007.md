# __Robots in healthcare could transmit contagious diseases if not properly sanitized between patients.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Madurai_elavarasan_restructured_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_664.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict sanitation protocols for robots and use of antimicrobial materials. regular cleaning and disinfection of robots between patient interactions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20979.md)

## Name of the risk
