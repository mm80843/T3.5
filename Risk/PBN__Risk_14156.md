# __Delay or prevention of timely healthcare access due to technical solutions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Siriwardhana_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_563.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensuring that technical solutions do not hinder timely healthcare access, conducting thorough testing and validation of the solutions, and obtaining legal advice on the impact of the solutions on healthcare access.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16084.md)

## Name of the risk

Violation of the right to access healthcare, potential wrong diagnosis or treatment, and infringement of fundamental rights.
