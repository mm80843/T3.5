# __Reliance on large training datasets for people detection__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rezaei_deepsocial_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_593.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regularly update and expand the training datasets to encompass diverse demographics, and use transfer learning to adapt the system to new environments](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17716.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccuracy in detecting and tracking people who do not fit the dataset's gender and age categories, leading to underreporting or missed detection
