# __Inaccurate readings from wearable sensors may lead to misdiagnosis or delayed diagnosis of diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and verification of wearable sensor technology, combined with medical professional interpretation of data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8624.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for mismanagement of health conditions and delayed treatment, leading to worsened health outcomes.
