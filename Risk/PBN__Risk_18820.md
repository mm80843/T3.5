# __Decrease in in-person surveys during the confinement may lead to a loss of data from individuals who are unable to participate in online surveys.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Marconi_green_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_659.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement alternative data collection methods, such as telephone surveys or mail-in questionnaires, to reach individuals without internet access.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20791.md)

## Name of the risk

Inadequate representation of individuals who do not have access to online surveys, leading to biased results and exclusion of certain demographic groups.

## People affected by this risk

* [Individuals without internet access or online survey participation capabilities](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_14676.md)

## Stakeholders who can mitigate this risk

* [Research team or survey administrators](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_14675.md)

## Technologies linked to the risk
