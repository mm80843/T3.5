# __Descriptive research involving on-site measurements and observations of a parking building increases the risk of exposure to infectious diseases for the researchers and personnel involved.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict health and safety protocols for on-site research activities, including the use of personal protective equipment ppe and adherence to infection control measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16035.md)
