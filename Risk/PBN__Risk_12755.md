# __Arranging objects in circular or elliptic rooms may create congestion and limit airflow, increasing the risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ugail_social_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_534.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing ventilation systems and airflow optimization strategies to improve air circulation and reduce congestion. enforcing strict occupancy limits to prevent overcrowding in circular or elliptic rooms.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14674.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission and outbreak due to limited airflow and congestion in crowded circular or elliptic rooms.
