# __Pseudonymous identity information may pose risks to patient privacy and confidentiality.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Christodoulou_health_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_439.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of additional privacy-preserving techniques such as zero-knowledge proofs, continuous monitoring of identity systems, and strict data access controls.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8917.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential exposure of patient identities and the possibility of de-anonymization, leading to privacy breaches and discrimination.
