# __Role of cloud, edge, and fog computing in telehealth monitoring systems may pose security and privacy risks for the sensitive health data transmitted and stored.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of robust encryption and security measures, compliance with data protection regulations, regular security audits and updates, user awareness and training on data security best practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8633.md)

## Name of the risk

Unauthorized access, breaches, or leaks of sensitive health data, compromising patient privacy and confidentiality.
