# __Lack of communication with firms that provide contract or temporary workers about the importance of sick employees staying home, leading to potential spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Assaad_guidelines_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_399.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Engage in proactive communication with firms about the importance of sick employees staying home and encourage the development of nonpunitive leave policies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6513.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of contagion within the construction workforce and potential transmission of diseases to other workers and customers.
