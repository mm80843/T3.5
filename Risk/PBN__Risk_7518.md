# __Digital gaps and limitations in the use of internet forms leading to potential exclusion of certain demographics and populations.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cuerdo-vilches_mixed_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_446.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide alternative data collection methods , telephone surveys, in-person interviews for those with limited internet access, and ensure accessibility features in online forms for diverse user groups.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9399.md)

## Name of the risk

Underrepresentation of specific demographics and populations in the study, limiting the generalizability of findings and the ability to address their needs.
