# __Interactive live broadcast of tourist attractions may contribute to a false sense of security and encourage travel, potentially leading to the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote responsible travel behavior, provide accurate information about the risks of congregation, and implement crowd control measures at tourist attractions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5985.md)
