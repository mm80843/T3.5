# __Simultaneous heating and cooling limitations__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement advanced hvac control systems, optimize heating and cooling schedules, and improve insulation and building envelope.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18670.md)

## Name of the risk

Inefficient use of energy, discomfort for occupants, and potential for uneven temperature distribution throughout the building.
