## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of capacity limits, reservation systems, and contactless payment options for pois. promotion of outdoor and open-air activities to disperse gatherings and reduce crowding.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12621.md)

## Name of the risk

Shifts in population movements and gatherings leading to potential concentration of people in specific areas, increasing the risk of disease transmission. Disruption of traditional urban activity patterns.
