# __Commuting environments with high population density and close contact can facilitate the spread of pandemics through public transportation systems and shared commuting spaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Yang_urban_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_504.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhancing sanitation and hygiene measures in public transportation, reducing crowding through staggered commuting hours, and promoting telecommuting options.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12613.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of exposure and transmission of contagious diseases during commuting, leading to higher infection rates in urban areas.
