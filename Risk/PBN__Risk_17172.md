# __Differential impact of job retention schemes on immigrant workers, leading to potential overrepresentation in certain sectors.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Oecd_what_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_626.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure fair and equal access to job retention schemes for all workers, monitor sector-specific distribution of participants, and provide additional support for at-risk sectors.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19126.md)

## Name of the risk

Unequal distribution of job retention benefits and potential clustering of immigrant workers in specific industries, increasing the risk of disease transmission in those sectors.
