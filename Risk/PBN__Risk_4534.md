# __Using a likert scale to measure agreement may result in subjective interpretation and response bias.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Asfour_housing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_398.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure clear and specific wording of scale points, provide training to respondents on scale interpretation, and use complementary qualitative methods for validation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6408.md)
