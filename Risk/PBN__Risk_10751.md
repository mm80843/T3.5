# __Incomplete quantification of viral titers due to lack of investigation on recovery rates for each step in the process.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wu_sars-cov-2_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_507.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough investigation of recovery rates for each step in the process and use appropriate reference standards for quantification.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12662.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate viral titer quantification could lead to incorrect assessments of contamination levels and disease risk.
