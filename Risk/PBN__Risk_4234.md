# __Sars-cov-2 particles can remain and live on fomites for a period varying from a few hours to five days, depending on the materials.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and disinfection of fomites, promoting good hygiene practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6105.md)
