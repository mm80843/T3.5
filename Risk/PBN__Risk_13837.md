# __Inadequate airflow and ventilation in the built environment leading to the potential spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_residential_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_557.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper ventilation and airflow systems are in place to reduce the concentration of airborne pathogens. follow guidelines for external venting and airflow control to minimize the risk of indoor transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15762.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne transmission of contagious diseases within the confined space, leading to higher infection rates.
