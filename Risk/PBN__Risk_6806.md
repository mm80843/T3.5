# __Clinical staff using pulse oximeters can contribute to the spread of contagious diseases if not properly sanitized between uses.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper sanitization of pulse oximeters between uses, and provide training on proper use and sanitation procedures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8681.md)

## Name of the risk

Increase the risk of spreading contagious diseases among clinical staff and patients.
