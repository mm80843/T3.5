# __Handling of biological samples and reagents in the laboratory increases the risk of exposure to contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ahmed_first_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_383.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Strict adherence to biosafety protocols, use of personal protective equipment, regular disinfection of laboratory surfaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5531.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of contagious diseases among laboratory personnel and the general population.
