# __5g-enabled pandemic control and prevention may lead to increased reliance on technology and decreased human interaction, potentially impacting mental and emotional well-being.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encourage balanced use of technology and human interaction, provide mental health support services, promote physical activity and in-person socialization.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5940.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced human interaction and increased reliance on technology can lead to social isolation and negative mental health outcomes.
