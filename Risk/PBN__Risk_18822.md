# __Heterogeneous distribution of ugss causing large areas to be further than a ten minutes walking distance, making them considered unavailable.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Marconi_green_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_659.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improve the distribution and accessibility of ugss through urban planning and infrastructure development to encourage physical activity.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20793.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased accessibility to UGSs may lead to reduced physical activity and increased sedentary behavior, impacting public health.
