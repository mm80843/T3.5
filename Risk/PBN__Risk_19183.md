# __Tourists avoiding public transport due to fear of contracting illnesses, leading to decreased usage of public transport and potential overcrowding of roads and highways.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Li_seeing_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_667.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhance sanitation and hygiene measures on public transport, promote alternative transport modes such as cycling and walking, and improve public transport infrastructure and services.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21157.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased usage of public transport may lead to increased traffic congestion, air pollution, and road accidents.
