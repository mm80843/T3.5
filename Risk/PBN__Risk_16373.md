# __Expansion of the digital twin framework to a bigger case may involve the introduction of new smartphones and badges, leading to potential risks of contamination and disease spread.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pilati_digital_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_609.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Thorough sanitization of new devices, strict guidelines for device handling, limiting physical interaction during deployment, and regular health screening for operators involved in the expansion.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18315.md)
