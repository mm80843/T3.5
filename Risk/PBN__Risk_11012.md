# __Former hunters, now working as wildlife keepers, may still pose a risk of disease transmission due to their previous hunting activities and close contact with wildlife.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide education and training on disease prevention and safe wildlife handling practices, conduct regular health screenings for wildlife keepers, and promote the use of personal protective equipment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12922.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of zoonotic disease transmission to wildlife keepers and the community.
