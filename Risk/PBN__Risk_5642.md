# __Shared common spaces for socialization may become hotspots for disease transmission if not properly managed and maintained.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bojovic_towards_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_416.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and disinfection of common spaces, implementation of capacity limits and social distancing measures, and promotion of virtual socialization options.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7515.md)

## Name of the risk
