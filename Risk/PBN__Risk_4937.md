# __Balconies as places for communication and social activities may facilitate the spread of contagious diseases between neighbors and residents.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aydin_questioning_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_403.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing guidelines for social distancing and hygiene practices in balcony usage. encouraging the use of masks and limiting the number of people gathering on balconies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6810.md)

## Name of the risk

Unmitigated spread of contagious diseases can lead to increased illness and transmission rates within the community.
