# __Lack of access to virtual reality technology in low-income countries due to high cost and computing power requirement.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mbunge_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_653.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Investment in affordable virtual reality devices, improvement of supporting infrastructure, and development of alternative virtual reality technologies that require lower computing power.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20434.md)

## Name of the risk

Limited access to virtual reality technology hinders medical education and training in low-income countries, leading to disparities in healthcare knowledge and skills.
