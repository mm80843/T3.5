# __Inadequate positioning of uv tubes for germicidal sources resulting in ineffective disinfection of ventilation ducts and flowing air.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Vranay_adaptation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_524.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct a thorough analysis to determine the optimal positioning of uv tubes and ensure effective disinfection.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14150.md)
