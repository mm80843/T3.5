# __Potential for transmission of contagious diseases through physical contact with natural elements in biophilic design buildings.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhong_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_498.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and maintenance of natural elements, implementation of touch-free technologies, and education on hygiene practices for building occupants.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12281.md)

## Name of the risk

Spread of contagious diseases and increased risk of infection among building occupants.
