# __Use of ai and machine learning to monitor public spaces may lead to increased reliance on surveillance and raise ethical concerns about privacy and consent.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rezaei_deepsocial_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_593.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement transparent and accountable ai systems, conduct active public engagement on surveillance practices, and enforce strict data protection laws.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17725.md)

## Name of the risk

Erosion of privacy, potential misuse of surveillance data, and public distrust of technology-based monitoring.
