# __Use of high fidelity mobile holographic capability may lead to increased physical interaction and close proximity among individuals, potentially facilitating the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Allam_future_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_390.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Limiting physical interaction by using remote collaboration tools and promoting hygiene and social distancing measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5836.md)

Unmitigated use of this technology could contribute to the transmission of contagious diseases and increase the risk of outbreaks in urban areas.
