# __The use of workplace contact tracing wearables may create a sense of surveillance and distrust among employees.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Chamola_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_430.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Transparent communication about the purpose of the device, emphasizing its role in maintaining a safe workplace environment rather than surveillance.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8510.md)
