# __Development and survival of bacteria and mold in ventilation systems and air filters.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dalessandro_covid-19_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_448.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and replacement of air filters, humidity control to prevent excessive moisture, use of antimicrobial coatings for ventilation components.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9510.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of respiratory diseases and allergies, as well as potential transmission of airborne contaminants.
