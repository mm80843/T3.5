# __Potential risk of contagious disease transmission through interaction with neighbors in communal green spaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Corley_home_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_445.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict social distancing measures and guidelines for interaction in communal green spaces. promote the use of face masks and hand hygiene.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9314.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of spreading contagious diseases, such as COVID-19, among older adults in communal green spaces.

## People affected by this risk

* [Older adults >60 years living in areas with communal green spaces](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_6214.md)
