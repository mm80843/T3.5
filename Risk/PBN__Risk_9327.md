# __Less stringent approach to reviewing the literature, such as rapid reviews, leading to scope for heterogeneity in the employment of rapid reviews__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Harris_respiratory_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_482.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establishing clear review protocols, promoting transparency and methodological rigor in rapid review processes, and ensuring diverse perspectives and expertise are involved.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11227.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for inconsistencies and biases in the rapid review process, affecting the reliability of the synthesized knowledge for policy decision-making.
