# __Lack of guidelines for safe implementation of vr and ar technologies in public places for security and patrol purposes.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Subramanian_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_556.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop and disseminate guidelines for the safe and responsible use of vr and ar technologies in public places, with a focus on disease transmission prevention.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15715.md)

## Name of the risk

Inappropriate use of these technologies may contribute to the spread of contagious diseases in public areas.
