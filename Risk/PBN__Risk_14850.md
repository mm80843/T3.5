# __Examination of the effect of implementing prevention methods on different types of buildings may lead to the spread of contagious diseases if the methods are not effective or if the buildings are not properly managed.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sarvari_global_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_576.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Thorough testing and validation of the effectiveness of the prevention methods, proper implementation and management of control measures, and regular monitoring of building conditions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16786.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of contagious diseases spreading and outbreaks in buildings of varying risk categories.
