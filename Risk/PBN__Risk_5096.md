# __Sars-cov-2 can remain infectious on surfaces for 1-7 days, potentially leading to surface-based transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Azuma_environmental_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_406.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Frequent cleaning and disinfection of frequently touched surfaces can reduce the risk of surface-based transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6968.md)

## Name of the risk

Prolonged viability of SARS-CoV-2 on surfaces increases the risk of transmission through contact with contaminated surfaces.
