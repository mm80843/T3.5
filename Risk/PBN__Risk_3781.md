# __Gas-based ovens in kuwait contribute to indoor air quality challenges and may pose a risk if not properly ventilated.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Al-mumin_occupants_2003](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_386.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper ventilation in kitchen areas with gas ovens, conduct regular maintenance of oven exhaust systems, and educate occupants on indoor air quality and ventilation best practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5654.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased indoor air pollution due to the operation of gas ovens, leading to respiratory issues and potential aggravation of existing health conditions.

## People affected by this risk

* [Occupants in the building who are exposed to indoor air pollution from gas oven operation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_3051.md)
