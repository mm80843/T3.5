# __Generation of respiratory aerosols from medical ventilators increases the risk of occupationally acquired infection among healthcare personnel.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_sars-cov-2_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_518.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement measures to minimize aerosol generation, improve ventilation systems, and provide adequate personal protective equipment for healthcare personnel.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13890.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of healthcare personnel acquiring infections from respiratory aerosols generated by medical ventilators.

## People affected by this risk

* [Healthcare personnel working with medical ventilators](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_9810.md)
