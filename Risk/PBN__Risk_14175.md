# __Scalability and resource demand challenges for 5g-enabled healthcare applications, including contact tracing and monitoring.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Siriwardhana_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_563.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Deploy mec servers at 5g base stations, allocate separate network slices for healthcare data, and continuously assess and optimize resource allocation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16103.md)

## Name of the risk

Inadequate scalability may lead to system overload, data processing delays, and compromised quality of service for healthcare applications.
