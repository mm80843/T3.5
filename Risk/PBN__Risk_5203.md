# __Utilization of advanced rna detection methods like crispr and dpcr for virus detection and quantification presents the risk of misinterpretation or inadequate understanding of the results compared to traditional methods like rt-qpcr.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Barcelo_wastewater-based_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_408.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide training and education on the proper interpretation and understanding of results from advanced rna detection methods. validate and compare results with traditional methods to ensure accuracy and reliability.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7077.md)

## Name of the risk

Misinterpretation or inadequate understanding of results from advanced RNA detection methods could lead to incorrect public health decisions and inappropriate containment measures for contagious diseases.
