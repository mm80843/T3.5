# __High density of shoppers in supermarkets during a pandemic__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_designing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_558.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Formal inclusion of half aisles or readily removable shelving to create half aisles. increase aisle spacing at the expense of open design areas. consider reducing the density of shoppers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15794.md)
* [Inclusion of half aisles, removable shelving, and increased aisle spacing in supermarket designs](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15792.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission and outbreaks due to crowded shopping spaces.
