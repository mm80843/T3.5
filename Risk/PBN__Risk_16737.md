# __Less than 50% of relevant heating loads are metered__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement metering for accurate measurement of heating loads and performance.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18684.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to accurately measure heating system performance and identify inefficiencies, leading to potential health risks from poor indoor air quality.
