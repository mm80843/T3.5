# __Narrow and long shopping aisles in supermarkets can lead to congestion and increased close contact between shoppers.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_designing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_558.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Widening shopping aisles to facilitate social distancing, promoting online ordering with delivery options, and reevaluating product placement strategies to reduce congestion.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15808.md)

## Name of the risk

Elevated risk of disease transmission within supermarket environments, potentially leading to higher infection rates among customers and staff.
