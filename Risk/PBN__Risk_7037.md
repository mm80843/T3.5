# __Possible risk of data tampering or corruption in the decentralized storage and exchange of medical information using blockchain technology.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Christodoulou_health_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_439.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement data verification mechanisms, cryptographic hashing of medical records, and regular integrity checks of stored information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8915.md)
