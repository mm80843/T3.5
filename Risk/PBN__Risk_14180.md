# __Uav-based monitoring of covid-19 patients may raise concerns regarding personal privacy and consent for data collection and surveillance.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Siriwardhana_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_563.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Obtaining explicit consent from the individuals being monitored, establishing clear guidelines for data collection and usage, and ensuring compliance with privacy regulations and ethical standards.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16108.md)

## Name of the risk

Invasion of privacy and potential objections from individuals being monitored, leading to legal and ethical challenges related to data collection, surveillance, and consent for UAV-based monitoring.
