# __Interference from devices using 2.4 ghz radio frequencies can reduce wi-fi speed or cause loss of internet connection.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Raza_mobile_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_596.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use 5 ghz wireless frequency to reduce interference, or reposition router and devices to minimize interference.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17924.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced speed or loss of internet connection can disrupt communication and access to information.
