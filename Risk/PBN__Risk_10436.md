# __Inadequate fluid barrier and air filtration in non-fda-cleared 3d-printed ppe devices.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhao_supporting_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_499.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Testing and certification of 3d-printed ppe devices for fluid barrier and air filtration, adherence to fda guidelines for ppe manufacturing.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12346.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of exposure to airborne diseases due to inadequate protection from the PPE.
