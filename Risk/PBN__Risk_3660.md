# __Risk of cross-contamination and infection spread during primer sequence removal, read trimming, and data analysis processes.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ahmed_first_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_383.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validation of bioinformatics pipelines, use of quality control measures, strict adherence to bioinformatics best practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5533.md)

## Name of the risk

Introduction of errors in data analysis, potential misinterpretation of results, and risk of exposure to contagious diseases.
