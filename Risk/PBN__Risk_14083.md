# __Inadequate interior finishing materials and lack of special installations in the car parking building may lead to poor hygiene conditions, increasing the risk of surface contamination and disease spread.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Upgrade interior finishing materials to easily cleanable surfaces, install hygiene and sanitation stations, and implement regular cleaning and maintenance schedules.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16011.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher potential for surface-based disease transmission and compromised hygiene.
