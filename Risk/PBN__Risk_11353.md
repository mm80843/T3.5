# __Frequent change in customer preferences during turbulent environments may lead to challenges in maintaining food safety standards and hygiene practices.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct regular assessments of customer preferences and adapt food safety protocols accordingly, invest in technology for real-time monitoring of food safety measures, and provide continuous training to staff on hygiene and sanitation best practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13265.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential increase in foodborne illnesses and contamination due to fluctuating demand and supply dynamics.
