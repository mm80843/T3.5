# __Close collaboration and physical interaction between municipal staff, researchers, and project partners may increase the risk of contagious disease spread.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Geraldi_addressing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_475.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict health and safety protocols, including regular testing, temperature checks, and sanitation measures. limit in-person interactions and prioritize virtual collaboration where possible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10885.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential outbreak of contagious diseases among project participants, leading to illness and project disruption.
