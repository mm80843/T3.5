# __Reliance on external data sources for wnf case data and temperature anomalies may introduce inaccuracies and inconsistencies.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Semenza_prototype_2015](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_575.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Verify the reliability and consistency of data sources before using them in the analysis. use multiple sources for validation where possible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16734.md)

Inaccurate data could lead to misinterpretation of risk factors and incorrect mitigation strategies.
