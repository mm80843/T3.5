# __Limited access to natural ventilation due to reduced outdoor air supply__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_characterization_2011](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_522.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Installation of mechanical ventilation systems, use of air filtering technologies, and regular maintenance of hvac systems](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14056.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced ability to dilute indoor air pollutants and microorganisms, leading to poor indoor air quality
