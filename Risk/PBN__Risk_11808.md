# __The use of filtration equipment may not effectively remove all contaminants, including contagious disease particles.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Westhaus_detection_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_514.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance and calibration of filtration equipment, combined with the use of additional treatment methods such as uv disinfection.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13721.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential contamination of water with contagious disease particles, leading to increased risk of disease transmission.
