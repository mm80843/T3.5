# __Inequality in access to housing and its impact on health outcomes creates a social gradient that may contribute to differential exposure to contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Gurney_dangerous_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_479.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
