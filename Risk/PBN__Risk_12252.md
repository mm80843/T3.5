# __Decreasing efficiency of uv-c system due to lamp degradation, air temperature, dust pollution, and reflective surface degradation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Vranay_adaptation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_524.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance, cleaning, and replacement of uv-c lamps. control of air temperature and dust pollution. monitoring and maintenance of reflective surfaces in ventilation ducts.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14168.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced effectiveness in eliminating or lowering the risk of contagious diseases in the built environment.
