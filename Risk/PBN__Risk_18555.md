# __Physical activity may ameliorate pathological outcomes of upper respiratory tract infections urti , including covid-19, by reducing excessive local inflammation and inducing the secretion of anti-inflammatory cytokines.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Maugeri_impact_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_654.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote regular physical activity to reduce the risk of excessive inflammation and cytokine activity.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20524.md)

## Name of the risk

If physical activity is not promoted, there may be an increased risk of excessive inflammation and prolonged T helper type 1 (Th1) cell population activity, leading to cell damage and necrosis.
