# __Inadequate circadian regulation due to poor design of bedrooms, leading to negative impacts on sleep quality and overall health.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Peters_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_612.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporating designs that maximize natural daylight exposure, provide options for cross ventilation, and support thermal comfort in bedrooms.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18506.md)
