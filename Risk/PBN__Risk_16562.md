# __Unclear optimal light levels for each room can lead to dissatisfaction with lighting conditions, impacting resident well-being.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Peters_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_612.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct research to determine optimal light levels for each room, considering location, climate, and cultural preferences. additionally, provide flexible lighting solutions for residents to customize their environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18507.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased resident satisfaction and well-being, potential negative impact on mental health.
