# __Using questionnaire surveys without proper safeguards can result in the transmission of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Gamil_impact_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_472.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Offer online survey options, implement strict hygiene protocols for paper-based surveys, and ensure proper sanitation of survey materials.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10781.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of spreading contagious diseases among construction practitioners participating in the survey.
