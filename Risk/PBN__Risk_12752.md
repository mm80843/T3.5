# __Imbalanced air distribution due to the positioning of fans and air-conditioners leading to stagnant air pockets.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ugail_social_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_534.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular airflow testing and adjustment of fan and air-conditioner positions to ensure even air distribution.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14671.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne disease transmission due to poor air circulation.
