# __Nurses working long hours in isolation facilities__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hossny_management_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_488.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict scheduling and rest policies, provide psychological support, and ensure regular breaks.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11603.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of fatigue and burnout, leading to decreased effectiveness in patient care and increased risk of errors.
