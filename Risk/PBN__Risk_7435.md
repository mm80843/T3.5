# __Risk of vitamin d deficiency among older scottish adults due to decreased sunlight exposure.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Corley_home_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_445.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encourage outdoor activities and safe exposure to sunlight while following public health guidelines. provide vitamin d supplements where necessary.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9315.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher prevalence of vitamin D deficiency, which can affect overall health and immune function in older adults.
