# __Inadequate filtration techniques for removing pathogens like sars-cov-2 from shared air environments.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dietz_2019_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_454.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular inspection and maintenance of filtration systems to ensure proper sealing and functioning.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9715.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased transmission potential of contagious diseases such as SARS-CoV-2 due to gaps in filter edges.

## People affected by this risk

* [Building occupants and healthcare staff in hospitals with filtering systems](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_6523.md)

## Stakeholders who can mitigate this risk

* [And healthcare facility management](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_80.md)

* [Facility managers](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_6524.md)

## Technologies linked to the risk
