# __Contamination of the experimental chamber by microorganisms due to inadequate filtration or backflow prevention.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Walker_effect_2007](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_523.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of high-efficiency particulate air hepa filters and periodic maintenance of filtration systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14129.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of exposure to harmful microorganisms and potential contamination of the environment.
