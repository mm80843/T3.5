# __Shift to unhealthy processed food consumption due to lack of nutritional guidelines for food parcels to the poor.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Oni_implications_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_623.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement nutritional guidelines for food parcels and educate on the importance of healthy eating. provide access to fresh food supplies for lower-income households.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19069.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of malnutrition and chronic conditions such as obesity, diabetes, and cardiovascular disease.
