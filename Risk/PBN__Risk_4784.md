# __Limited use of physiological/psychometric measurements for occupant health assessments__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Awada_occupant_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_401.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Invest in cost-effective measurement tools, provide training on data collection and analysis, and encourage the use of both subjective and objective health assessment methods](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6657.md)

## Name of the risk

Missed opportunities to capture accurate health data, limited understanding of occupant health and comfort, and potential neglect of important health indicators
