# __Handling sewage samples without proper pasteurization increases the risk of exposure to live viruses.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wu_sars-cov-2_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_507.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use adequate pasteurization protocols to inactivate viruses before handling samples, and strictly adhere to biosecurity guidelines.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12674.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential transmission of live viruses from sewage samples to individuals, leading to infection.
