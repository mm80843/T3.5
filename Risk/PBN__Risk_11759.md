# __Risk of missed exposure and contact tracing due to the absence of mobile phones or mobile service.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Whitelaw_applications_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_513.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Public health education on the importance of carrying mobile phones for contact tracing, alternative contact tracing methods for individuals without access to mobile phones, and increased support for mobile phone access.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13671.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased difficulty in identifying and notifying contacts of infected individuals, potentially leading to higher transmission rates and more severe COVID-19 outbreaks.
