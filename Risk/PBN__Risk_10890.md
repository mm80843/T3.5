# __Use of chemical-based vector control tools may lead to environmental contamination and harm non-target species.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wilson_importance_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_510.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use integrated vector management strategies that involve a combination of methods to reduce reliance on chemical-based tools, conduct environmental impact assessments, and implement targeted application techniques.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12801.md)

Environmental contamination and harm to non-target species can result in ecosystem disruption and loss of biodiversity.
