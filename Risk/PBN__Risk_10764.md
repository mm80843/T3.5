# __Testing and quantifying viral titers in wastewater samples involves the risk of accidental exposure to sars-cov-2 virus.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wu_sars-cov-2_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_507.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict handling and testing protocols, use appropriate personal protective equipment, and follow containment procedures to minimize the risk of exposure.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12675.md)
