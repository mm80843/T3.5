# __The coexistence of informal and professional signs may create confusion and uncertainty for individuals navigating retail environments, leading to potential violations of social distancing guidelines.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establish clear guidelines for the use of signage and remove any conflicting or redundant signage from the retail environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20384.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of close contact and non-compliance with social distancing protocols, contributing to disease transmission.
