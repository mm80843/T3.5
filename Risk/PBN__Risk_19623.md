# __Handling of pcr reagents and samples in a laboratory setting poses a risk of contamination and spread of infectious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [La_rosa_first_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_675.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Strict adherence to laboratory protocols, use of personal protective equipment, and regular decontamination of work surfaces and equipment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21601.md)

## Name of the risk

Uncontrolled contamination can lead to the spread of infectious diseases within the laboratory and to individuals handling the samples.
