# __Decreased effectiveness of uvgi system when fixtures are placed on one side of the room.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Memarzadeh_applications_2010](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_645.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Install upper-room uvgi systems to provide the most uniform uvgi distribution in the upper air possible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20057.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced ability to kill or inactivate microorganisms, leading to increased risk of disease transmission.
