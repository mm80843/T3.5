# __Spread of contagious diseases through close contact during podcast interviews and broadcasting activities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rogers_city_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_589.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conducting interviews in well-ventilated spaces, encouraging vaccination, and implementing virtual interviewing options. educating staff and guests about proper hygiene practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17545.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of infecting podcast guests and hosts, potentially leading to widespread transmission of the disease among the targeted audience and the wider community.
