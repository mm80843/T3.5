# __Limited space and lack of dedicated entry zones for managing outdoor to indoor transitions may lead to an increased risk of bringing contaminants into the home.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elrayies_prophylactic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_462.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Creating designated pre-entry zones and post-entry zones with facilities for removing outdoor footwear and clothing.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10065.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher likelihood of outdoor contaminants being tracked into the home, contributing to potential disease transmission.
