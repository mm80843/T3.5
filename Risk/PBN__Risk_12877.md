# __Walking or running in a synthetic environment may convey fewer health benefits compared to a natural environment, potentially impacting public health.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote and encourage access to natural environments for physical activity, and provide education on the benefits of green exercise.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14795.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced physical and mental health benefits for individuals who engage in physical activity in synthetic environments.
