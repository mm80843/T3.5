# __Potential for inadequate disinfection due to lack of control over the inactivation process and moisture content__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_disinfection_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_520.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict control measures for inactivation process and moisture content, and evaluate advanced microwave technologies for biotherapeutic product drying](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13955.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of pathogens surviving disinfection and spreading infectious diseases
