# __Misinformation spread through smart city platforms and technologies may exacerbate the spread of pandemics and hinder control efforts.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sharifi_contributions_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_573.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement content moderation and fact-checking measures on smart city platforms, educate users about the risks of misinformation, and promote transparent communication from official health authorities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16585.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased confusion, panic, and resistance to public health measures due to misinformation, leading to greater challenges in controlling the spread of pandemics.
