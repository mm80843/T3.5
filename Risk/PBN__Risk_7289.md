# __Relying on a government database for hospitalization data introduces a risk of data accuracy and integrity issues.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Collin_using_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_443.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular data validation and verification processes, data quality checks, and collaboration with healthcare authorities to ensure data accuracy and completeness.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9170.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate or incomplete hospitalization data can lead to incorrect assessments of the COVID-19 epidemic and its impact on the healthcare system.
