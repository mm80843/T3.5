# __Potential risks of using uv light in disinfection, including human exposure to hazardous uv radiation and incomplete disinfection of wastewater.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_disinfection_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_520.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of safety protocols to minimize human exposure to uv radiation and regular maintenance of uv disinfection systems to ensure effectiveness.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13970.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of UV-induced health issues and potential for incomplete disinfection, leading to waterborne disease transmission.
