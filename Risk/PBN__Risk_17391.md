# __Conducting an online survey may lead to a biased sample composition, potentially skewing the data analysis and conclusions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Noszczyk_impact_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_630.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement targeted outreach strategies to reach underrepresented age groups and conduct supplementary in-person surveys to validate online responses.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19346.md)

## Name of the risk

Incorrect conclusions and recommendations based on biased survey data may negatively impact public health interventions and policies related to contagious diseases.
