# __Inclusion of additional sensors could lead to increased risk of contamination and spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ates_wearable_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_400.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular sanitation and cleaning of sensors, as well as strict hygiene practices when handling or using the sensors.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6592.md)

## Name of the risk

Unmitigated, this could result in increased transmission of contagious diseases within the population.
