# __Unknown uv susceptibility of pathogenic viral aerosols under varying relative humidity rh conditions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Walker_effect_2007](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_523.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct studies to evaluate the uv susceptibility of pathogenic viral aerosols at different rh levels and develop guidelines for uv disinfection in environments with fluctuating rh.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14137.md)

## Name of the risk

Lack of understanding about how RH affects the UV susceptibility of pathogenic viral aerosols.
