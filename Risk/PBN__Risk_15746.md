# __Utilizing a transfer learning approach with pre-trained models may introduce biases and assumptions from the source dataset microsoft coco into the human detection model, leading to biased predictions and potential fairness issues.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rezaei_deepsocial_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_593.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Careful examination of the source dataset for biases, fairness-aware training techniques such as adversarial debiasing, and fairness evaluation metrics to detect and mitigate biased predictions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17687.md)

## Name of the risk

Biased predictions, fairness and ethical concerns, and misrepresentation of certain demographic groups in the human detection model's output.
