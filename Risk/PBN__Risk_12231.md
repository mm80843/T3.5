# __Use of germicidal emitters or sources may lead to the production of harmful by-products or chemicals.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Vranay_adaptation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_524.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular monitoring and maintenance of germicidal emitters to ensure they are functioning properly. use of air purification technologies to remove harmful by-products from the air.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14147.md)

## Name of the risk

Exposure to harmful by-products or chemicals can pose health risks to occupants and maintenance personnel.
