# __High concentration of pharmaceutical products in wastewater due to increased demand, posing challenges for old age wastewater treatment plants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Matheri_water_2023](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_655.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Upgrade wastewater treatment plants to handle high-concentration pharmaceutical wastewater, implement advanced treatment processes, and monitor effluent for pharmaceutical residues.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20584.md)
