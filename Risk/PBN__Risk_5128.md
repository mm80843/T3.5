# __Inadequate air filtration in hvac systems__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Azuma_environmental_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_406.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Upgrade to high-efficiency air filters, use ultraviolet germicidal irradiation, and perform regular maintenance](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7000.md)
