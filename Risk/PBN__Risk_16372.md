# __Testing mobile application in a smaller clinic may result in potential exposure to contagious diseases for the operators and patients.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pilati_digital_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_609.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene protocols, use protective gear, and conduct regular testing for operators and patients. limit the number of people involved in testing.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18314.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of contagious diseases among operators and patients, leading to increased health risks and potential outbreak.
