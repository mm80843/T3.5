# __Dependence on estimation methods for building occupancy__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Li_building_2016](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_668.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validation of estimation methods through comparison with ground truth data, continuous monitoring and calibration of estimation models.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21207.md)

## Name of the risk

Reliance on estimation methods may lead to false sense of security and inadequate preparedness for contagious disease transmission control.
