# __Inadequate adoption and implementation of virtual reality technology in healthcare education due to infrastructure limitations or resistance to change.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Singh_significant_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_564.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Invest in infrastructure for virtual reality training, provide incentives for healthcare institutions and staff to adopt virtual reality technology, offer comprehensive training and support.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16144.md)
