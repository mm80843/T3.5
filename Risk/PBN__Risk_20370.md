# __Submetering of building energy usage requires physical installation of ct coils on each circuit, potentially increasing the exposure of maintenance staff and installers to contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kawka_impacts_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_689.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide personal protective equipment ppe to maintenance staff and installers, implement strict hygiene protocols and social distancing measures during installation and maintenance activities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22350.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased physical contact during installation and maintenance activities can heighten the risk of disease transmission among maintenance staff and installers.
