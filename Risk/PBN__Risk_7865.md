# __Inadequate consideration of general facility design for different patient conditions and operational requirements may lead to increased risk of viral transmission within the hospital or healthcare facility.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dietz_2019_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_454.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct comprehensive risk assessments to evaluate the impact of design decisions on disease transmission. implement measures such as improved ventilation, isolation capabilities, and enhanced triage systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9748.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased potential for the spread of contagious diseases among patients and healthcare workers, leading to more severe outbreaks and higher rates of infection.
