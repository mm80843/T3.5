# __Default simulation time step may not be optimal for modeling contagious diseases with different transmission rates and behaviors.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lee_designing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_672.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Allow customization of simulation time step to accommodate varying transmission rates and behaviors of contagious diseases. conduct sensitivity analysis to identify optimal time steps for different scenarios.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21407.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate simulation outcomes, leading to poor decision-making in space design and policy implementation for COVID-19 safety measures.
