# __Potential spread of contagious diseases through shared personal comfort systems pcs in the built environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kaklauskas_covid-19_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_693.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and disinfection of pcs, implementation of individualized pcs systems, and promotion of personal hygiene practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22482.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of contagion and infection if PCS are not properly managed or maintained.
