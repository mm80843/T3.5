# __Data captured from smart ieq sensor nodes is stored in text files, posing a risk of data security and integrity.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Calvo_scalable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_427.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement encryption and access control measures for stored data. regular data backups and integrity checks to detect and prevent data tampering.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8168.md)

Potential unauthorized access, manipulation, or loss of IEQ data, compromising the accuracy and reliability of monitoring and analysis.
