# __Use of wearables to detect covid-19 symptoms may lead to false positives or false negatives, resulting in incorrect diagnoses.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burki_wearable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_425.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and validation of wearables with clinical testing, and clear guidance on action to take in case of an alert.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7932.md)

## Name of the risk

Misdiagnosis and unnecessary isolation or lack of isolation, contributing to the spread of the virus.
