# __Relying solely on ai for contagious disease screening may lead to overlooked cases or inaccurate results, contributing to the spread of the disease.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Madurai_elavarasan_restructured_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_664.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Combining ai screening with traditional diagnostic methods, regular updates of ai algorithms based on new data, and rigorous testing and validation of ai results.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21022.md)

Failure to accurately screen for contagious diseases can lead to increased transmission, outbreaks, and overwhelmed healthcare systems.
