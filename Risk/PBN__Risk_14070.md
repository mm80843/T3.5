# __Variation in illumination standards between car parking buildings and educational spaces poses a challenge in providing adequate lighting for the adapted spaces, increasing the risk of discomfort and potential safety hazards.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Design and implement a lighting plan that meets the recommended light levels for educational spaces, considering natural lighting, artificial lighting, and energy-efficient fixtures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15998.md)

## Name of the risk

Insufficient lighting may lead to discomfort, eyestrain, and reduced safety in the educational spaces.
