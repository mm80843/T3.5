# __Lack of visibility into the number of patients and their contact with the badges increases the difficulty of contact tracing and containment of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pilati_digital_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_609.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement a system to track badge usage and monitor patient interactions, deploy contact tracing technology to identify potential exposure, establish clear protocols for isolation and testing of individuals in contact with badges.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18331.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to effectively track and isolate potential disease carriers, leading to uncontrolled spread within the facility.
