# __Airborne transmission of infectious agents__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Memarzadeh_applications_2010](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_645.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improvement of ventilation systems, use of air purifiers, wearing masks, and maintaining proper humidity levels](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20087.md)

## Name of the risk

Long-lasting presence of infectious droplet nuclei in the air, leading to increased risk of infection
