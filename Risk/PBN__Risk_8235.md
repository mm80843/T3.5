# __Lack of adequate quarantine facilities and isolation spaces within housing environments, leading to uncontrolled transmission of contagious diseases.__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Designation of designated quarantine areas within housing, implementation of flexible housing designs to accommodate isolation spaces, and investment in community healthcare facilities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10121.md)
