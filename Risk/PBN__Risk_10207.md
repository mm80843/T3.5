# __Condensed arrangement of equipment may increase the risk of close contact and the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Reorganize equipment layout to allow for more space between each machine and implement a queuing system to limit the number of people in the hall at a given time.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12115.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased likelihood of disease transmission among gym members.
