# __Increased time spent at home and on balconies during the pandemic may have led to higher exposure to contaminants and air pollutants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aydin_questioning_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_403.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encourage regular ventilation, indoor air purification, and maintenance of a clean and safe indoor environment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6836.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of respiratory illnesses and airborne infection transmission due to prolonged exposure at home and on balconies.
