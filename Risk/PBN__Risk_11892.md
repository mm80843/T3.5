# __Impact of non-contact interaction on community service facilities may lead to differential impacts on different types of facilities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_urban_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_517.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct a comprehensive assessment of the impacts of non-contact interaction on each type of community service facility and develop adaptive strategies to address the differential impacts.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13805.md)

## Name of the risk

If the differential impacts of non-contact interaction are not considered, some community service facilities may become inadequate or obsolete.
