# __Development of makeshift ventilation solutions using off-the-shelf components and 3d printing techniques may pose risks related to device safety, reliability, and function.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhao_supporting_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_499.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Thorough testing and quality assurance of components, collaboration with regulatory bodies to ensure compliance, and continuous monitoring and maintenance of makeshift ventilator systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12324.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential device malfunctions, inadequate performance, and safety hazards for patients relying on makeshift ventilators.
