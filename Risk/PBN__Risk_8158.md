# __Inadequate consideration of climatic conditions in balcony design may lead to indoor environmental conditions that are conducive to disease transmission.__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough environmental impact assessments and implement appropriate design measures to ensure proper ventilation and thermal comfort. educate residents on the safe use of designed outdoor spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10044.md)
