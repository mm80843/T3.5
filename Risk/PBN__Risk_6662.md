# __False sense of security provided by the voice-detection app for covid-19 screening__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Chamola_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_430.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Clearly communicate the limitations of the app, encourage users to consult healthcare professionals, and emphasize that the app is not a replacement for medical testing](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8536.md)

## Name of the risk

Delay in seeking medical attention, neglect of conventional diagnostic methods, and potential spread of the virus
