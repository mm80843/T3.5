# __Risk of virus transmission through shared touch surfaces on holographic display interfaces__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Allam_future_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_390.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of touchless interfaces, regular cleaning and disinfection of touch surfaces, and user education on hygiene practices](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5835.md)
