# __Ramping up the uptake of low emission vehicles and constructing more electric car charging points may increase human interactions within urban environments, potentially contributing to the transmission of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibn-mohammed_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_494.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote contactless charging options, implement regular sanitation of charging points and transportation facilities, and provide public health education on disease prevention.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12021.md)

## Name of the risk

Elevated risk of disease transmission due to increased human activity around charging points and public transportation facilities.
