# __Use of individual hvac systems with lower ventilation rates__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Azuma_environmental_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_406.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Consider using centralized hvac systems with higher ventilation rates and air filtration, or upgrade individual systems with better filtration and ventilation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7001.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of COVID-19 transmission due to lower air exchange rates and higher concentration of infectious particles
