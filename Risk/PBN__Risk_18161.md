# __Health data collection from fitness trackers and vital signs monitoring raises concerns about data accuracy, reliability, and potential misuse.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mehta_pandemic_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_646.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure rigorous validation and calibration of the data collection devices, and provide clear guidelines for interpreting the collected health data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20125.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate health data could lead to incorrect identification of symptoms and unnecessary panic or complacency.
