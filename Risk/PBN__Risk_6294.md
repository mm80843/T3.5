# __Centralization of the ieq monitoring system may lead to a single point of failure for the entire system, increasing the risk of widespread impact if the central system fails.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Calvo_scalable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_427.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement redundant monitoring and control systems to distribute the risk of system failure. regular maintenance and testing of the central system to ensure its reliability.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8166.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential loss of monitoring and control capabilities for the entire building, leading to compromised indoor environmental quality (IEQ) and potential health risks.
