# __Predicting the spread of coronavirus and planning healthcare capacity based on data analysis may result in insufficient consideration of social and behavioral factors that influence disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mehta_pandemic_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_646.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate social and behavioral analysis into healthcare capacity planning and resource allocation, and ensure collaboration between data analysis experts and public health professionals.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20116.md)

## Name of the risk

Inadequate consideration of social and behavioral factors may lead to underestimation of healthcare resource demand and ineffective allocation of resources for controlling the spread of the virus.
