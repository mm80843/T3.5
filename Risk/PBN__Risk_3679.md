# __Viral rna shedding in stool not included in the model, leading to underestimation of prevalence.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ahmed_first_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_383.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate the proportion of infected patients shedding viral rna in stool into the model, accounting for geographic variation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5552.md)

## Name of the risk

Underestimation of infection prevalence by as much as 4-fold.
