# __Relying on sensor-only estimation of people flow__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Li_building_2016](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_668.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Integration of multiple data sources and estimation methods to improve accuracy of people flow estimation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21206.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate estimation of people occupancy, leading to potential overcrowding in certain areas and increased risk of contagious disease transmission.
