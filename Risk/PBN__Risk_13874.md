# __Inadequate recreational spaces in public external spaces of apartment complexes__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_designing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_558.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Adapt design concepts for inclusion of social pods, implement modular and adaptable recreational spaces in apartment complexes, and provide options for safe socialization and recreation during pandemic times](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15801.md)

## Name of the risk

Limited space for safe and socially distanced recreation during a pandemic, leading to potential overcrowding and increased risk of disease transmission
