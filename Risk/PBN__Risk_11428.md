## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict quality control measures for the collection and storage of used coffee, and conduct thorough cleaning and sanitization of the materials before production.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13340.md)

Contaminated materials can lead to the production of unhygienic and potentially harmful footwear, increasing the risk of spreading contagious diseases through contact.
