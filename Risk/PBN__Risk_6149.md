# __The settling time of small aerosols being comparable to ventilation timescales, leading to prolonged exposure to aerosols in indoor spaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increasing air exchange rates, reducing occupancy levels, and implementing air disinfection technologies to minimize aerosol exposure.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8021.md)

## Name of the risk

Extended exposure to aerosols carrying contagious diseases, increasing the risk of infection for individuals in the space.
