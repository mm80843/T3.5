# __Balconies as areas for observation and leisure activities can lead to close proximity and interaction between individuals, increasing the risk of contagious disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aydin_questioning_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_403.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enforcing policies on balcony usage, promoting awareness of the risks of close interaction, and encouraging responsible behavior on balconies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6811.md)

## Name of the risk

Unregulated use of balconies for leisure and socializing can contribute to the spread of contagious diseases within the building.
