## Mitigations of this risk

* [Public awareness campaigns, community engagement, targeted testing and surveillance in affected areas, and strategic lockdown measures. encourage remote work and limit non-essential gatherings in affected communities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22813.md)
