# __Use of beta coefficients as a quantitative measure of the associations between green-related variables and psychological outcomes.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spano_association_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_559.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide effect size measures and confidence intervals alongside beta coefficients to improve the interpretation of the associations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15894.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential misinterpretation of the strength and direction of the associations, leading to erroneous conclusions about the impact of green features on psychological health.
