# __Wearable devices used for maintaining social distancing and contact tracing may raise concerns about data privacy and surveillance.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Transparent communication on data collection and usage, strict adherence to data protection laws, anonymization of location and contact data, user consent and opt-in policies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8634.md)

## Name of the risk

Invasion of personal privacy, potential misuse or unauthorized access to location and contact data, public distrust in the use of wearables for COVID-19 prevention.
