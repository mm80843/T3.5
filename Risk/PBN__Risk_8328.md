# __Inadequate pressure differential leading to potential for cross-contamination of air between safe and unsafe areas.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper airflow direction from safe to unsafe areas, and from personal use areas to public areas. utilize technology to monitor and maintain pressure differentials.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10218.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of cross-contamination and potential spread of airborne pathogens between different areas within a building.

## People affected by this risk

* [Occupants in buildings with varying degrees of air pressure differentials](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_6911.md)
