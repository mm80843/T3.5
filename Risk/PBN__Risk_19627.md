# __Handling enveloped viruses without proper chloroform treatment__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [La_rosa_first_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_675.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure proper inactivation methods are followed according to safety guidelines for handling enveloped viruses](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21605.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of not completely inactivating the enveloped viruses, leading to potential exposure and spread of the virus
