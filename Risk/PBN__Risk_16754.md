# __Evaluation and assessment of building automation and control systems could lead to design flaws that impact building performance.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough testing and simulations, involve building occupants in the assessment, and incorporate feedback loops for continuous improvement.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18701.md)
