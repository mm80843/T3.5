## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Huy-tran_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_493.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Installation of air purification systems, regular maintenance of hvac systems, and use of natural ventilation where feasible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11893.md)
