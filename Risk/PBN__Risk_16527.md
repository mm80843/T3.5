# __Limited access to restorative spaces__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Peters_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_612.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Create and maintain nonbedroom spaces within buildings that promote relaxation, physical activity, and social interactions. design outdoor areas and shared common spaces to support restorative experiences and positive social interactions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18472.md)

## Name of the risk

Lack of access to restorative spaces can lead to increased stress, mental health issues, and disrupted sleeping patterns among residents.
