## Articles mentionning the risk

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Collaboration with epidemiologists and public health experts to validate the relationship between sewage monitoring data and clinical case data, and ongoing refinement of the estimation methodology.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20309.md)

## Name of the risk

Incorrect estimation of the COVID-19 prevalence based on sewage monitoring could result in inaccurate public health decisions and interventions.
