# __Potential exposure to uv-c radiation for maintenance personnel or occupants if not properly shielded or turned off during maintenance.__

* [Vranay_adaptation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_524.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
