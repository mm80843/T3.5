# __Lack of consideration for local cultural and religious practices during travel increases the risk of cultural transmission of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Budd_responsible_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_422.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encourage travelers to be sensitive to local customs and practices to minimize the risk of cultural transmission of contagious diseases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7862.md)

## Name of the risk

Disregarding local cultural practices can lead to the transmission of contagious diseases and strain relationships between travelers and local communities.
