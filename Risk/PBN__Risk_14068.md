# __Inadequate protection of interior surfaces from water, heat, moisture, and abrasion, potentially leading to mold growth and indoor air quality issues.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Select and apply finishing materials that are resistant to water, heat, moisture, and abrasion. implement regular inspection and maintenance of interior surfaces to prevent mold growth.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15996.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of mold growth, degradation of indoor air quality, and potential respiratory health issues due to inadequate protection of interior surfaces.
