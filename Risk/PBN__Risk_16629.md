# __Lack of onsite energy generation, integrated storage units, or grid connection in healthcare buildings__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement onsite energy generation, integrated storage units, and connection to the grid to enhance energy flexibility and storage capacity.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18575.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to achieve energy flexibility and storage, leading to potential energy shortages and inefficiencies.
