# __Inadequate ventilation leading to the buildup of airborne contaminants__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase outdoor air ventilation, use appropriate filtration, and implement natural or forced ventilation techniques](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10232.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne disease transmission, discomfort, and reduced indoor air quality
