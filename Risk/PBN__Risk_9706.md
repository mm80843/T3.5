# __Multiple zones in the hospital may lead to cross-contamination if not managed effectively.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hossny_management_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_488.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict protocols for movement between zones, enforce proper use of ppe, and conduct regular cleaning and disinfection of common areas.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11611.md)

## Name of the risk

Cross-contamination could lead to the spread of the virus to different zones, increasing the risk of infection for patients and staff.
