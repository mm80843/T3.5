# __Potential risk of increasing the re-suspension of virus-laden particles within the space due to the use of desk fans__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Avoid the use of desk fans unless stagnant zones are identified, and ensure proper orientation of fans to increase mixing with the wider space](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8075.md)
