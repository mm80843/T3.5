# __Large-scale uptake of contact tracing apps may raise privacy concerns and lead to data breaches.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sharifi_contributions_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_573.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict data privacy regulations and encryption protocols to protect user data. communicate transparently with the public about data security measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16637.md)
