# __Use of iot devices for tracking and surveillance may raise privacy concerns for individuals being monitored.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Subramanian_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_556.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict data privacy policies and obtain consent from individuals before monitoring them using iot devices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15727.md)

## Name of the risk

If privacy concerns are not addressed, it may lead to distrust in using these devices and hinder effective tracking of COVID-19 cases.
