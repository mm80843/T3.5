# __Use of shared gpu resources in the google colab environment may pose a risk of exposure to contagious diseases through shared computational resources.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Shorfuzzaman_towards_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_569.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilize dedicated or private gpu resources for computation, or ensure stringent cleaning and sanitization protocols for shared gpu resources.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16363.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission when accessing and utilizing GPU resources in shared computing environments.
