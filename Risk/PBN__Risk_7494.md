# __Use of nvivo software for generating word clouds may lead to misinterpretation of data or bias in the results.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cuerdo-vilches_mixed_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_446.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct manual verification and validation of the word clouds to ensure accurate representation of data. use multiple data analysis tools for cross-validation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9375.md)

## Name of the risk

Misleading insights into the most frequent terms and their frequencies, leading to inaccurate conclusions.
