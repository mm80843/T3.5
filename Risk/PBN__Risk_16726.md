# __Ineffective control of lighting systems leading to inadequate visibility and potential safety hazards.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance of lighting systems, installation of backup lighting, and use of motion-activated emergency lighting.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18673.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of accidents and injuries due to poor lighting conditions.
