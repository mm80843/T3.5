# __High airflow rates without increased outside air fraction can increase transmission potential of contagious diseases__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dietz_2019_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_454.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Focus on increasing outside air fraction along with airflow rates, and utilize air purification technologies to reduce the transmission potential of indoor air](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9727.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased transmission potential of contagious diseases poses a health risk to building occupants
