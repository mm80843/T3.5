# __Frequent use of washing machines and dryers in the built environment can increase the risk of spreading contagious diseases through contaminated laundry.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Al-mumin_occupants_2003](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_386.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement regular cleaning and maintenance of washing machines and dryers, encourage proper hand hygiene, and educate occupants on the importance of hygienic laundry practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5652.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of infections and illnesses among occupants in the building if proper hygiene and sanitation measures are not in place.

## People affected by this risk

* [Especially those who come in contact with contaminated laundry](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_165.md)
* [Occupants in the building](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_3047.md)
