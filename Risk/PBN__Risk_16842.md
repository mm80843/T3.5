# __Uneven spatial coverage and limited spatial coverage of traditional ground stations may lead to incomplete representation of pollution levels experienced by populations in their proximity.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pansini_covid-19_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_616.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Expand the spatial coverage of ground stations or supplement with additional monitoring tools. utilize spatial interpolation techniques to estimate pollution levels in uncovered areas.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18789.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate assessment of pollution levels for populations living near traditional ground stations.
