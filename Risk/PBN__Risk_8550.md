# __Competing or incompatible ecosystem services in urban green spaces may lead to suboptimal provision of services to humans and wildlife.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Felappi_green_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_467.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Developing a framework for prioritizing ecosystem services and identifying potential synergies and trade-offs. implementing sustainable management practices to balance the needs of humans and wildlife in urban green spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10443.md)

## Name of the risk

Suboptimal provision of services may negatively impact the health of humans and wildlife and increase the risk of disease transmission within urban green spaces.
