# __Continuous operation of equipment such as refrigerators and stand-by plug loads in elementary and nursery schools__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Geraldi_addressing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_475.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing energy-efficient appliances, promoting power management practices, and conducting energy efficiency awareness campaigns](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10920.md)
