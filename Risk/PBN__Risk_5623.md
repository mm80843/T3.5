# __Lack of flexibility in apartment layout for functional reorganization during a pandemic__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bojovic_towards_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_416.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement flexible apartment layouts with movable partitions and modular furniture to enable quick reorganization.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7496.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to create working space or isolation areas when needed, leading to compromised social distancing and increased risk of disease spread.
