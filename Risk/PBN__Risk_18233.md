# __Reliance on mechanical ventilation and artificial environmental conditions__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Megahed_indoor_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_648.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement natural ventilation and passive design strategies, conduct air quality monitoring, and use appropriate disinfection and purification techniques.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20200.md)

## Name of the risk

Increases the risk of chronic diseases, allergies, and sick building syndrome due to poor indoor air quality.
