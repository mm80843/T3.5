# __Use of blockchain technology for provenance tracking and credit incentive may pose security and privacy risks.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_construction_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_521.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement robust security measures, regular security audits, and privacy-enhancing technologies such as encryption and access control.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13995.md)

Vulnerabilities in the blockchain system could lead to unauthorized access, data tampering, and exposure of sensitive information.
