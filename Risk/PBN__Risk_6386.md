# __The service may fail to adequately communicate the limitations of safe routes and the possibility of unavoidable exposure to dangerous areas.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cantarero_covid19-routes_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_428.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide clear and transparent information on the limitations of safe routes and the potential for exposure to dangerous areas despite route optimization.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8258.md)

## Name of the risk

Users may falsely assume that following designated safe routes guarantees avoidance of high-risk areas, leading to complacency and increased risk of exposure to contagious diseases.
