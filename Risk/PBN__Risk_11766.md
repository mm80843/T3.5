# __Inadequate screening and testing strategies in some countries may result in underreporting of covid-19 cases and transmission rates.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Whitelaw_applications_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_513.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement comprehensive and widespread testing strategies for both symptomatic and asymptomatic individuals, use of advanced screening technologies, and data integration for better surveillance.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13678.md)

## Name of the risk

Underestimation of the true prevalence and transmission dynamics of COVID-19, leading to inadequate public health measures.
