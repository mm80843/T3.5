# __Implementing a digital twin for each specific clinic due to non-linear relationships between clinic size and configuration may result in increased resource and time investment for implementation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pilati_digital_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_609.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop modular digital twin frameworks that can be customized for different clinic sizes, invest in automation for digital twin creation, and use data-driven algorithms to optimize configuration based on clinic size and properties.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18316.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher resource and time investment for creating individual digital twins for different clinics, leading to potential delays and increased costs.
