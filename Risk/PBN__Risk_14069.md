# __Transformation of car parking building to educational spaces may require adaptation of the building envelope for heat exchange control, posing a potential risk of improper ventilation and air circulation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct a thorough assessment of ventilation and air conditioning requirements for the educational spaces, and implement appropriate ventilation and filtration systems to ensure indoor air quality.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15997.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of indoor air pollution, discomfort, and potential spread of contagious diseases.

* [Building occupants](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_97.md)
