# __Lack of greenery near living and working areas may impact mental well-being and stress levels of occupants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Maharani_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_663.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate green spaces and indoor plants, create designated areas for indoor gardening and greenery, optimize access to outdoor views and natural elements.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20935.md)
