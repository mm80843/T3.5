# __Inefficient treatment of outside air in hvac systems may lead to the escape of microscopic particles, including covid-19 viruses, into treated air, posing a risk of infection.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improve the efficiency of air treatment processes, enhance filtration, and redesign hvac components to minimize the escape of microscopic particles.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10158.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of COVID-19 and other airborne diseases within the built environment, leading to increased illness and transmission rates.
