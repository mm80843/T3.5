# __Inadequate outdoor air supply leading to poor ventilation and increased risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase the amount of outdoor air supply and utilize 100% outdoor air when possible. utilize technology to monitor and control outdoor air supply.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10215.md)

## Name of the risk
