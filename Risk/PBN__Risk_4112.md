# __High-definition, no delay video consultation in the hospital may lead to over-reliance on remote healthcare and delay in seeking in-person medical attention for critical conditions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encourage individuals to seek in-person medical attention for critical conditions, promote awareness of the limitations of remote healthcare, and provide access to in-person medical care.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5984.md)
