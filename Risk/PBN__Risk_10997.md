# __High level of staff and management rotation may increase the risk of spreading contagious diseases among different groups of employees.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop strict hygiene protocols for staff members, provide comprehensive training on disease prevention, and ensure regular health screenings for all employees. limit contact between rotating employees when possible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12907.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased potential for the spread of contagious diseases as employees rotate between different communities and groups.
