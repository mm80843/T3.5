# __Reliance on wearable devices for monitoring patients and potential inaccuracies__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Malliga_comprehensive_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_661.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and testing of wearable devices, cross-referencing data with traditional medical assessments, and training healthcare professionals to interpret wearable device data](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20875.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of inaccurate readings and unreliable data leading to misdiagnosis and improper patient care
