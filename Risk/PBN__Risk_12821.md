# __Visits to agricultural lands by older individuals outside of urban areas increases risk of contagious disease spread.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Uchiyama_access_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_535.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement health and safety measures, conduct awareness campaigns, and limit the number of visitors to agricultural lands.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14740.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential higher transmission of contagious diseases in rural areas due to older visitors with easy access to agricultural lands.
