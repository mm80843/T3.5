# __Gaps in filter edges contributing to system failures in removing pathogens from shared air environments, impacting transmission of infections.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance and inspection of air filtration systems, use of high-quality filter materials, and sealing gaps for better air filtration.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6113.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced effectiveness of air filtration systems in mitigating the transmission of pathogens, including COVID-19.
