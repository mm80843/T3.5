# __Wire mesh floors in animal cages may not effectively prevent faecal-oral transmission of tb.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Escombe_upper-room_2009](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_464.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Replacing wire mesh floors with solid floors or implementing additional infection control measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10295.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission among guinea pigs housed in the exposure chambers.
