# __Lack of research on the impact of vr and ar technologies on disease propagation in educational settings.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Subramanian_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_556.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough research on the effects of vr and ar technologies on disease transmission in educational environments and establish best practices for safe usage.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15714.md)
