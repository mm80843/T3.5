# __Inadequate access to open air spaces leading to confinement-related mental health issues in isolated occupants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_residential_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_557.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Design and provide access to external open spaces or self-containable courtyards to allow isolating occupants safe access to the outside environment. consider the impact of spatial design on mental well-being.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15763.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of mental health issues, such as anxiety and depression, due to lack of access to open air and outdoor environments.

## People affected by this risk

* [Isolating occupants and residents in the built environment](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_11258.md)
