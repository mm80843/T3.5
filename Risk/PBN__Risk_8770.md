# __Bias in model due to inclusion of deaths from non-locally acquired infections__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Flaxman_report_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_470.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Create criteria to differentiate locally acquired and non-locally acquired infections, and exclude non-locally acquired deaths from the model.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10664.md)

## Name of the risk

Skewing the model's accuracy and reliability by including deaths from non-locally acquired infections, leading to inaccurate estimation of the impact of interventions.
