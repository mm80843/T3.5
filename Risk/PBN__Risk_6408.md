# __The reliance of the service on occupancy and danger models may introduce inaccuracies in identifying high-risk areas for covid-19 infection.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cantarero_covid19-routes_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_428.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular review and update of models based on real-time data and feedback from public health authorities. incorporation of data on infection rates and transmission patterns.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8280.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased likelihood of directing pedestrians to areas with high risk of COVID-19 infection, leading to potential exposure and transmission of the virus.
