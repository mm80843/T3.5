# __Integration of voice assistant technology in cooking apps may lead to cross-contamination of devices in the kitchen.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote the use of voice command devices with touchless activation and regularly sanitize devices used in cooking environments.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13199.md)

## Name of the risk

Cross-contamination could lead to the spread of foodborne illnesses and other contagious diseases.
