# __Increased use of digital techniques and technologies in construction may facilitate the spread of misinformation and fake news related to contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheshmehzangi_revisiting_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_437.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote credible sources of information, educate workers and stakeholders on identifying misinformation, and implement fact-checking processes for digital communication channels in the construction industry.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8865.md)

## Name of the risk

Misinformation and fake news can lead to panic, fear, and non-compliance with health and safety guidelines, potentially exacerbating the spread of contagious diseases.
