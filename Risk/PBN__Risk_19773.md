# __Misinformation and cyberchondria spread through excessive consumption of covid-19 content on social media.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Laato_what_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_678.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement interventions to nudge people to consume manageable amounts of covid-19 content, educate social media users to consume content in a sustainable manner, and restrict the amount of covid-19 specific information people are exposed to.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21751.md)
