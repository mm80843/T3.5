# __Overreliance on robots in healthcare may lead to reduced human interaction and personalized care, impacting patient well-being.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Madurai_elavarasan_restructured_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_664.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Balance the use of robots with human interaction, prioritize human-led care for emotional and psychological support. train healthcare staff to integrate robot assistance with personalized care.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20980.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased patient satisfaction and emotional support, potential decline in mental health of patients.
