# __Robots assisting healthcare workers may fail to prevent the spread of contagious diseases if not properly sanitized or maintained.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Madurai_elavarasan_restructured_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_664.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance and sanitization schedules for the robots, along with proper training for healthcare workers in robot interaction.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21004.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of healthcare workers getting infected and spreading the disease to patients and other healthcare workers.
