# __Regular data collection and monitoring of electricity use in housing units may lead to increased human contact in the built environment, potentially facilitating the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kawka_impacts_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_689.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement remote data collection methods or use automated energy monitoring systems to minimize human contact during data collection.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22349.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased human contact in the built environment can lead to higher risk of contagious disease transmission, contributing to public health concerns.
