# __Delivery of items via beyond line-of-sight blos uav may lead to technological failures or errors, increasing the risk of delayed or inaccurate deliveries.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Siriwardhana_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_563.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance and testing of uav technology, backup delivery systems in place for emergencies, and comprehensive training for operators to handle unexpected situations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16106.md)

## Name of the risk

Delayed or inaccurate item deliveries may affect essential supplies and increase the risk of physical interactions to obtain necessary items, potentially contributing to the spread of contagious diseases.
