# __Bacteria studied primarily as single cells aerosolized in deionized water, lacking mucus coating, may be more sensitive to uvgi.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Memarzadeh_applications_2010](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_645.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct studies on bacteria with mucus coating to understand uvgi effectiveness in real-world conditions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20058.md)

## Name of the risk

Misrepresentation of UVGI effectiveness on bacteria with mucus coating, leading to inadequate mitigation strategies.
