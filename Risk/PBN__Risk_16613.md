# __Model simulates infection dynamics based on sars-cov-2 parameter estimates, without considering age stratification.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Perez-saez_effect_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_613.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
