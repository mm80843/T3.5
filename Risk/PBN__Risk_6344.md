# __Crowdsourced smartphone data and social network analysis may lead to biased or inaccurate conclusions about the mental state of citizens.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cantarero_covid19-routes_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_428.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validate and cross-reference data from multiple sources, consider demographic and cultural differences, and involve mental health professionals in data interpretation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8216.md)

Inaccurate conclusions can lead to inappropriate interventions or inaccurate assessments of the population's mental state.
