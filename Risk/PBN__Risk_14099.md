# __Inefficient design of large car parking leading to queues back onto the public roadway__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Design efficient circulation roadways and gates to avoid queues, and plan large parking for long-stay and/or frequent users](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16027.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of traffic congestion, close contact between drivers, and potential spread of airborne diseases
