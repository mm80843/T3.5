# __Reliance on technical measures for health outcomes evaluation__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhang_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_500.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporating direct health efficacy observations and real-world data into health outcome evaluations, in addition to technical measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12431.md)

## Name of the risk

Over-reliance on technical measures may limit the understanding of direct health outcomes and real-world effectiveness of interventions in controlling contagious diseases.
