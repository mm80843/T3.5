# __Use of chlorine disinfection in hospital wastewater may result in chlorine-resistant pathogens.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_disinfection_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_520.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular testing of wastewater for chlorine-resistant pathogens and consideration of alternative disinfection methods.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13935.md)

## Name of the risk

Spread of chlorine-resistant pathogens in the wastewater may contribute to the ongoing propagation of contagious diseases.
