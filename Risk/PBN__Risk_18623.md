# __Inadequate consideration of vaccination and immunity rates in the model__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Matheri_water_2023](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_655.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate vaccination and immunity rate data into the model and adjust model parameters accordingly](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20593.md)
