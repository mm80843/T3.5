# __One-way mobility circulation may cause congestion and bottlenecks at entry and exit points, increasing the risk of close contact and disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheshmehzangi_10_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_436.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement staggered entry and exit times, and provide clear signage for one-way circulation routes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8824.md)
