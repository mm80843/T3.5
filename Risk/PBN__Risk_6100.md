# __Production of a wide range of droplet sizes during conversational speech.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of ventilation systems, social distancing, and use of face masks to reduce inhalation of speech droplets.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7972.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased likelihood of airborne transmission of droplets during speech, leading to potential exposure to infectious particles.
