# __High rh relative humidity providing protection against uv-induced dna or rna damage for viruses.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Memarzadeh_applications_2010](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_645.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement measures to control and maintain rh levels to ensure the effectiveness of uvgi, and utilize supplemental control measures in addition to uvgi.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20074.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of viruses being less susceptible to UVGI under high RH, leading to potential ineffectiveness of UVGI.
