# __Refurbishment plans and changes in indoor climate may impact ventilation and air quality, potentially affecting disease transmission rates within the building.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saari_effect_2006](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_584.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough assessments of ventilation systems and air quality to ensure optimal circulation and filtration of indoor air. implement ventilation upgrades and air quality monitoring systems as needed.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17285.md)

## Name of the risk

Disrupted airflow and ventilation systems may contribute to higher levels of disease transmission and reduced indoor air quality.
