# __Use of multiple assays with varying sensitivity for detecting sars-cov-2 in wastewater samples may lead to conflicting or inconclusive results.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ahmed_first_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_383.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
