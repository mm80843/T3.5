# __Use of uvgi for reducing droplet nuclei concentration in the room as an alternative to mechanical ventilation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Memarzadeh_applications_2010](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_645.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Combine uvgi with mechanical ventilation systems to achieve optimal reduction of droplet nuclei concentration.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20059.md)

## Name of the risk

Reliance on UVGI for reducing droplet nuclei concentration without adequate ventilation, potentially leading to inadequate indoor air quality.
