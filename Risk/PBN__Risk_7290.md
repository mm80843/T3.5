# __Use of dynamic modeling and population-based kalman filter approach introduces a risk of model complexity and interpretation challenges.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Collin_using_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_443.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide clear documentation and explanation of the modeling approach, conduct sensitivity analyses, and involve domain experts in the interpretation of results.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9171.md)

## Name of the risk

Complex models and methodologies may lead to misinterpretation of results and flawed decision-making regarding NPIs and public health interventions.
