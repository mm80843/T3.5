# __Human coronaviruses can remain infectious on inanimate surfaces for up to 9 days.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kampf_persistence_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_692.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular and systematic surface disinfection using effective agents like sodium hypochlorite or ethanol.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22448.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for prolonged surface contamination and increased risk of transmission through fomites.
