# __Increased time spent in the bedroom not sleeping, but engaging in activities like watching movies or using social media, which can be problematic for resident well-being.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Peters_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_612.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encourage spatial variety in small apartment units to support different activities, limit or eliminate pre-bedtime fluorescent light exposure, and create shared spaces for entertainment and socializing.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18499.md)

## Name of the risk

Disruption of sleep, increased nighttime alertness, reduced melatonin, and shifted circadian rhythms, leading to decreased well-being.
