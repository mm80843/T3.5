# __Use of diagnosis related groups charges and outpatient charges for unit cost estimates may not accurately capture the full cost of treatment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Santini_mental_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_577.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct a sensitivity analysis using alternative cost estimation methods and consult healthcare economists for validation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16818.md)
