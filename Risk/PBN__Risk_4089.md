# __Rapid adoption of 5g technology for telemedicine and tele-treatment may lead to insufficient training and preparedness among medical staff, increasing the risk of errors and misuse of technology.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide comprehensive training programs for medical staff on the use of 5g-enabled medical devices and telemedicine protocols. establish clear guidelines and best practices for remote medical procedures to ensure safe and effective use.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5962.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate diagnosis, improper use of remote medical devices, and compromised patient safety due to lack of training and familiarity with 5G systems.
