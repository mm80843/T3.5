# __Use of smartphone technology for diagnosis and imaging may lead to misinterpretation of medical images and misdiagnosis of contagious diseases like covid-19.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Raza_mobile_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_596.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensuring proper training and guidelines for using mobile technology in diagnosis and imaging. implementing quality control measures for image transmission and interpretation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17865.md)
