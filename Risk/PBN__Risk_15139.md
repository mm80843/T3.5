# __Differential access to green areas leading to avoidance of parks in poorer neighborhoods during the pandemic.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sainz-santamaria_governance_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_582.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide incentives and support for residents in poorer neighborhoods to access green spaces, and implement measures to distribute crowding across all public spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17079.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of crowding in other public spaces, leading to higher transmission of contagious diseases.
