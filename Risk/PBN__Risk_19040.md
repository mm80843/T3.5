# __Dependence on a single ventilator design and manufacturer__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Madurai_elavarasan_restructured_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_664.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Diversifying the sources of ventilator supply, developing standardized protocols for alternative ventilator use, and investing in local ventilator manufacturing capabilities](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21012.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of shortage if the production of the specific ventilator is disrupted, leading to inadequate treatment for patients with respiratory illnesses
