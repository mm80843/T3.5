# __Uv-c exposure time of only 5 minutes resulted in undetectable virus levels for mers-cov and sars-cov.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bedell_efficacy_2016](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_412.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Emphasize the importance of combining uv-c disinfection with standard cleaning practices and other preventive measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7305.md)

## Name of the risk

Effective disinfection may lead to a false sense of security and result in reduced adherence to other preventive measures.
