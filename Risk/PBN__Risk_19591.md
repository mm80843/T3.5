# __Potential spread of contagious diseases through the analysis and synthesis of a wide range of materials from online sources.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Leal_filho_covid-19_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_674.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene and safety measures during data collection and review, including regular hand hygiene and wearing masks.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21569.md)
