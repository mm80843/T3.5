# __Risk of increased disease transmission due to shared housing arrangements for construction workers__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Assaad_guidelines_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_399.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Revise housing arrangements to ensure single occupancy, provide isolation facilities for symptomatic workers, implement regular testing and health checks](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6538.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of disease transmission among construction workers living in shared housing, leading to potential outbreaks
