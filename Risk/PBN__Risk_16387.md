# __Use of shared badges by multiple patients can lead to indirect contact and potential transmission of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pilati_digital_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_609.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement a system of regular sanitation and disinfection of the badges between uses, or switch to a non-contact method of data transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18329.md)

## Name of the risk

Spread of contagious diseases within the patient population and to the employees.
