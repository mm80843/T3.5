# __Inefficient use of battery capacity due to inaccurate dimensioning based on energy consumption data.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Calvo_scalable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_427.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Accurately dimension batteries based on energy consumption data and consider fluctuations in energy usage.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8161.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of downtime and disrupted operations if battery capacity is inadequate for the actual energy consumption.
