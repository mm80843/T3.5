# __Laboratory experiments using visual stimuli and students as participants may not fully represent the reality of user experience in green spaces or the characteristics of the whole population.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Felappi_green_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_467.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate diverse participant groups, use more immersive assessment methods, and conduct complementary field studies to validate laboratory findings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10493.md)

## Name of the risk

Limited generalizability of findings to the broader population and real-world settings.
