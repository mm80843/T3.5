# __Contamination and spread of viruses in the toilet area due to shared use by multiple patients, potentially leading to a higher risk of infection.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ding_toilets_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_455.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing strict sanitation and cleaning protocols for the toilet area, and reducing the number of patients sharing the facilities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9773.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of cross-contamination and viral spread among patients using the shared toilet area.
