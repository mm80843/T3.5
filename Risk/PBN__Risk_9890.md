# __Sensitivity of estimates to withholding regional data blocks may introduce uncertainty in the assessment of policy effectiveness.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hsiang_effect_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_491.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Ensure comprehensive data collection from all regions, validate the robustness of estimates through sensitivity analysis, and consider the influence of regional variations in policy implementation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11797.md)

Challenges in accurately evaluating the impact of policies on infection rates, leading to limited understanding of their effectiveness.
