# __Potential spread of contagious diseases through the use of drones for delivery__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Chamola_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_430.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of strict sanitation protocols for drone maintenance and package handling, including regular disinfection and use of protective coverings](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8494.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission if proper sanitation and hygiene measures are not implemented for the drones and delivery packages
