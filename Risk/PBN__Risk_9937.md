# __Idiosyncratic events within a country, such as holidays or backlog in testing laboratories, could generate nonuniform country-wide changes in infection growth for individual days.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hsiang_effect_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_491.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement standardized testing and reporting procedures across different regions within a country to minimize the impact of idiosyncratic events on infection growth.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11844.md)

## Name of the risk

Increases the risk of unpredictable and nonuniform spread of infections within a country, potentially leading to higher transmission rates.
