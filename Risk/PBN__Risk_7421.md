# __High frequency of garden usage may increase the risk of exposure to airborne diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Corley_home_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_445.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encourage the use of face masks and proper ventilation in garden areas, and limit the number of people in crowded garden spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9301.md)
