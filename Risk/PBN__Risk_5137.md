# __Inadequate maintenance of air-conditioning and ventilation systems in specific buildings can lead to poor ventilation, increasing the risk of covid-19 transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Azuma_environmental_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_406.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enforce regular maintenance of air-conditioning and ventilation systems, implement air quality monitoring, and promote proper ventilation practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7009.md)

## Name of the risk

Elevated risk of COVID-19 transmission within specific buildings due to inadequate ventilation from poorly maintained systems.
