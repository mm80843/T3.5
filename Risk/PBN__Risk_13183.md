# __Inadequate control of air pollutants within and outside the building could lead to poor indoor air quality and potential health risks for occupants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tleuken_readiness_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_542.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement measures to control and reduce air pollutants, such as air purifying equipment, use of low voc construction materials, and regular maintenance of ventilation systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15103.md)
