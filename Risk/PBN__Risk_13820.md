# __Conversion of a front room into a containment space places the containment space in the heart of the sleeping quarters.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_residential_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_557.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Exploration of alternative locations for the containment space, such as within or adjacent to the front room without compromising on room functionality.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15745.md)

## Name of the risk
