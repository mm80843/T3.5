# __Inflexible layout design may lead to inadequate access to daylight and natural light, impacting the overall health and well-being of occupants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Maharani_biophilic_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_663.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement flexible layout designs, use adjustable partitions and window treatments to optimize natural light, prioritize window placement.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20933.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced physical and mental well-being due to lack of natural light and flexibility in living spaces.
