# __Inadequate protective equipment and cleaning tools for janitors__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide janitors with adequate protective equipment and cleaning tools, and centralize sanitization tasks to reduce workloads](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12136.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of janitors interacting with sterilized surfaces and waste, potentially leading to disease transmission
