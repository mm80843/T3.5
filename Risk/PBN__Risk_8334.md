# __Inadequate control of temperature and relative humidity leading to suboptimal conditions for disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Monitoring and control of temperature and relative humidity, use of air conditioning and humidification systems, relocation of air-cleaning devices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10224.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased susceptibility to infection and spread of contagious diseases due to unsuitable temperature and humidity levels.
