# __Lack of objective data on noise measurement and sound insulation of buildings hinders accurate assessment of indoor noise sources.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lee_attitudes_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_673.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement low-cost and portable measurement systems using smartphones and raspberry pi for indoor noise measurements. collect additional building data such as year of construction and window type for improved assessment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_21470.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to accurately assess the impact of building sound insulation on noise complaints and propagation.
