# __A cloud of infected aerosol may be mixed by the wakes of many people in spaces like supermarkets, increasing the risk of exposure to subsequent individuals.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing frequent and thorough cleaning procedures, encouraging the use of face masks, and maintaining physical distancing in crowded spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8020.md)

## Name of the risk

Elevated risk of infection from viruses present in the mixed aerosol, especially for subsequent individuals passing through the same space.
