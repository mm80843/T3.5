# __High risk of false-negative results in self-collected at-home covid-19 test kits due to improper sample collection.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhao_supporting_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_499.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide comprehensive and easily understandable instructions for self-collection, and offer virtual guidance for sample collection.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12330.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate test results and potential for undetected cases, leading to increased transmission of the virus.
