# __Lack of adequate ventilation in home isolation spaces can lead to increased risk of virus spread within the household.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Zhao_supporting_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_499.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improving ventilation in home isolation spaces and using designated, adequately ventilated rooms for isolation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12334.md)

Higher likelihood of transmission and infection within the household, leading to increased community spread of the virus.
