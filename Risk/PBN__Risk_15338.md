# __Inadequate ventilation rates may lead to increased prevalence of respiratory diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saari_effect_2006](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_584.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase ventilation rates, implement air purification systems, and enforce regular maintenance of ventilation systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17278.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of respiratory diseases and increased sick leave.
