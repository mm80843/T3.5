# __Dismantling of partition walls may disrupt the containment of contagious diseases and facilitate their spread within the building.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saari_effect_2006](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_584.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Re-evaluate the design to incorporate effective containment measures for contagious diseases, such as partition walls or other barriers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17283.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of spreading contagious diseases among building occupants.

## People affected by this risk

* [Building occupants](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_97.md)
* [Especially those in close proximity to the dismantled partitions](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_12373.md)
