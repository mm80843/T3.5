# __Inadequate ventilation on small balconies may limit air circulation and increase the risk of airborne disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aydin_questioning_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_403.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Installation of portable air purifiers, strategic placement of potted plants to enhance natural ventilation, and regular cleaning and maintenance of balcony vents.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6847.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of disease transmission, particularly airborne diseases, due to poor ventilation on small balconies.
