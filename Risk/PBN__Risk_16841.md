* [Pansini_covid-19_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_616.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Harmonizing and validating the data from both sources to ensure accurate correlation analyses. implement statistical adjustments to account for discrepancies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18788.md)
