# __Single-exposure regression analysis used to estimate associations between indoor-outdoor green features and psychological health outcomes.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spano_association_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_559.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Include multiple exposure regression analysis to account for potential confounding variables and ensure robust associations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15892.md)

## Name of the risk

Misinterpretation of associations, leading to incorrect conclusions about the impact of green features on psychological health.
