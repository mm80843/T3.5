# __Inconsistent compliance with government regulations due to lack of resources for smaller retailers, potentially leading to increased risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide financial assistance to smaller retailers for compliance measures, offer training and support for regulatory compliance, and implement standardized guidelines across all retail establishments.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20389.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher likelihood of disease spread in smaller retail establishments with limited resources and personnel to enforce and communicate government regulations.
