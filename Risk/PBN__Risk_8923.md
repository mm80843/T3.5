# __Assessing room acoustic performance in an unoccupied room with hvac running and closed windows may not accurately reflect real-life conditions for disease propagation.__

* [Geng_intelligent_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_474.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
