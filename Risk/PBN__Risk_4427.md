# __Lack of defined or organized space for remote work and study__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ara_dilshad_shangi_adaptive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_396.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate designated work/study spaces in home design or adapt existing spaces for work/study purposes](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6300.md)

## Name of the risk

Difficulty for people to effectively work and study from home, increasing the potential for mental stress and reduced productivity
