# __Lack of sensitivity in experimental methods for capturing all droplets emitted during coughing and sneezing may lead to incomplete understanding of droplet propagation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improving experimental methods, such as using advanced high-speed photography and particle detection techniques, to capture a wider range of droplet sizes and trajectories.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7990.md)

## Name of the risk

Misinterpretation of experimental results and inaccurate assessment of social distancing guidelines.
