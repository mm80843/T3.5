# __Lack of awareness and access to iaq monitoring systems for non-tech savvy individuals can hinder the adoption of preventive measures and lead to prolonged exposure to unhealthy indoor air.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Saini_comprehensive_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_583.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Developing mobile app-based monitoring systems that are user-friendly and easy to operate, providing simple and clear guidance for preventive measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17193.md)

## Name of the risk
