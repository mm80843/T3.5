# __Lack of ventilation and natural light due to building envelope design__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Optimize building envelope design to maximize natural ventilation and light, incorporate air purification systems, and promote indoor plant growth.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16012.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne disease transmission, poor indoor air quality, and reduced occupant well-being.
