# __Closed-end hallways or corridors leading to restricted air circulation__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide open-ended corridors, avoid closed-end hallways, use ventilation louvers on doorstops](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6121.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for hot air circulation and barrier to air movement, increasing the risk of airborne infection
