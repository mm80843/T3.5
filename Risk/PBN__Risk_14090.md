# __Inadequate workshop facilities and limited space may impact the ability to implement proper hygiene and distancing measures, increasing the risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Soliman_building_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_561.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Optimize workshop layout for distancing, install additional hygiene stations, and schedule staggered usage to reduce crowding.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16018.md)

## Name of the risk

Greater vulnerability to disease outbreaks within the workshops, posing a health risk to staff and students utilizing the facilities.
