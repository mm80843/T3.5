# __Workers with comorbidities are at a higher risk of covid-19 infection and severe complications.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sunandar_preventing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_554.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement work-from-home policies for workers with comorbidities, and grant exemptions only to essential workers with proper risk assessment and medical recommendations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15663.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of severe illness, hospitalization, and death for workers with comorbidities if not mitigated.
