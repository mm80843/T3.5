# __Dispersion of infected aerosol caused by people movement delays the effectiveness of ventilation in controlling contagious diseases in built environments.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Optimizing ventilation systems to counteract the dispersion caused by people movement and implementing strict ventilation control measures during infectious disease outbreaks.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8023.md)

## Name of the risk

Ineffective ventilation control may lead to increased exposure to contagious diseases and higher transmission rates.
