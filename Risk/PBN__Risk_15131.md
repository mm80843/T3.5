# __Failure to incorporate green areas into urban policy may lead to inadequate planning for disease mitigation and public health in urban environments.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sainz-santamaria_governance_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_582.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporating green infrastructure considerations into urban policy and planning. engaging green area managers and stakeholders in decision-making processes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17071.md)
