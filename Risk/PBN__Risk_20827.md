# __Potential for the spread of contagious diseases due to increased infrastructure development and construction activities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Jallow_impact_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_697.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict health and safety protocols for construction workers, conduct regular testing and screening, promote vaccination, and enforce social distancing measures on construction sites.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22811.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission among construction workers, project stakeholders, and members of the public in close proximity to construction sites. Potential for a surge in disease spread if not mitigated.
