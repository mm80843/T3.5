# __Potential inaccuracies in early detection of covid-19 using wearable technology.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cheong_wearable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_435.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular validation and calibration of wearable technology, in conjunction with traditional testing methods and clinical assessment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8791.md)

## Name of the risk

Misidentification of infected individuals leading to delayed treatment and further transmission of the virus.
