# __Limited data accuracy or completeness in the epidemiological data and npi timelines from the ministry of health and other sources.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Perez-saez_effect_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_613.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validation and cross-referencing of data from multiple reliable sources, data cleaning and preprocessing, and sensitivity analysis of the model to account for data limitations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18565.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate modeling and analysis of SARS-CoV-2 transmission and effectiveness of NPIs, leading to flawed conclusions and recommendations.
