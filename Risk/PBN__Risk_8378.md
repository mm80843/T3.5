# __Potential exposure to uv radiation__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Clear guidelines for safe operation, proper shielding and enclosures for uvgi equipment, and training for personnel working with uv technology.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10268.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of skin and eye damage for individuals exposed to UV radiation from air purification systems, especially in cases of accidental exposure or equipment malfunction.
