# __Use of b&k multigas photoacoustic analyzers for measuring sf6 concentration__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Howard-reed_effect_2002](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_490.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
