# __Lack of clarity around what constitutes acceptable reasons for absence may lead to employees with contagious diseases coming to work.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Clear and specific communication of acceptable reasons for absence, provision of remote work options, and strict adherence to public health guidelines.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13530.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission within the workplace, potential outbreak, and increased employee absenteeism due to illness.
