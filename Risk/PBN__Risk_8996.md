# __Potential spread of covid-19 due to unusual occupation of social assistance buildings and community centers during the pandemic.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Geraldi_addressing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_475.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene and sanitation protocols, limit occupancy, and promote vaccination efforts in these buildings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10892.md)
