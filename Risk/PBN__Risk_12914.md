# __Inadequate piloting of data extraction sheet may result in incomplete or inaccurate recording of study details, leading to potential data errors and bias in the synthesis and analysis.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Thoroughly pilot the data extraction sheet on a representative sample of manuscripts to identify and address potential issues. provide clear guidelines and training for data extraction to ensure uniform and accurate recording of study details.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14833.md)

## Name of the risk

Incomplete or inaccurate data extraction may compromise the reliability and validity of the systematic review findings, undermining the evidence-based recommendations for disease mitigation in the built environment.
