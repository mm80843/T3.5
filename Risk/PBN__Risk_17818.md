# __Relying solely on web scraping for covid-19 data may result in incomplete or inaccurate information, increasing the risk of incorrect diagnosis and decision-making.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mufid_risk_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_640.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Verify and cross-reference web-scraped data with multiple reliable sources to ensure accuracy and completeness. implement algorithms for data validation and error-checking to minimize the impact of inaccurate data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19778.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate data may lead to ineffective mitigation strategies and delayed response to outbreaks, exacerbating the spread of the disease.
