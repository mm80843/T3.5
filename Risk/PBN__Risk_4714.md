# __Use of smartwatch data and self-reported symptoms to detect covid-19 may lead to false positives or false negatives.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ates_wearable_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_400.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Combine smartwatch data with other diagnostic tests to confirm covid-19 status before taking action.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6586.md)
