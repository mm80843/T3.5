# __Mandatory in-person data collection for section b of the survey increases the risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Marconi_green_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_659.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide alternative virtual or contactless options for section b data collection. ensure strict hygiene and safety protocols if in-person collection is unavoidable.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20806.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for spread of contagious diseases among survey participants during mandatory data collection.
