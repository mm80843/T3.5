# __Pressure differentials between zones not maintained as per guidelines, leading to potential cross-contamination of air__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_review_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_478.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular monitoring and adjustment of pressure differentials, proper maintenance of pressurized ventilator and exhaust systems](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11054.md)
