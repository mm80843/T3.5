# __Centralization of daily activities in hyper-affluent areas leading to potential interaction among people from all parts of the city.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Brizuela_understanding_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_421.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
