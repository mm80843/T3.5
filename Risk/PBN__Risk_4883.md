# __Inadequate ventilation and cooling capacity during extreme events leading to body heat overload and heat-related illnesses.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Awada_ten_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_402.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Upgrade ventilation and cooling systems, establish heat preparedness plans, and set up cooling centers for vulnerable populations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6757.md)

## Name of the risk

Dizziness, fatigue, fainting, heat strokes, emergency department visits, and deaths.
