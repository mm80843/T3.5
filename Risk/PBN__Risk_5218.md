# __Proteomic identification of disease biomarkers in sewage systems may lead to the unintentional tracking and monitoring of health conditions of different populations, potentially violating privacy and ethical standards.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Barcelo_wastewater-based_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_408.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict privacy measures and obtain ethical approvals for research involving the monitoring of health conditions in sewage systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7091.md)

## Name of the risk

Invasion of privacy through the tracking and monitoring of health conditions of populations, raising ethical concerns and privacy issues.
