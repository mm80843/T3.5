# __Higher gene equivalent concentrations in the effluent than the influent due to repartitioning of gene material during wastewater treatment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Westhaus_detection_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_514.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improved wastewater treatment processes to effectively eliminate or reduce gene material in the effluent.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13711.md)
