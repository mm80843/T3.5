# __Reliance on the assumption that the proposed service provides a substantial improvement in reducing exposure to the virus, without concrete evidence of its effectiveness in real-world scenarios.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cantarero_covid19-routes_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_428.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct controlled studies and field trials to validate the effectiveness of the service in reducing covid-19 exposure. communicate the limitations and uncertainties of the service to users.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8281.md)

## Name of the risk

Misleading the public into believing they are safe from COVID-19 exposure, leading to complacency and failure to adhere to other essential preventive measures.
