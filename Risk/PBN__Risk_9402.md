# __Use of outdated or inaccurate modeling software for hydraulic modeling could lead to errors in the representation of wastewater flow rates and travel times.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hart_computational_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_483.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular software updates and validation against real-time data, investment in modern modeling and simulation software.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11303.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate modeling may result in incorrect assessments of system capacity, leading to insufficient or excessive infrastructure investments.
