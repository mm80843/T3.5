# __Air cleaning and disinfection devices may be beneficial in environments with poor ventilation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Morawska_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_641.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of local air cleaning or disinfection devices such as germicidal ultraviolet guv in environments where improving ventilation is difficult.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19802.md)

## Name of the risk

Without proper ventilation, the risk of viral transmission within the indoor environment may remain high. Air cleaning and disinfection devices can help reduce this risk.
