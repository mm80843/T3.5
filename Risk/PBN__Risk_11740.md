# __Informal urban settlements with limited access to healthcare may experience a rapid spread of contagious diseases due to overcrowding and inadequate sanitation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wilkinson_local_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_512.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improving access to healthcare, sanitation, and public health education within informal urban settlements.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13652.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission and high mortality rates within the settlements, as well as potential spillover to surrounding areas.
