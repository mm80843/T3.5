# __Indoor spread of infectious diseases by the conditions of hvac systems.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance of hvac systems, use of high-efficiency air filters, and implementation of uvgi technology.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10203.md)
