# __High mobility rates in highly populated districts with low ugs densities, such as districts 6 and 7.__

* [Marconi_green_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_659.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase ugs density and accessibility in densely populated districts to disperse crowd and reduce transmission risk.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20795.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased mobility in densely populated areas with limited UGSs may lead to higher risk of contagious disease transmission and overcrowding, impacting public health.
