# __Verifying the validity of the expert system without considering real-world medical scenarios may result in inaccurate and unreliable early detection results.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mufid_risk_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_640.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct validation tests using real-world medical scenarios and diverse patient profiles to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the expert system.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19771.md)
