# __Limited monitoring of certain circuits due to the constraints of the home energy monitoring system may result in incomplete data collection, affecting the accuracy of energy use analysis.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kawka_impacts_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_689.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement advanced energy monitoring systems with higher input capacity, conduct regular maintenance and calibration of monitoring equipment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22351.md)

## Name of the risk

Incomplete data collection may lead to inaccurate assessments of energy usage, potentially resulting in inefficient energy management strategies.
