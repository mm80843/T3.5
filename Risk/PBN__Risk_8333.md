# __Lack of regular inspection and maintenance of water seals leading to potential water leaks and ingress of contaminants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular inspection and maintenance of water seals, use of proper sealants, and immediate repair of leaks.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10223.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of waterborne diseases, such as Legionnaires' disease, and deterioration of indoor air quality.
