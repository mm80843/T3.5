# __Inadequate ventilation in the indoor running track area may lead to stagnant air and increased risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Installation of proper ventilation systems, periodic air quality monitoring, and promoting good hygiene practices among users.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12111.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of airborne disease transmission due to poor air circulation in the enclosed space.
