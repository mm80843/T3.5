# __Closure of parks with physical barriers and instruction not to visit other parks may lead to overcrowding in the open parks.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sainz-santamaria_governance_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_582.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement a system to monitor park occupancy and enforce capacity limits. encourage the use of alternative outdoor spaces to distribute visitors.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17097.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of contagious disease transmission due to overcrowding in open parks.
