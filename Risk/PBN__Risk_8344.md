# __Improper ventilation maintenance leading to reduced effectiveness of ventilation systems__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance and inspection of ventilation systems, replacement of filters as needed, and monitoring of airflow rates](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10234.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of exposure to airborne contaminants, decreased indoor air quality, and potential equipment malfunction
