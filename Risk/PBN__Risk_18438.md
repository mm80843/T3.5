# __Difficulty in accessing digitized information for customers without online access__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide alternative methods for accessing product information and pricing, such as printed flyers or in-store displays, ensure accessibility to information for all customers](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20405.md)

## Name of the risk

Exclusion of customers from accessing important product information and pricing, potentially leading to unequal shopping experiences
