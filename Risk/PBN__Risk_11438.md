# __Increased use of face masks as a result of air quality concerns and the covid-19 pandemic.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Willems_how_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_511.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop and promote environmentally-friendly face mask materials, invest in technologies for decomposition, and educate consumers on sustainable disposal methods.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13350.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential environmental impact from the disposal of face masks, as well as the risk of ineffective protection against air particulate matter.
