# __Customer uncertainty due to unreadable signage and lack of clear information about open services and hours of operation__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mcneish_retail_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_652.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Design and implement clear, visible, and informative signage with appropriate font size and color contrast, provide accurate and updated information about services and operating hours](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20404.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential confusion and frustration for customers, leading to increased risk of non-compliance with safety measures and suboptimal shopping experience
