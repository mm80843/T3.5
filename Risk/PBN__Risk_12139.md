# __Potential for indoor air pollution due to reduced outdoor air supply__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_characterization_2011](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_522.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular air quality monitoring, use of indoor air purification systems, and implementation of ventilation rate standards](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14054.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission and health issues among building occupants

* [Building occupants](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_97.md)
* [Especially those in close proximity on the same floor](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_9937.md)
