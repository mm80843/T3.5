# __Inadequate consideration of infection control strategies in the early stages of building design__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_review_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_478.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Engage in an integrated design process to consider appropriate infection control strategies early in the building design, especially for buildings with a higher risk of transmission, and implement ashrae guidelines](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11030.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of infectious disease transmission due to lack of integrated infection control strategies
