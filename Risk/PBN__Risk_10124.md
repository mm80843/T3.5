## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement restrictions on air travel, enhanced screening and testing at entry points, and investment in technology for air quality control in transportation hubs.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12032.md)
