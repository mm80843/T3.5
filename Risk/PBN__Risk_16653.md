# __Limited energy efficiency improvement package targeting specific domains such as domestic hot water and heating__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of targeted ventilation and air quality improvements in areas with limited energy efficiency improvements, and regular assessment of indoor air quality in all domains.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18600.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential uneven distribution of energy-efficient benefits, leading to areas with less improvement having lower air quality and ventilation.
