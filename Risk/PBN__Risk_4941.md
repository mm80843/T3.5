# __Aesthetic use of balconies can lead to overcrowding and close proximity, increasing the risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aydin_questioning_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_403.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
