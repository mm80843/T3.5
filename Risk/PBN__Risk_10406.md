* [Zhao_supporting_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_499.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of stringent infection control measures, provision of adequate personal protective equipment ppe , and vaccination of healthcare workers.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12316.md)
