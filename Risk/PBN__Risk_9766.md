# __Relocation of operation expenses to workplace modification activities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hou_study_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_489.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct modifications during off-peak hours, enforce strict health and safety protocols during modifications, and limit the number of workers present during modifications.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11672.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for increased risk of exposure and transmission of contagious diseases during workplace modifications.
