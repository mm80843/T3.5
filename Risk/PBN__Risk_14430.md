# __Utilization of pedestrian detection models may lead to increased interaction with potentially infected individuals during data collection.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Shorfuzzaman_towards_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_569.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Minimize in-person data collection by using pre-labeled datasets or remote sensing techniques for model training.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16362.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of exposure to contagious diseases when deploying and training pedestrian detection models in public spaces.
