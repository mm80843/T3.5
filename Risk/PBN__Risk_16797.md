# __Potential risk of contamination if the sensor network is not properly sanitized or maintained, leading to the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Parkinson_continuous_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_615.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular sanitization and maintenance of the sensor network, along with clear guidelines for handling and hygiene practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18744.md)

## Name of the risk

Spread of contagious diseases within the built environment, resulting in occupant health issues and reduced productivity.
