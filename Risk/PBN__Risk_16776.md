# __Automatic generation and distribution of weekly reports may lead to complacency among users in conducting their own detailed follow-up diagnostics, potentially overlooking critical issues in indoor environmental quality.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Parkinson_continuous_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_615.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement regular education and training programs for users to emphasize the importance of conducting detailed follow-up diagnostics despite the availability of weekly summaries. encourage proactive engagement with the dashboard beyond the weekly reports.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18723.md)

## Name of the risk

Missed opportunities for timely identification and resolution of indoor environmental quality problems, resulting in prolonged exposure to unhealthy conditions for building occupants.
