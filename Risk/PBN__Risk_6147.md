# __Highly turbulent wake with a reynolds number of about 10^5 in spaces with high people density and movement.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing ventilation systems to quickly remove aerosols from the space, reducing the risk of exposure.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8019.md)
