# __Graphene oxide go and reduced graphene oxide rgo contain oxygen functional groups hydroxyl, carboxyl, carbonyl, and epoxy that affect their physicochemical properties.__

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Proper handling and disposal of graphene derivatives, implementation of safety protocols, and use of personal protective equipment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6875.md)

## Name of the risk

Uncontrolled exposure to graphene derivatives may lead to unintended physiological effects due to their antiviral properties.
