# __Antiviral drugs, if present in low levels in the environment, could cause irreversible effects to viral genomes, leading to resistance.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Sims_future_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_566.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct comprehensive environmental impact studies on the presence of antiviral drugs and their metabolites in water bodies, develop strategies to minimize environmental exposure, and research alternative drug compounds with lower environmental impact.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16183.md)

## Name of the risk

Development of antiviral drug resistance, especially in wildlife populations exposed to contaminated surface waters.
