# __Lack of awareness and education about the use of iot-based devices and mobile applications in the context of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Comprehensive educational programs for patients, healthcare staff, and family members on the proper use and maintenance of iot-based devices, and clear communication of hygiene protocols.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8668.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of improper use, leading to increased vulnerability to contagious diseases and potential spread of infection.
