## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement stringent quarantine and monitoring procedures for rescued animals, conduct regular health screenings, and provide training on zoonotic disease prevention for staff and local communities.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12896.md)
