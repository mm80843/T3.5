# __Inadequate handling of dirty filters during replacement can lead to the spread of biologic particles, including molds, bacteria, and viruses, posing a risk of contamination to individuals involved in maintenance activities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Nembhard_ventilation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_634.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Provide proper training and equipment for safe filter replacement, including gloves, respirators, masks, and disposal procedures for dirty filters.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19534.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential exposure of maintenance personnel to harmful biologic particles during filter replacement.
