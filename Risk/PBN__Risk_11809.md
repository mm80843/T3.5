# __Inaccurate quantification of endogenous parameters like creatinine and urea could lead to incorrect assessments of water quality and potential health risks.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Westhaus_detection_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_514.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Calibration and verification of the analytical instruments, adherence to standardized testing protocols, and regular proficiency testing.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13722.md)

## Name of the risk

Misguidance in determining the safety of the water supply, posing a health risk to individuals exposed to the water.
