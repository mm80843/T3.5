# __Reliance on subjective questionnaires for health assessments in buildings__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Awada_occupant_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_401.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement a combination of subjective and objective health assessment methods, provide training for accurate self-reporting, and ensure privacy and confidentiality of health data](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6656.md)
