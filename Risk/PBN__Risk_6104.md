# __Increased droplet size and number during speech, especially after eating__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote behaviors that reduce droplet emissions, such as speaking softly and avoiding close contact after eating](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7976.md)

## Name of the risk

Elevated risk of droplet transmission due to larger and more numerous droplets
