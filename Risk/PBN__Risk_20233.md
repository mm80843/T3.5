# __High abundance of deinococcus in building dust and mechanically ventilated offices.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kembel_architectural_2014](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_687.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular cleaning and maintenance of ventilation systems, monitoring of indoor humidity levels, and use of uv light disinfection.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22212.md)
