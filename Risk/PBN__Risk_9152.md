# __Insufficient ventilation leading to poor air quality and potential disease spread.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_review_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_478.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement proper ventilation systems, use air cleaners with hepa filters, and increase outdoor air in air-conditioning systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11051.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne disease transmission and compromised health of occupants.
