# __No daylight or motion sensors implemented in rooms__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Deploy advanced sensors and controls to optimize lighting based on occupancy and natural light availability, reducing energy waste and ensuring occupant well-being.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18659.md)

## Name of the risk

Lack of daylight and motion sensors hinders energy-efficient lighting and occupant comfort, leading to potential waste of energy and discomfort.
