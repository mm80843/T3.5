# __Use of 5g video and ar glasses for remote medical care may lead to overreliance on technology and reduced in-person interaction, impacting patient care and doctor-patient relationships.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement training for doctors on managing remote medical care and emphasize the importance of in-person assessments when necessary.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5974.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced quality of patient care, diminished doctor-patient relationships, and potential misdiagnosis due to limited in-person interaction.
