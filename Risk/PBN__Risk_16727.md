# __Inefficient cooling system maintenance leading to poor temperature control and potential discomfort for occupants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pedersen_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_614.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance of cooling systems, implementation of temperature monitoring, and improvement of fault detection systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18674.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of thermal discomfort and reduced productivity among building occupants.
