# __Improper disposal of infectious hospital wastes and wastewater could cause serious risks to public health and the environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wang_disinfection_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_520.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Proper management and disposal of infectious hospital wastes, implementation of strict guidelines and regulations for waste handling, and training of healthcare staff on safe waste management practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13983.md)
