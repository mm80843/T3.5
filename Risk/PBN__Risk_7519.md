# __Potential bias in participant qualification due to a higher representation of teleworking individuals.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Cuerdo-vilches_mixed_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_446.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Balance participant recruitment by actively targeting non-teleworking individuals through community outreach and engagement, and incorporating their perspectives in the analysis.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9400.md)

Skewed data and insights, potentially misrepresenting the experiences and needs of those not engaged in telework during the lockdown.
