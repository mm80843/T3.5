# __Modular construction for emergency hospitals may not have effective pathogen control measures in place, leading to increased transmission of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Navaratnam_designing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_635.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement rigorous pathogen control measures in the design and construction of modular emergency hospitals, including ventilation and cleaning protocols.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19611.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of spreading contagious diseases and higher infection rates among COVID-19 patients and healthcare workers.
