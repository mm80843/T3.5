# __Different building codes and systems of sustainability assessments in different countries may lead to inconsistencies and lack of standardization in pandemic-sustainable criteria for residential buildings.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ujikawa_how_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_532.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Develop a global standard for pandemic-sustainable criteria in residential buildings and encourage collaboration among different countries and certification systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14587.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to establish consistent pandemic-sustainable criteria may result in increased vulnerability of residential buildings to pandemics and hinder international scientific and industrial development.
