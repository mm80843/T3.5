# __Relying solely on digital technology for pandemic policy and response may lead to a false sense of security.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Whitelaw_applications_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_513.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Combine digital technology with traditional public health measures, such as testing, surveillance, and clinical management, to ensure comprehensive response.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13658.md)

## Name of the risk

Inadequate containment and mitigation efforts due to overreliance on digital solutions, leading to a resurgence of infections.
