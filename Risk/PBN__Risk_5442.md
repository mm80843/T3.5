# __Inaccurate adjustment of z values for use with upper-room uvgi systems may lead to ineffective disinfection of microbes suspended in the air.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Beggs_upper-room_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_413.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Carefully validate and calibrate the z value adjustments for upper-room uvgi systems based on experimental data and conditions specific to the room.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7315.md)

## Name of the risk

Ineffective disinfection may allow for the survival and propagation of contagious microbes in the room.
