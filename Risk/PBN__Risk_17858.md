# __Airborne transmission of contagious diseases such as tuberculosis, measles, chickenpox, sars-cov, mers-cov, rsv, and influenza.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Morawska_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_641.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Improving indoor air ventilation, using air filtration systems, and promoting mask-wearing and hand hygiene.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19819.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of infection in indoor environments, especially during close contact situations.
