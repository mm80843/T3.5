# __Lack of travel histories in case data raising the possibility of infections acquired away from the location of residence.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Tatem_integrating_2014](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_548.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Integrate travel history data into case data collection and analysis to differentiate between local and imported cases.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15388.md)

## Name of the risk

Inability to differentiate between likely local versus imported cases and assess the travel histories of imported cases.
