# __Frequent use of balconies for social activities and food storage increases the risk of contagious disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aydin_questioning_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_403.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement regulations on balcony use and hygiene practices, promote ventilation, and provide guidelines for safe food storage.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6835.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of contagious diseases among balcony users and to other household members.
