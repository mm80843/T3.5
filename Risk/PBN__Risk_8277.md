# __Aerosols remaining in the air after a person leaves a room, potentially remaining airborne for hours.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Elsaid_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_463.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Enhancing ventilation to remove lingering aerosols, wearing masks, and minimizing time spent in enclosed spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10165.md)

## Name of the risk

Risk of exposure to lingering aerosols containing the virus.
