# __The use of iot, drones, robots, and wearable devices can lead to privacy and data security risks, as personal health information may be compromised.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Subramanian_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_556.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strong encryption and data security measures, ensure compliance with data protection regulations, and provide clear guidelines for data access and usage.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15694.md)
