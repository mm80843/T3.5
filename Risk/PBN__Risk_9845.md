# __Variation in air change rate due to window opening width and location.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Howard-reed_effect_2002](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_490.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough ventilation analysis to understand the impact of window opening width and location on air change rate.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11752.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate understanding of ventilation patterns may lead to poor indoor air quality and increased risk of contagious disease transmission.
