# __Insufficient data and small sample sizes leading to unreliable estimates of virus inactivation rate constants__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Buonanno_far-uvc_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_424.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Increase sample sizes, validate results with additional experiments, use alternative statistical methods for estimation](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7927.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate estimates of virus inactivation rate constants, leading to incorrect understanding of UV susceptibility factors and potential misinterpretation of experimental results
