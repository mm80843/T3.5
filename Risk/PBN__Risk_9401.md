# __No incorporation of leakage or infiltration in the hydraulic model could underestimate the potential for pathogen transport and spread in the collection system.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Hart_computational_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_483.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementation of leak detection technology, regular inspection and maintenance of the collection system to identify and repair leaks and infiltration points.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11302.md)

## Name of the risk

Undetected leakage and infiltration can contribute to the spread of contaminants, increasing public health risks and environmental contamination.
