# __Nbpi may produce reactive ions and reactive oxygen species in the air, which could have unknown health effects.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Navaratnam_designing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_635.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct thorough research and testing to understand the health effects of reactive ions and oxygen species, and implement appropriate measures to mitigate any potential harm.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19585.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential harm to occupants' health if negative health effects are not thoroughly understood and mitigated.
