# __Detection and quantification of sars-coronavirus-2 rna in sewage samples.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Medema_presence_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_650.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular validation of the detection and quantification methods, including cross-validation with clinical data, and continuous improvement of rna extraction and quantification techniques.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20308.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to accurately detect and quantify SARS-Coronavirus-2 RNA could lead to a misrepresentation of the prevalence of COVID-19 in the population served by the WWTP.
