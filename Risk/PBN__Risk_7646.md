# __Introduction of new high-performing computer networks like 5g may result in increased exposure of the population to radio frequency electromagnetic fields, leading to potential health risks.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dalessandro_covid-19_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_448.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct comprehensive scientific research on the health effects of 5g technologies, implement regulations and guidelines to limit exposure levels, and educate the public on potential risks and safety measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9527.md)

## Name of the risk

Uncertain health effects linked with exposure to 5G wireless technologies, potential risk of adverse health impacts for individuals exposed to high-frequency electromagnetic fields.
