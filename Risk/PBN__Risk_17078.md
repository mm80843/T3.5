# __Survey data collected from multiple continents and countries, potentially increasing the risk of exposure to contagious diseases during data collection.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Paikan_effects_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_621.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict health and safety protocols for data collection, including use of personal protective equipment, frequent testing, and adherence to local health regulations.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19029.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of contagious diseases among survey respondents and data collectors, leading to health risks and potential outbreak.
