# __Use of balconies for daily activities like drying laundry and storing items can potentially spread contaminants or pathogens in the air or on surfaces.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aydin_questioning_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_403.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Educating residents on proper hygiene and maintenance of balconies, implementing guidelines for cleanliness and safe storage practices on balconies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6812.md)
