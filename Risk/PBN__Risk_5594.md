# __Lack of consideration for distancing and segregation within the residential area may contribute to the spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bojovic_towards_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_416.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporating design elements that promote physical distancing, such as creating communal spaces with adequate spacing and implementing traffic flow guidelines within the buildings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7467.md)
