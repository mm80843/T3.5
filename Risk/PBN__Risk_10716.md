# __Development of robotic systems for remote access may lead to reduced human interaction, impacting mental health.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Yang_combating_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_505.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement regular check-ins and support systems for individuals using remote robotic systems. encourage and facilitate in-person interactions in addition to remote interactions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12628.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of loneliness, depression, and mental health issues due to reduced social interactions.
