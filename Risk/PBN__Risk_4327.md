# __Inadequate utilization of natural ventilation and filtration systems may result in an unhealthy indoor environment.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Incorporate natural ventilation and improved filtration efficiency in early design stages, specification writing, and construction of healthcare settings.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6198.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of viral and bacterial infections in healthcare settings, leading to compromised patient and staff safety.
