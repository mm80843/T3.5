# __Incorporating thermal sensors and vision algorithms onto autonomous or remotely operated robots for temperature screening may lead to inaccurate or misleading results.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Yang_combating_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_505.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular calibration and maintenance of thermal sensors, supplemented with manual temperature checks where necessary.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12636.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to detect individuals with elevated temperatures, potentially allowing infected individuals to go undetected.
