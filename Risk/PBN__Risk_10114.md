# __Facilitating a step change towards sustainable improvements in air quality may lead to increased construction activities, potentially causing airborne particle pollution and respiratory health risks for urban residents.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibn-mohammed_critical_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_494.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict emission control regulations for construction activities, invest in advanced air filtration systems for construction sites, and monitor air quality in urban areas.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12022.md)

## Name of the risk

Unmitigated air pollution leading to respiratory health issues and exacerbation of respiratory diseases.
