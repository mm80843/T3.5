# __Potential spread of contagious diseases in office buildings due to close proximity of occupants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Parkinson_continuous_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_615.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing measures to control indoor air quality, ventilation, and sanitation to reduce the likelihood of disease transmission.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18717.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of disease transmission and outbreaks, leading to loss of productivity, operational disruptions, and potential harm to occupants.
