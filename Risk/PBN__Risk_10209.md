# __Close proximity of individuals and shared equipment increases the risk of fomite transmission of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict cleaning and sterilization protocols for all equipment and enforce the use of personal protective equipment ppe for all individuals in the hall.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12117.md)
