# __Potential for reduced focus on disease prevention and social distancing due to lifelike remote experiences.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Promote education and awareness on the importance of disease prevention, implement virtual social distancing measures, and encourage the use of remote technologies for essential tasks only.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5999.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased adherence to public health guidelines and increased risk of disease transmission in both remote and on-site locations.
