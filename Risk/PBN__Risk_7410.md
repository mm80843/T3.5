# __The use of a 5-point scale for self-rated health measures may lack granularity and sensitivity to capture nuanced changes in health status.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Corley_home_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_445.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Consider using a finer-grained scale or supplementing self-rated measures with validated health assessment tools to capture a wider range of health changes.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9290.md)

## Name of the risk

Limited ability to accurately capture subtle changes in health perception, potentially missing important variations in health outcomes.
