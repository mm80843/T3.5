# __Real-time updates and dynamic content may encourage user interaction but also increase the risk of contagious disease propagation if the dashboard is accessed by infected individuals.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Parkinson_continuous_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_615.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict hygiene protocols for users accessing the dashboard, including hand sanitization and regular cleaning of shared devices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18721.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential spread of contagious diseases within the built environment if infected individuals interact with the dashboard.
