# __Privacy and data security risks associated with contact tracing apps and ai-powered video analytics tools, as they collect and process sensitive personal information.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Mehta_pandemic_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_646.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strong encryption and anonymization techniques for data storage and transmission, and strictly adhere to data protection regulations such as gdpr.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20124.md)

## Name of the risk

Data breaches or misuse of personal data could lead to loss of trust and privacy violations for the individuals involved.
