# __Modeling sars-cov-2 rna copies/l of wastewater as a point estimate with a uniform distribution may lead to underestimation or overestimation of viral load.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ahmed_first_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_383.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilize additional data sources and modeling techniques to validate the estimates, and consider the range of uncertainty in viral load measurements.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5535.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate estimation of viral load may affect public health decisions and interventions based on wastewater surveillance.
