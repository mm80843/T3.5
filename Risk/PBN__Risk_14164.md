# __Increased network congestion due to rapid deployment of new healthcare applications and increased number of 5g users__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Siriwardhana_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_563.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement ns in 5g with dynamic scalability, deploy virtual nf based on demand at mec servers, use edge computing systems and distributed clouds for visual processing, and utilize phy techniques such as full beamforming technologies](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_16092.md)
