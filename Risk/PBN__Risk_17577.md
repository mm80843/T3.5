# __Inadequate ventilation and air flow may result in poor indoor air quality and contribute to the spread of airborne diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Nembhard_ventilation_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_634.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular inspection and maintenance of ventilation systems, proper air flow management, and use of high-efficiency filters.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19536.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne disease transmission and compromised respiratory health of building occupants.

* [Building occupants](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Stakeholder/PBN__Stakeholder_97.md)
