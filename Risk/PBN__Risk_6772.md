# __Cough and lung sound monitoring for contagious disease symptoms__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Development of accurate and reliable sensor technology for cough and lung sound monitoring, integration with digital health platforms for real-time analysis.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8646.md)

## Name of the risk

Failure to monitor and detect cough and lung sounds could result in delayed identification and isolation of contagious disease cases.
