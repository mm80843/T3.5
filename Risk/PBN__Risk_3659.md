# __Inadequate handling of high-throughput sequencing equipment and reagents can pose a risk of contamination and spread of contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ahmed_first_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_383.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance and cleaning of equipment, use of sterile techniques, proper training of personnel.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5532.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential introduction of contaminants into the sequencing process, leading to inaccurate results and potential exposure to contagious diseases.
