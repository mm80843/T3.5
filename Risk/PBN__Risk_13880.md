# __Confined classroom spaces with traditional desk layouts increase the risk of disease transmission and close contact between students.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Spennemann_designing_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_558.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Rearranging classroom layouts to accommodate social distancing, implementing strict hygiene protocols, and promoting outdoor teaching where feasible.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_15807.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased potential for the spread of contagious diseases within the school environment, leading to higher infection rates among students and staff.
