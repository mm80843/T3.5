# __Risk of high relative humidity and low humidity levels__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Guo_review_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_478.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Maintain indoor humidity within moderate range, avoid very low and very high humidity conditions](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11038.md)

## Name of the risk

Sensitivity to infections at very low relative humidity and lack of reduction in virus viability in moderate humidity
