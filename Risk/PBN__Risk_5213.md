# __High lods for lassa fever, west nile fever, and influenza could lead to missed detection of the viruses in wastewater, increasing the risk of environmental contamination.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Barcelo_wastewater-based_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_408.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of more sensitive biosensor devices with lower lods and development of effective clean-up methods for wastewater samples.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7086.md)
