# __Inequality in susceptibility to uv damage among different coronaviruses.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Beggs_upper-room_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_413.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Conduct specific research on the susceptibility of the target virus to uv light. use multiple sources of data for comparison and analysis.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7334.md)
