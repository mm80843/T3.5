# __Willingness to pay for green roofs__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Manso_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_660.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Educate the public on the benefits of green roofs, offer subsidies for green roof installations, and incentivize building developers to integrate green spaces.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20856.md)

## Name of the risk

Low willingness to pay for green roofs may limit the integration of accessible green spaces in buildings, impacting the overall health and well-being of residents.
