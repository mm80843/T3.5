# __Collecting survey responses during the covid-19 pandemic may introduce bias due to the impact of the pandemic on respondents’ perspectives and behaviors.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rokooei_perception_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_588.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Include specific questions addressing the pandemic’s influence and analyze data with pandemic-related variables as covariates.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17452.md)

## Name of the risk

The survey results may not accurately reflect the pre-pandemic or post-pandemic conditions, leading to skewed insights.
