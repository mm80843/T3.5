# __Use of ehealth wearables for covid-19 data interpretation and monitoring.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Channa_rise_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_432.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Validation of data interpretation models, regular calibration of wearables, and cross-verification with traditional medical diagnostics.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8629.md)

## Name of the risk

Inadequate data interpretation models and inaccuracies in monitoring can lead to misdiagnosis and incorrect treatment of COVID-19.
