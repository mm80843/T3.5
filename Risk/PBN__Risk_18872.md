# __Limited contact with nature during childhood due to urban upbringing and current urban living situation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Manso_role_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_660.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encouraging and facilitating regular access to green areas and natural environments for both children and adults.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20843.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduced exposure to natural environments may lead to compromised immune systems and increased susceptibility to contagious diseases.
