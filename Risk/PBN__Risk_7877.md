# __Use of bioaerosol samplers in a hospital environment can lead to potential aerosolized pathogen exposure.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ding_toilets_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_455.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of personal protective equipment ppe while handling bioaerosol samplers, conducting sampling in controlled environments, and proper disinfection of sampling equipment.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9760.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of pathogen transmission and healthcare-associated infections.
