# __Potential contamination of sequencing data by environmental dna or other contaminants during sample preparation and processing.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Kembel_architectural_2014](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_687.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Adopt strict laboratory protocols for sample handling and processing to minimize the risk of contamination. use negative controls and blank samples to monitor for contamination. perform bioinformatic analysis to identify and remove potential contaminants from the dataset.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_22233.md)

## Name of the risk

Misleading or erroneous results leading to incorrect conclusions about the microbial community.
