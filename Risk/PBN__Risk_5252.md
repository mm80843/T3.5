# __Increased contact between biting insect vectors, animal hosts, and humans due to deforestation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bardosh_addressing_2017](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_409.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing measures to reduce vector breeding sites, controlling insect populations, and promoting protective agricultural practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7125.md)
