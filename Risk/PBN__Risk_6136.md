# __The use of face shields as an alternative to face masks may not effectively prevent the transmission of airborne particles and aerosols, leading to potential exposure to contagious diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Burridge_ventilation_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_426.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Encouraging the use of face shields in conjunction with face masks or coverings to provide comprehensive protection against airborne particles and aerosols.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8008.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of exposure to infectious aerosol particles, particularly for health care workers and individuals in the service industry.
