# __Poor indoor air quality due to contamination from chemical and biological pollutants.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Dalessandro_covid-19_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_448.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Frequent window opening and natural ventilation, use of air extractors, regular cleaning of ventilation systems.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_9508.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of respiratory diseases and transmission of contagious diseases such as COVID-19.
