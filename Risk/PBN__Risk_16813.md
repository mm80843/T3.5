# __Limited control of tenants over indoor environmental quality may lead to suboptimal conditions for mitigating contagious diseases propagation.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Parkinson_continuous_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_615.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Collaborate with tenants to establish guidelines and standards for indoor environmental quality that support the mitigation of contagious diseases propagation.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18760.md)

## Name of the risk

Higher risk of contagious diseases transmission within buildings when tenants have limited influence on indoor environmental quality.
