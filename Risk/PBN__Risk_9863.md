# __Open windows may lead to the uneven distribution of pollutants in a building, with some areas experiencing rapid reduction in pollutant concentration while others take longer to clear.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Howard-reed_effect_2002](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_490.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Use of a more effective method for evacuating pollutants from the entire house, such as opening multiple windows across different levels of the home.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_11770.md)

## Name of the risk

Uneven distribution of pollutants can lead to health risks for occupants in areas where pollutants linger for longer periods.
