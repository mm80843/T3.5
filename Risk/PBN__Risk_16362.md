# __Constant wait time of 15 minutes for post-inoculation waiting may result in patients being in close proximity for an extended period, increasing the risk of disease transmission.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Pilati_digital_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_609.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement appointment scheduling, use technology for automated patient notifications, and create designated waiting areas for post-inoculation patients.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18304.md)

## Name of the risk

Prolonged close contact in waiting areas can lead to higher risk of disease transmission and potential exposure to infectious individuals.
