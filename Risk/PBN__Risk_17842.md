# __Airborne transmission of infectious viruses due to air recirculation in public buildings__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Morawska_how_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_641.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Avoid air recirculation, use particulate filters, and disinfection equipment in recirculated air streams](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_19803.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of airborne infection transmission and potential contamination of areas connected to the same ventilation system
