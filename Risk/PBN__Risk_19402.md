# __Lack of trust in virtual human agency and simulated human behaviors may result in skepticism and resistance to using the system for evaluating designs.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Lee_designing_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_672.md)

## Mitigations of this risk
