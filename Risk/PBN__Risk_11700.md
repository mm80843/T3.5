# __Misinformation and rumors spread through radio and social media may foster confusion, distrust, or panic within the community.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Wilkinson_local_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_512.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establishment of credible communication channels to manage misinformation and rumors, and promotion of accurate public health information.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13612.md)

## Name of the risk

Decreased trust in public health information and guidelines, leading to non-compliance and increased risk of transmission.
