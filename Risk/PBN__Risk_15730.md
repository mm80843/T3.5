# __Identifying couples based on proximity and time could lead to inaccurate assumptions about their actual relationship, potentially missing true social contacts or interactions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Rezaei_deepsocial_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_593.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Consider additional contextual information or behavioral patterns to validate the nature of relationships between individuals, such as shared activities or interactions.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_17671.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate identification of social contacts could lead to ineffective measures for controlling the spread of infectious diseases.
