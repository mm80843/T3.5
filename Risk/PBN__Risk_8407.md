# __Potential for transmission of contagious diseases among guinea pigs due to ventilation and ionization of air__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Escombe_upper-room_2009](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_464.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implement strict quarantine measures, regular health monitoring, and air treatment to minimize the risk of disease transmission](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_10297.md)
