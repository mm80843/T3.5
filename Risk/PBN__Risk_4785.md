# __Limited use of objective health measures in research studies__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Awada_occupant_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_401.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Facilitate access to objective health data, address privacy concerns, and promote the integration of objective health measures in research studies](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6658.md)

## Name of the risk

Lack of accurate and comprehensive health data, potential oversight of critical health indicators, and reliance on potentially biased self-reported health information
