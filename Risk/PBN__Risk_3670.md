# __Use of wastewater samples for early detection of covid-19 may lead to false positives or false negatives due to different assay sensitivities.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ahmed_first_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_383.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Standardize the use of specific assays and validate their performance in detecting sars-cov-2 in wastewater samples.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5543.md)

## Name of the risk

Inaccurate detection may result in delayed response to outbreaks or unnecessary panic in communities.
