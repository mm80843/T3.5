# __Potential transmission of contagious diseases through contact with nature and outdoor environments.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Twohig-bennett_health_2018](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_537.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Monitoring and controlling animal populations in green spaces, promoting public awareness of disease risk, and implementing preventive measures such as insect repellents.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_14838.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of spreading diseases such as Lyme disease, West Nile virus, and other zoonotic diseases.
