# __Close proximity of individuals in the swimming pool and jacuzzi areas may lead to the spread of contagions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Ibrahim_assessment_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_495.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Implementing strict hygiene protocols, regular disinfection, and promoting social distancing measures.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12110.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of transmission of waterborne or airborne diseases in the pool and Jacuzzi.
