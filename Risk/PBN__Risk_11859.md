# __Rollout of mass drug-administration campaigns in response to unsubstantiated recommendations, causing diversion of drug supplies from neglected disease programs.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Watson_rise_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_516.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Establishing guidelines for evidence-based drug distribution and implementing oversight measures for mass drug administration programs.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_13772.md)

## Name of the risk

Reduction in drug supplies for tackling neglected tropical diseases like river blindness, potentially leading to increased morbidity and mortality from these diseases.
