# __Risk of misdiagnosis or delayed treatment in telemedicine due to potential information loss or distortion.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Aloulou_design_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_392.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Utilize high-resolution imaging and video technologies, ensure synchronous operations between remote and on-site locations, and provide training for medical staff on telemedicine best practices.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_5998.md)

## Name of the risk

Incorrect diagnosis or delayed treatment for patients, leading to worsened health outcomes.
