# __Use of low-cost opensource hardware may result in lower reliability and accuracy of ieq data.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Calvo_scalable_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_427.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Invest in higher-quality hardware and conduct regular calibration and maintenance of sensors to ensure accuracy of ieq data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_8142.md)
