# __Insufficient ventilation can lead to high levels of indoor air pollution, including airborne viral particles and diseases.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Amran_review_2022](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_394.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regular maintenance of air-handling units, use of effective air filters, and implementation of appropriate ventilation strategies to introduce outdoor air.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_6166.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of transmission of contagious diseases and compromised indoor air quality, impacting the health of building occupants.
