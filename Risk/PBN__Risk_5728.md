# __Displacement ventilation performs worse when patients are lying sideways, leading to the persistence of exhalation jets over a long distance.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Bolashikov_methods_2009](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_417.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Optimize ventilation systems for different patient positions and consider the use of air curtains or barriers to contain exhalation jets.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7601.md)

## Name of the risk

Increased risk of spreading respiratory pathogens and decreased air quality in healthcare settings.
