# __Allocations of points of interest pois in a city can impact infection rates by influencing the movement and congregation of people in specific areas.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Yang_urban_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_504.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Strategic allocation of pois, promoting alternative leisure and recreational spaces, and implementing crowd management strategies.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_12614.md)

## Name of the risk

Unequal distribution of POIs can lead to overcrowding in certain locations, increasing the risk of disease transmission in those areas.
