# __Wireless transmission of data between nodes may facilitate the spread of contagious diseases if nodes are contaminated.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Parkinson_continuous_2019](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_615.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Regularly sanitizing and inspecting the hardware components of the nodes to prevent contamination.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_18771.md)

## Name of the risk

Unintentional transmission of pathogens through the wireless network, posing health risks to individuals in the vicinity.
