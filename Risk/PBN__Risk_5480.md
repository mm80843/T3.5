# __Reliance on upper-room uvgi as the sole intervention for inhibiting covid-19 transmission in buildings.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Beggs_upper-room_2020](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_413.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Combining uvgi with other effective interventions such as ventilation and filtration to ensure comprehensive coverage of the building.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_7353.md)

## Name of the risk

Potential for incomplete inactivation of virus in other areas of the building, leading to continued spread of the disease.
