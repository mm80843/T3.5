# __Overreliance on iot technology may lead to a lack of human judgment and intervention in critical healthcare decisions.__

## Articles mentionning the risk

* [Malliga_comprehensive_2021](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Article/PBN__Article_661.md)

## Mitigations of this risk

* [Combine iot data with medical professional input, establish clear protocols for human intervention, and provide training on interpreting iot data.](https://github.com/mm80843/T3.5/blob/pages/Mitigation/PBN__Mitigation_20881.md)
